Overview

The itel A90 emerges as a compelling budget smartphone option in 2025, offering impressive features typically found in more expensive devices. Launched in May 2025, this device combines a smooth 90Hz display with capable performance and reliable battery life, making it an excellent choice for budget-conscious consumers .

With its IP54 dust and water resistance rating and upgraded Aivana 2.0 AI assistant, the A90 stands out in the crowded budget segment. Running on Android 14 Go Edition with itel OS 14, this device provides a modern and intuitive user experience for everyday tasks, social media, and basic applications .
