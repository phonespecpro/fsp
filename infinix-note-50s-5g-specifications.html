Infinix Note 50S 5G

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Titanium Grey

Overview

The Infinix Note 50S 5G represents a significant step forward in the budget 5G smartphone segment, launching in April 2025 with the powerful MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultimate processor. This device combines premium features with accessible pricing, making advanced technology available to a wider audience.

With its stunning 144Hz AMOLED display, versatile 64MP camera system, and robust 5500mAh battery with 45W fast charging, the Note 50S 5G delivers exceptional value. The device also features military-grade durability certification, IP64 dust and water resistance, and runs on the latest Android 15 with Infinix's refined XOS 15 interface.
