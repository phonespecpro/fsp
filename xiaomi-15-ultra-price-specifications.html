The Xiaomi 15 Ultra is now available globally with varying regional pricing. The device was officially announced on February 27, 2025, and released the same day. Below are the latest verified prices from different markets.

Overview & Market Position

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra represents the pinnacle of smartphone photography and performance in 2025, building upon the successful foundation of its predecessor. Announced and released on February 27, 2025, this flagship device continues Xiaomi's strategic partnership with Leica while introducing the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite processor and comprehensive AI capabilities through HyperOS 2.

Positioned as a direct competitor to Samsung's Galaxy S25 Ultra and Apple's iPhone 17 Pro Max, the Xiaomi 15 Ultra distinguishes itself with an unparalleled camera system featuring a 1-inch main sensor and industry-leading 200MP periscope telephoto lens. The device maintains Xiaomi's reputation for offering flagship-level specifications at a competitive price point, though regional variations significantly affect its market positioning.

Bundle Offers & Promotions

Several markets offer attractive bundle deals with the Xiaomi 15 Ultra. In Hong Kong, early purchases include the Professional Photography Kit (valued at HK$1,399) at no additional cost. This kit includes a case with an integrated grip, shutter button, and support for 67mm photography filters, significantly enhancing the photography experience for enthusiasts.

Additional regional promotions may include extended warranties, screen protection services, and subscriptions to services like YouTube Premium or Spotify. These value-added offerings can substantially improve the overall purchase proposition in competitive markets.

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra features an "All Around Liquid Display" with ultra-thin bezels and a quad-curved design that improves grip and reliability. The Silver Chrome variant uses aerospace-grade glass fiber combined with PU leather for a distinctive look and feel. The display supports Dolby Vision®, HDR10+, and HDR Vivid content playback, with TÜV Rheinland certifications for low blue light, flicker-free, and circadian-friendly viewing.

The Snapdragon 8 Elite platform delivers exceptional performance for gaming, multitasking, and AI processing. Xiaomi's 3D Dual-Channel IceLoop cooling system ensures sustained performance during intensive tasks like gaming or 8K video recording. The software commitment of 4 Android version updates and 6 years of security patches, while less than some competitors, represents Xiaomi's most substantial software support promise to date.

The camera system represents the most significant aspect of the Xiaomi 15 Ultra. The quad-camera setup covers 14-200mm focal lengths with large apertures across all lenses. The 200MP ultra telephoto uses a 1/1.4-inch sensor—exceptionally large for a periscope lens—providing 136% improved light capture compared to the previous generation.

Notably, the device lacks the variable aperture found in the Xiaomi 14 Ultra, moving to a fixed f/1.6 aperture with software-simulated effects. The camera supports Leica's Authentic and Vibrant color styles, along with professional features like 10-bit Log video recording across all focal lengths and Dolby Vision® HDR capture.

The global variant features a 5410mAh silicon-carbon battery, while the China model includes a larger 6000mAh capacity. The 90W wired and 80W wireless charging capabilities represent some of the fastest available in flagship devices, though note that the power adapter is sold separately in some regions. Connectivity options are comprehensive, with Wi-Fi 7 support (where available), extensive 5G band coverage, and satellite communication capabilities in specific markets.

AI & Software Features

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra introduces "Xiaomi HyperAI" alongside Google's Gemini AI integration. Key AI capabilities include:

  • AI Writing: System-wide assistance for content creation and editing
  • Circle to Search with Google: Visual search without leaving apps
  • AI Creativity Assistant: Image enhancement, reflection removal, and content generation
  • Xiaomi AISP 2.0: Computational photography with FusionLM, ToneLM, ColorLM, and PortraitLM
  • AI Speech Recognition & Interpreter: Real-time translation and transcription

These features require an internet connection for full functionality. The AI integration aims to enhance productivity, creativity, and photography while maintaining Xiaomi's ecosystem connectivity through HyperConnect features.

Final Verdict & Recommendations

The Xiaomi 15 Ultra stands as one of 2025's most compelling photography-focused flagships, particularly for enthusiasts who prioritize camera versatility over other considerations. The 200MP periscope telephoto represents genuine innovation in mobile photography, while the 1-inch main sensor continues to deliver exceptional image quality.

Who Should Buy the Xiaomi 15 Ultra?

Photography Enthusiasts: The comprehensive camera system with Leica optics and extensive manual controls makes this an ideal choice for mobile photographers. The optional Photography Kit further enhances this experience.

Tech Early Adopters: Those seeking the latest processor technology (Snapdragon 8 Elite) and comprehensive AI integration will find the device compelling, especially in markets with competitive pricing.

Considerations Before Purchase

Regional Availability: Verify official availability in your region, as warranty and network compatibility vary significantly.

Software Support Timeline: While improved, the 4-year Android update commitment trails Samsung's 7-year promise for competing flagships.

Camera Evolution: The removal of the variable aperture may disappoint some photography purists who appreciated this feature in the previous model.

Value Proposition: In regions with higher pricing (particularly Europe), carefully compare against competitors like the Galaxy S25 Ultra which may offer better long-term software support and local warranty conditions.
