Overview

The Itel S17 is an entry-level smartphone that focuses on delivering essential features at an incredibly affordable price point. Originally launched in 2021, this device continues to be relevant in 2025 due to its massive 5000mAh battery and large 6.6-inch display, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious consumers and first-time smartphone users in Nigeria.

Running on the optimized Android 11 Go Edition, the S17 provides a smooth experience for basic daily tasks despite its modest hardware. With its triple camera setup, fingerprint sensor, and face unlock capabilities, it offers surprising value for its price segment, proving that you don't need to break the bank for a reliable smartphone experience.
