Itel S25 Ultra

November 10, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Bromo Black

Overview

The Itel S25 Ultra represents a significant step forward for Itel in the competitive budget smartphone market, offering premium features typically found in more expensive devices. Launched in November 2024, this device combines an impressive AMOLED display with capable cameras and reliable performance at an accessible price point.

With its premium design that resembles flagship Samsung models, 120Hz AMOLED display, and comprehensive feature set including IP64 rating for dust and water resistance, the S25 Ultra challenges the perception of what budget smartphones can deliver. Itel's promise of two major Android updates further enhances its value proposition for Nigerian consumers looking for a device that won't become outdated quickly.
