OnePlus 15 Pre-Orders Go Live in the US — Pricing and Key Features Revealed

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack

US Pre-Orders Launch After FCC Delay

OnePlus has officially opened pre-orders in the United States for its latest flagship, the OnePlus 15, following a significant delay caused by a prolonged U.S. government shutdown. The phone, which launched globally in November 2025, is now available to order exclusively through the official OnePlus US website.

The delay was due to the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) being unable to certify the device during the shutdown, which lasted from October 1 to November 12, 2025. With that regulatory hurdle now cleared, the phone is set to start shipping to customers around December 22, 2025.

US Pricing & Configurations

The OnePlus 15 is positioned as a high-value flagship, starting at a competitive price point for its feature set.

Base Model (12GB + 256GB) $899.99
Upgraded Model (16GB + 512GB) $999.99

The phone is available in three color options: Infinite Black, Ultra Violet (a light purple), and Sand Storm (a sandy tan finish).

Aggressive Pre-Order Bonuses & Deals

To attract early buyers and compensate for the launch delay, OnePlus is offering one of its most generous pre-order campaigns to date. The offers are first-come, first-served and available with a direct pre-order from OnePlus.

Free Gift (Choose One)
  • OnePlus Watch 3 (43mm) – Valued at $299.99. Available only with the 512GB model and reported to be out of stock quickly.
  • OnePlus Buds Pro 3 – Valued at $179.99.
  • OnePlus AIRVOOC 50W Magnetic Charger – Valued at $79.99.
  • OnePlus 15 Magnetic Phone Case – Sandstone or Aramid Fiber options.
Trade-In Program
  • Guaranteed $100 Instant Credit for any smartphone in any condition.
  • Additional $100 Cashback Bonus when the purchase includes a trade-in.
  • Higher-value bonuses (up to $899 total) for recent flagship phones.
Other Perks Up to 30% off accessories when bundled, and a 5% student discount.

Key Specifications & Standout Features

The OnePlus 15 is built around several headline features, most notably its record-breaking battery capacity.

Display 6.78-inch LTPO AMOLED, 1.5K resolution, dynamic refresh rate up to 165Hz, 1.15mm ultra-slim bezels.
Processor Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5, with LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage.
Battery & Charging
  • 7,300mAh Silicon NanoStack Battery – Marketed as the largest in a consumer smartphone in North America, promising up to 31 hours of video playback. Early reviews praise "spectacular" battery life, nearing two full days of use.
  • 80W Wired SUPERVOOC Charging (US cap) and 50W Wireless AIRVOOC Charging.
Camera System Triple 50MP rear array (main, ultrawide, periscope telephoto with 3.5x optical zoom). 32MP front camera.
Software & AI OxygenOS 16 based on Android 16, featuring "Mind Space" for organization and deep integration with Google Gemini AI.
Gaming & Cooling 3200Hz touch response chip, "360° Cryo Velocity Cooling System" with a vapor chamber. Note: Some early reviews report the device can get hot under sustained load.

The Bottom Line for Buyers

The OnePlus 15 presents a compelling package, especially for users who prioritize battery life and raw performance. The pre-order deals significantly enhance its value proposition.

Consider the OnePlus 15 if you: Need the absolute best battery life on a flagship, want top-tier gaming performance with a high-refresh-rate display, and can take advantage of the generous trade-in and freebie offers.

Be aware that: Early reviews indicate the phone can run hot during intensive, sustained gaming. Some camera analyses suggest it's very capable but may not lead the pack in computational photography compared to rivals like Google or Samsung. Also, a rare but serious password authentication bug has been reported on a user forum, though it does not appear to be widespread.

The pre-order window is open now and is scheduled to close on December 11, 2025, after which the phone will go into open sale.
