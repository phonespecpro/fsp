TECNO Spark Go 2

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Ink Black

Overview

The TECNO Spark Go 2 represents excellent value in the budget smartphone segment, offering modern features at an accessible price point. Released in June 2025, this device brings impressive specifications including a smooth 120Hz display, capable Unisoc T7250 processor, and massive 5000mAh battery that make it stand out in its class.

With its IP64 dust and water resistance rating and drop resistance up to 1.5 meters, the Spark Go 2 is built to withstand daily wear and tear. The phone runs on the latest Android 15 operating system with TECNO's HIOS 15 interface, ensuring a modern software experience with promised longevity through a 4-year fluency guarantee.
