TECNO Camon 30 Premier 5G

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Hawaii Lava Black

Overview

The TECNO Camon 30 Premier 5G represents a significant milestone in TECNO's smartphone evolution, combining professional-grade photography capabilities with flagship-level performance. Launched in 2025, this device features an iconic rangefinder-inspired design that pays homage to classic photography equipment while incorporating modern smartphone aesthetics.

At the heart of the Camon 30 Premier 5G is a revolutionary triple 50MP camera system powered by Sony's advanced imaging technology, including the flagship IMX890 sensor and a dedicated Sony CXD5622GG imaging chip. This dual-chip architecture enables industry-first 4K 30fps full-scene AI-NR HDR video capabilities, making it one of the most capable camera phones in its class. The device also boasts a MediaTek Dimensity 8200 Ultimate processor that delivers exceptional performance for both photography and gaming applications.
