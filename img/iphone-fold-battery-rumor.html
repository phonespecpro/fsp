Rumor: iPhone Fold Could Feature a Larger Battery Than iPhone 17 Pro Max

November 19, 2025
By: Shedrack

Breaking News

Apple's highly anticipated entry into the foldable smartphone market could feature the largest battery ever seen in an iPhone, according to new leaks emerging from supply chain sources. The iPhone Fold, expected to launch in 2026, is reportedly being tested with battery capacities ranging from 5,400 to 5,800mAh, potentially surpassing even the current battery champion, the iPhone 17 Pro Max.

The information comes from Korean leak aggregator yeux1122, who has previously provided accurate insights into Apple's component strategy. This development signals Apple's serious commitment to ensuring its first foldable device delivers competitive battery life despite the power demands of dual displays.

Technical Analysis

The substantial battery capacity makes logical sense given the iPhone Fold's expected display configuration. With an inner screen measuring approximately 7.58 inches - significantly larger than the iPhone 17 Pro Max's 6.9-inch display - the device will require ample power reserves. However, the smaller 5.38-inch cover display should draw considerably less power when used in folded mode.

Industry observers note that Apple may be prioritizing battery performance over slimness for its first foldable entry. The device is expected to be thicker and heavier than current iPhones, partly to accommodate the larger battery. This approach contrasts with some Android manufacturers who are focusing on reducing foldable thickness at the expense of battery capacity.

Market Context & Strategic Importance

Apple's move into the foldable market comes as the segment continues to gain consumer acceptance. The company appears to be addressing one of the key concerns with current foldable devices - battery life - by equipping its first entry with a substantial power cell that could potentially outperform most competitors.

Analysts suggest that Apple cannot afford to deliver subpar battery performance given the expected premium pricing, likely starting around $2,000. After battery concerns affected the iPhone Air earlier this year, the company seems determined to ensure its foldable iPhone meets or exceeds battery life expectations for flagship devices.

Expected Timeline & Final Thoughts

Current industry consensus points to a 2026 launch for the iPhone Fold, potentially alongside the iPhone 18 Pro models in fall 2026. However, some reports still suggest the possibility of delays pushing the release to 2027.

While the leaked battery specifications are encouraging for potential battery life, the actual user experience will depend on Apple's optimization of both hardware and software. The company's traditional strength in power management, combined with what could be the largest battery ever in an iPhone, suggests the iPhone Fold might set new standards for foldable device endurance when it eventually launches.
