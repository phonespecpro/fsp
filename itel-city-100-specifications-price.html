Overview

The itel City 100 represents a significant step forward in budget smartphone technology, combining durability with modern features at an accessible price point. Launched in July 2025, this device brings premium features like IP64 rating, 90Hz display, and substantial battery life to the budget segment.

With its Unisoc T7250 processor, 5200mAh battery, and 6.75-inch 90Hz display, the City 100 is designed for users who need reliable performance without compromising on essential features. The IP64 rating makes it resistant to dust and water splashes, while the 60-month fluency guarantee ensures long-term reliability.
