Overview

The itel P70 (also known as Power 70) redefines budget smartphone expectations in 2025 with its revolutionary battery system that offers up to 10,000mAh total capacity. This device combines exceptional battery life with modern features typically found in more expensive smartphones, making it an ideal choice for users who prioritize longevity and reliability.

Featuring a 6.67-inch 120Hz display, MediaTek Helio G50 processor, and IP54 dust and water resistance, the P70 delivers a premium experience at an accessible price point. With its unique bundled 4,000mAh battery case and reverse charging capability, this phone is designed to keep you powered through multiple days of heavy usage without needing to recharge.
