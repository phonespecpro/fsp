Infinix Note 50 Pro+

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Titanium Grey

Overview

The Infinix Note 50 Pro+ represents a significant leap forward in Infinix's smartphone offerings, positioning itself as a premium mid-range device with flagship-level features. Launched in March 2025, this device combines powerful performance with exceptional camera capabilities and rapid charging technology that rivals much more expensive alternatives.

With its MediaTek Dimensity 8350 Ultimate chipset, sophisticated triple camera system featuring a periscope telephoto lens, and blazing-fast 100W wired charging complemented by 50W wireless MagCharge, the Note 50 Pro+ delivers a comprehensive premium experience at an accessible price point. The device also boasts an aerospace-grade aluminum frame and IP64 rating for dust and water resistance, making it both durable and aesthetically pleasing.
