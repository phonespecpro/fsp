Infinix Hot 60 Pro+

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Sleek Black

Overview

The Infinix Hot 60 Pro+ makes a bold statement in the 2025 smartphone market by setting a new global record as the world's slimmest 3D-curved screen phone. Measuring an incredibly thin 5.95mm and weighing just 155 grams, this device redefines what's possible in budget smartphone design while maintaining impressive functionality and durability.

Beyond its record-breaking slim profile, the Hot 60 Pro+ delivers a premium experience with its 144Hz AMOLED display, capable MediaTek Helio G200 processor, and versatile 50MP Sony camera system. With IP65 dust and water resistance, aerospace-grade aluminum frame, and Corning Gorilla Glass 7i protection, this phone proves that premium features can indeed be accessible at an affordable price point.
