Infinix Note 40 Pro

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Obsidian Black

Overview

The Infinix Note 40 Pro represents a significant leap forward in the mid-range smartphone segment, combining premium features with exceptional value. Launched as Infinix's flagship offering in the Note series, this device brings together professional-grade photography capabilities, blazing-fast charging technology, and a stunning display that rivals more expensive competitors .

With its revolutionary 108MP OIS camera system, 70W All-Round FastCharge 2.0 technology, and beautiful 3D curved AMOLED display, the Note 40 Pro delivers a flagship-like experience at a fraction of the cost. The device also introduces innovative features like the Active Halo lighting system and Infinix's first self-developed power management chip, the Cheetah X1, making it one of the most technologically advanced offerings in its class .
