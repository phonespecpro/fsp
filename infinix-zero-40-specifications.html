Infinix Zero 40

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Rock Black

Overview

The Infinix Zero 40 represents a significant leap forward in the mid-range smartphone segment, combining flagship-level features with an accessible price point. Launched as a vlogging-focused device in partnership with GoPro, this phone brings professional-grade video capabilities to content creators .

With its powerful MediaTek Dimensity 8200 Ultimate processor, stunning 144Hz AMOLED display, and versatile camera system featuring 108MP OIS stabilization, the Zero 40 challenges the notion that premium features are exclusive to high-end devices. The phone's 50MP front camera capable of 4K video recording makes it particularly appealing for vloggers and content creators .
