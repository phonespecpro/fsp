Overview

The Realme 13 Pro+ takes camera performance to the next level with its professional-grade triple camera system featuring dual Sony sensors with OIS. Equipped with a 50MP Sony LYT-701 main sensor and a 50MP Sony LYT-600 periscope telephoto camera with 3x optical zoom, this device is designed for photography enthusiasts who demand exceptional image quality in all lighting conditions.

Beyond its impressive camera capabilities, the Realme 13 Pro+ delivers a premium experience with its Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 chipset, stunning 6.7-inch 120Hz curved AMOLED display with 2000 nits peak brightness, massive 5200mAh battery with 80W fast charging, and elegant Monet-inspired design. With IP65 water resistance and advanced AI features, this mid-range flagship offers remarkable value for users seeking flagship performance without the premium price tag.
