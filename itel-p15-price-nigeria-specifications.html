Overview

The Itel P15 is an ultra-budget smartphone designed for first-time smartphone users and those looking for reliable basic functionality at an incredibly affordable price point. Originally released in 2019, this device continues to be relevant in 2025 due to its massive 4000mAh battery and essential features that cover the fundamental needs of entry-level smartphone users.

Running on the optimized Android 9 Pie Go Edition, the P15 delivers surprisingly good performance for basic daily tasks despite its modest hardware specifications. With its dual camera setup, fingerprint sensor, and impressive battery life, the P15 represents excellent value for money in Nigeria's competitive budget smartphone market.
