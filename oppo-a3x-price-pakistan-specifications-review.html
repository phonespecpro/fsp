Oppo A3x

December 4, 2025
By: Shedrack
Ocean Blue

Price in Pakistan & Overview

The Oppo A3x is positioned as a budget-friendly smartphone in Pakistan's competitive market. According to the latest market data from December 2025, the official price for the base variant (4GB+64GB) in Pakistan is Rs. 27,999 PKR . However, prices can vary, with some online retailers listing it around Rs. 30,600 and other market listings showing it as low as Rs. 27,999 . The phone was announced in August 2024 and became available shortly after .

Positioned in the budget segment, the Oppo A3x emphasizes durability with military-grade shock resistance and multiple liquid resistance certifications, along with fast charging capabilities that are unusual for its price range . For Pakistani consumers looking for a rugged device with quick charging at an affordable price, the A3x presents an interesting option, though it has received mixed user reviews regarding performance .

Performance Analysis: User reviews consistently report performance issues with the Oppo A3x. Many users describe it as "laggy" with complaints about apps crashing, games not running smoothly, and general sluggishness in daily operations . One user noted that the Snapdragon 6s Gen 1 is a rebranded Snapdragon 662, which is considered a weak processor for modern smartphone tasks . The combination of this chipset with eMMC 5.1 storage likely contributes to the performance limitations reported by users.

Camera Performance: User feedback on the camera is generally negative, with multiple reviews describing it as "not good" and complaining about lag during camera operation and video recording . The 8MP main sensor is quite basic by 2025 standards and is likely sufficient only for the most casual photography needs. The inclusion of a flicker sensor is a minor positive for reducing banding under artificial lighting .

Battery & Charging Analysis: The 5100mAh battery combined with 45W fast charging is arguably the phone's strongest selling point . Users consistently praise the charging speed as a positive aspect . The battery longevity claim of maintaining 80% capacity after four years is notable if accurate . However, some user reviews contradict the battery performance claims, with one Pakistani user stating "battery time 10/0.00001" and complaining about excessive heat .

User Reviews & Common Feedback

User feedback for the Oppo A3x is overwhelmingly negative, with particular consistency across several key issues:

Critical User Feedback:

  • Severe Performance Issues: The most common complaint across dozens of reviews is laggy performance. Users report that the phone "can't even run games or apps smoothly" and mention specific problems with games like COD Mobile, Roblox, and even basic video apps like TikTok .
  • Heating Problems: Multiple users report the phone heats up quickly during regular use, with one Pakistani user specifically mentioning excessive heat during WhatsApp video calls .
  • Camera Quality & Performance: Users describe the camera as "so bad" and note that it lags during operation and recording .
  • Network Issues: Despite having 4G, some users report poor network performance that feels like 3G even in areas with strong signals .
  • Screen Problems: Reports of "ghost touching" after a month of use and screen responsiveness issues during gaming .

Positive Aspects Noted:

  • Fast Charging: Nearly all reviews that mention charging praise the 45W SUPERVOOC speed as a redeeming feature .
  • Design: Some users acknowledge the phone has a "nice design" and "gorgeous" appearance .
  • Durability Features: The IP54 rating and military-grade resistance are marketing points, though user reviews don't specifically comment on these .

A recurring sentiment in user reviews is regret over the purchase, with terms like "fraud," "scam," "useless," and "waste of money" appearing frequently. One user summarized: "I have never seen such a horrible phone in my life" .

Final Verdict: Who Should Consider It?

Consider the Oppo A3x only if:

  • Your absolute maximum budget is Rs. 28,000 PKR and you prioritize charging speed above all other factors.
  • You need a phone with basic durability certifications (IP54, shock resistance) for rough environments.
  • Your smartphone usage is exceptionally minimal—primarily calls, SMS, and perhaps light WhatsApp—with no gaming, multitasking, or camera expectations.
  • You can tolerate significant performance lag, app crashes, and potential heating issues.

Avoid the Oppo A3x if:

  • Smooth performance is important to you for daily tasks, app switching, or social media.
  • You plan to play games, even casual ones like Roblox or COD Mobile.
  • You care about camera quality or reliable video recording.
  • You experience hot climates or are concerned about device heating.
  • You can stretch your budget slightly to consider alternatives from Xiaomi, Samsung, or even other Oppo models in the Rs. 30,000-35,000 range that may offer better performance.

Priced at Rs. 27,999 in Pakistan, the Oppo A3x offers a puzzling combination of excellent fast charging and durability claims alongside what appears to be fundamentally flawed performance based on overwhelming user feedback . While the 45W charging and IP54 rating are notable for this price point, the consistent reports of lag, heating, and poor camera performance suggest significant compromises in core smartphone functionality. Potential buyers should approach with extreme caution and seriously consider whether slightly increasing their budget would yield a dramatically better user experience.
