itel P55 5G – Latest Price, Detailed Specs & Key Features

November 10, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Overview

The itel P55 5G represents a significant milestone in budget smartphone technology, bringing 5G connectivity to the masses at an unprecedented price point. Launched in late 2023 and continuing to dominate the budget segment in 2025, this device proves that advanced connectivity doesn't have to come with a premium price tag.

Powered by the capable MediaTek Dimensity 6080 processor and featuring a 50MP AI camera system, the P55 5G delivers performance that belies its budget positioning. With its massive 5000mAh battery, 90Hz display, and multiple RAM/storage configurations, this phone offers exceptional value for users seeking reliable 5G performance without breaking the bank.
