Overview & Current Price in Pakistan (2025)

The vivo V29, launched internationally in the latter half of 2023, has established itself as a strong contender in Pakistan's competitive mid-range smartphone segment. As of December 2025, the official market price for a new vivo V29 in Pakistan with 12GB RAM and 256GB storage is PKR 159,999. The phone is widely available through official retailers and reputable online platforms across major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.

Positioned as a camera-centric mid-ranger, the V29 builds upon the success of its V-series predecessors by focusing heavily on portrait photography, a premium design with a unique color-changing back panel, and very fast charging—all at a relatively accessible price point for its feature set.

Detailed Review & Performance in 2025

Design, Display & Build

The V29 boasts a sleek, premium design with a slim 7.5mm profile and a manageable weight of 186g. The highlight is its unique back panel (especially in Peak Blue or Starry Purple) which uses "3D magnetic particles" or "Fluorite AG Glass" to create a color-shifting effect under light. The 6.78-inch 3D curved AMOLED display is excellent, offering a sharp 1.5K resolution, smooth 120Hz refresh rate, and high peak brightness for good outdoor visibility. Build quality is solid with an IP68 rating for dust and water resistance in its global variant, a rare feature in this segment.

Performance & Software

Powered by the tried-and-tested Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G 5G chipset, the V29 delivers reliable performance for daily tasks, social media, and most popular mobile games. While not a flagship gaming chip, it handles multitasking smoothly, especially in the 12GB RAM variant. The phone launched with Android 13 and Funtouch OS 13 and is promised three major Android upgrades. Some long-term users have reported issues with battery drain or UI lag after major OS updates (like Android 14), which is a point to consider.

Camera Performance - The Star Feature

This is where the V29 truly shines. The 50MP main camera with Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) captures detailed and vibrant photos in good light. Its standout feature is the Aura Light Portrait 2.0—a large, ring-LED flash that acts as a constant fill light. It automatically adjusts color temperature to match the environment, enabling well-lit, natural-looking portrait shots even in very low light, a capability often missing in mid-range phones. The 50MP autofocus selfie camera is also among the best in its class. The 8MP ultrawide and 2MP depth sensors are functional but noticeably lower in quality.

Battery Life & Charging

The 4600mAh battery provides solid all-day battery life for average users. The headline feature is the incredibly fast 80W FlashCharge, which can charge the phone from 1% to 50% in approximately 17 minutes, making top-ups incredibly convenient. Note that wireless charging is not supported.

Pros, Cons & Final Verdict for 2025 Buyers

Pros:

  • Exceptional Portrait Camera: The 50MP OIS main sensor paired with the smart Aura Light offers unique low-light portrait capabilities.
  • Stunning Design & Display: Beautiful curved AMOLED screen and eye-catching, unique back panel design.
  • Extremely Fast Charging: 80W charging minimizes downtime significantly.
  • Premium Build: IP68 rating (on global variant) adds durability.

Cons:

  • Inconsistent Long-Term Software Experience: Some users report bugs or battery drain after OS updates.
  • Average Secondary Cameras: The 8MP ultrawide and 2MP sensors are underwhelming.
  • No Wireless Charging or Expandable Storage: Misses some convenient features.
  • Processor is Showing Its Age: The Snapdragon 778G, while capable, is a 2021 chip and lags behind newer mid-range processors in raw power.

The 2025 Verdict: The vivo V29 remains a very compelling choice in late 2025 for a specific buyer: the photography enthusiast who prioritizes camera innovation, design, and charging speed over absolute peak gaming performance. Its Aura Light system is genuinely unique. However, if you prioritize the latest chipset for heavy gaming or want guaranteed long-term software stability, it's worth comparing with newer models featuring the latest Snapdragon 7 series or Dimensity chips. At its current PKR 160,000 price point, it faces stiff competition from newer launches.
