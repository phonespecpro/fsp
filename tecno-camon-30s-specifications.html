Overview

The TECNO Camon 30S is a feature-packed mid-range smartphone that was first released in October 2024, offering impressive specifications at an affordable price point. This device stands out with its capable MediaTek Helio G100 processor, vibrant 6.78-inch AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate, and a versatile camera system headlined by a 50MP main sensor with Optical Image Stabilization.

Designed for users who prioritize photography and media consumption, the Camon 30S combines modern aesthetics with practical features like IP53 dust and water resistance, ensuring durability against everyday splashes and dust exposure. With its massive 5000mAh battery and 33W fast charging capability, this phone is built to keep up with your daily demands without frequent charging interruptions.
