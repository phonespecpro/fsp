November 14, 2025

Overview

The TECNO Camon 30 5G represents a significant leap forward in the mid-range smartphone segment, combining professional-grade photography capabilities with robust 5G performance. Launched in 2024, this device continues TECNO's legacy of delivering camera-focused smartphones that cater to content creators and photography enthusiasts who demand exceptional image quality without the flagship price tag.

With its MediaTek Dimensity 7020 chipset, stunning 6.78-inch AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate, and industry-leading 70W fast charging technology, the Camon 30 5G stands as a compelling option in the competitive mid-range market. The device features IP54 dust and water resistance, ensuring durability against everyday splashes and exposure, while the premium glass and eco-leather back options provide both style and substance.
