Official Certification Confirms Imminent Launch

The Nothing Phone 4a has taken a significant step toward its commercial release with the appearance on India's BIS certification website. The listing, dated November 13, 2025, reveals the model number A069 which follows Nothing's established naming pattern - the previous Phone 3a carried model number A059 while its Pro variant was A059P .

This regulatory milestone is typically one of the final steps before a smartphone's commercial launch in India. The certification doesn't reveal specific hardware details but confirms that the device is progressing through the mandatory approval process required for the Indian market .
