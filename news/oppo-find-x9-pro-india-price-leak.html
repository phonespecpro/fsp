Oppo Find X9, Find X9 Pro India Prices Leak Ahead of Release

November 15, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Price Leak Confirmed

Oppo Find X9 series India pricing has leaked online just days before the official November 18 launch. The standard Find X9 appears listed at ₹74,999 while the Find X9 Pro carries a ₹99,999 price tag according to retailer listings .

Flagship Pricing Revealed

In what appears to be an accidental early listing by Indian retailer Poorvika, the complete pricing structure for Oppo's upcoming Find X9 series has been revealed ahead of the official November 18, 2025 launch event. The leaks confirm Oppo's positioning in the premium smartphone segment with significant pricing for both models .

The standard Find X9 shows a notable price increase compared to its predecessor, while the Find X9 Pro maintains the same premium positioning as the previous generation. Both phones are set to become the first devices in India powered by MediaTek's flagship Dimensity 9500 chipset .

Hasselblad Camera Excellence

The Find X9 Pro's camera system represents a significant leap forward, featuring a 200MP periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom and Optical Image Stabilization. This setup enables up to 120x super zoom capability, bringing distant subjects into clear focus . The Hasselblad partnership brings professional-grade color calibration and imaging algorithms to both devices.

Both models feature the Hasselblad Master Camera System with advanced computational photography capabilities. The Pro model's 200MP telephoto sensor uses a Samsung HP5 sensor, while the main camera utilizes a custom Sony LYT-828 sensor developed specifically for Oppo .

Market Positioning & Launch

The Oppo Find X9 series is scheduled for official India launch on November 18, 2025, following their initial debut in China on October 16. The leaked pricing positions the standard Find X9 as a direct competitor to other premium Android flagships, while the Find X9 Pro competes in the ultra-premium segment .

With the Find X9 receiving a ₹5,000 price increase over its predecessor, Oppo appears to be positioning the device as a more premium offering. However, the company is backing this move with substantial hardware improvements including the flagship Dimensity 9500 chipset, larger batteries, and enhanced camera systems with Hasselblad tuning .
