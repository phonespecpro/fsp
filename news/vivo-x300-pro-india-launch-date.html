Vivo X300, X300 Pro India Launch Date Confirmed in New Announcement

November 15, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Latest Update

Vivo has officially confirmed that the X300 series will launch in India on December 2, 2025, at 12 PM IST. The company will also introduce its professional Photographer Kit with telephoto extender accessories .

Official Launch Announcement

Vivo has made it official: the much-anticipated X300 series flagship smartphones are set to debut in the Indian market on December 2, 2025 . This confirmation comes through official channels and follows the series' successful launch in China and other global markets last month.

The Indian variant will mark a significant milestone as Vivo's first smartphones to feature the new OriginOS 6 interface right out of the box, replacing the familiar FuntouchOS that Indian users have known . This launch positions Vivo to compete directly with other flagship devices entering the Indian market during the festive season.

Professional Imaging Capabilities

The Vivo X300 series continues the brand's partnership with ZEISS, incorporating advanced camera technologies that cater to both photography enthusiasts and professionals. The X300 Pro specifically features a 200MP ZEISS APO telephoto camera with 3.5× optical zoom capability, while the standard X300 utilizes a 200MP main sensor .

Vivo will also offer a separate Photographer Kit in India, which includes a 2.35x Telephoto Extender that attaches to the phones for enhanced optical zoom capabilities. This professional accessory is compatible with both X300 and X300 Pro models and will be sold separately . The series also benefits from the dual-chip design combining MediaTek's Dimensity 9500 with Vivo's dedicated V3+ imaging chip for superior computational photography .

However, Indian pricing may vary slightly due to import duties and local market factors.

Market Impact & Final Thoughts

The Vivo X300 series enters a competitive flagship smartphone market in India, going up against recently launched devices from OnePlus, iQOO, and other brands. Its combination of MediaTek's powerful Dimensity 9500 chipset, ZEISS-engineered camera systems, and the new OriginOS 6 interface positions it as a strong contender in the premium segment.

With the confirmed December 2 launch date, Indian consumers can look forward to experiencing Vivo's latest imaging innovations and performance capabilities. The availability of the professional Photographer Kit further strengthens its appeal to content creators and photography enthusiasts who demand more than what standard flagship smartphones offer.
