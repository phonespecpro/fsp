Samsung Galaxy Devices Now Available on Instamart With 10-Minute Instant Delivery in India

December 5, 2025
By: Shedrack

Breaking News: Ultra-Fast Tech Delivery Lands in India

In a significant move that redefines tech retail convenience, Samsung India has announced a strategic partnership with Swiggy's quick-commerce platform, Instamart, to deliver a wide range of Galaxy devices to customers' doorsteps in approximately 10 minutes. This expansion of Samsung's omnichannel strategy brings smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, wireless earbuds, and accessories into the realm of instant gratification previously reserved for groceries and daily essentials.

Announced on December 5, 2025, this partnership represents a major shift in how consumers can purchase electronics in India. With this collaboration, customers in select metro cities can now order Galaxy products through the Instamart app and receive them within minutes, providing unprecedented convenience for urgent needs like replacing a lost phone or grabbing last-minute travel accessories.

Partnership Details and Available Products

The Samsung-Instamart partnership expands on an earlier initiative that began with Galaxy smartphones earlier in 2025. The service has now been extended to include a broader range of Galaxy devices, creating what Samsung describes as a more comprehensive "Galaxy experience" available in minutes.

Available Products Galaxy smartphones, tablets, smartwatches, wireless earbuds (like Galaxy Buds Core), USB cables, wall chargers, power banks
Delivery Time Approximately 10 minutes (may vary based on location and conditions)
Platform Swiggy Instamart app (available on Google Play Store)
Confirmed Cities Bangalore (Bengaluru) and Delhi (expansion to more metros underway)
Sample Devices Galaxy M36, Galaxy M56, Galaxy F06, Galaxy Buds Core

Executive Perspectives on the Partnership

Rahul Pahwa, Director of Samsung MX in India, emphasized the consumer-centric approach: "At Samsung, we are driven by meaningful innovations that are accessible to everyone. Our partnership with Instamart is another step toward strengthening our omnichannel strategy and making Galaxy experience available to users in a matter of minutes. We are bringing our most loved devices closer to the users."

Manender Kaushik, AVP at Instamart, highlighted the evolving nature of consumer convenience: "At Instamart, our goal has always been to anticipate and adapt to the changing lifestyles of our consumers. By partnering directly with Samsung, we're ensuring that high-quality devices are now just a few taps and 10 minutes away, redefining what convenience in tech truly means."

Market Context and Competitive Landscape

This move by Samsung follows its existing partnership with Zomato-owned Blinkit, which was announced in 2024 and allowed customers in cities like Delhi-NCR, Mumbai, and Bengaluru to receive devices including the Galaxy S24 series with special offers. The expansion to Instamart represents Samsung's strategy to diversify its quick-commerce presence and reach more consumers through multiple rapid-delivery platforms.

Instamart has already been delivering devices from other tech brands including Apple, Motorola, OnePlus, and Redmi, as well as Asus laptops and various accessories. The Indian quick-commerce space for electronics is becoming increasingly competitive, with platforms like Blinkit and Zepto also offering instant smartphone delivery services.

The partnership aligns with a broader industry trend where quick-commerce platforms are expanding beyond groceries into higher-value categories like electronics, responding to consumer demand for immediate access to a wider range of products.

Benefits for Indian Consumers

The 10-minute delivery service offers several compelling advantages for Indian consumers:

Ultimate Convenience Get Galaxy devices delivered like daily essentials without visiting stores
Emergency Solutions Perfect for urgent situations like lost/broken phones or last-minute travel needs
Financial Offers Access to discounts, no-cost EMI plans, bank card offers, and coupon-based deals
Expanded Access Brings Galaxy devices closer to users who live far from brand stores
Seamless Experience Integrated purchasing through an app already used for daily needs

While the service promises 10-minute deliveries, actual delivery times may vary depending on factors such as location, traffic conditions, and weather. The companies have not yet disclosed the full list of metro cities included in the initial rollout, but expansion to more cities is expected following the initial launch in Bangalore and Delhi.

Future Implications for Tech Retail

This partnership between Samsung and Instamart signals a transformative shift in how technology products are retailed and distributed. By leveraging Instamart's established hyperlocal delivery network, Samsung gains immediate last-mile reach without significant additional infrastructure investment.

The collaboration represents more than just a new sales channel—it's part of Samsung's comprehensive omnichannel strategy to make Galaxy devices accessible through every possible touchpoint. As quick commerce continues to evolve beyond groceries, partnerships like this one are likely to become increasingly common, potentially reshaping consumer expectations around electronics purchasing and delivery timelines across the industry.

Brands already on Instamart include Apple, Motorola, OnePlus, Redmi, and Asus. Samsung's partnership strengthens Instamart's tech portfolio while expanding Samsung's retail reach.
