Samsung One UI 8 India Rollout Schedule Announced

December 01, 2025
By: Shedrack

One UI 8 India Rollout: What You Need to Know

Samsung has officially announced the detailed rollout schedule for its Android 16-based One UI 8 update in India. The company revealed the timeline through the Samsung Members app, providing clarity for millions of Galaxy users awaiting the latest software update.

The stable One UI 8 rollout began in September 2025 with the Galaxy S25 series, and is now expanding to include most eligible Galaxy devices throughout October and November 2025. This phased approach ensures stability and reliability across different device categories.

One UI 8: Key Features and Improvements

Based on Android 16, One UI 8 introduces several enhancements including improved AI capabilities, enhanced security features, and a more personalized user experience. The update brings Samsung closer to its vision of AI democratization by making advanced features accessible to more users.

Advanced AI Integration

Gemini Live allows natural communication with AI that understands visual context without app switching.

Enhanced Security

Knox Enhanced Encrypted Protection (KEEP) creates encrypted, app-specific storage environments.

Circle to Search Improvements

Real-time translations and in-the-moment gaming help with enhanced Circle to Search capabilities.

Customization

Adaptive clock design that wraps around wallpapers and enhanced FlexWindow customization.

Final Thoughts

The One UI 8 rollout in India demonstrates Samsung's commitment to providing timely software updates across its extensive device portfolio. While the update isn't as feature-rich as previous major updates, it brings important security enhancements and AI improvements that enhance the overall user experience.

Galaxy users in India should watch for update notifications in the coming weeks. The rollout follows Samsung's typical pattern, with flagship devices receiving priority followed by mid-range and budget devices. As always, the schedule remains subject to change based on carrier testing and regional variations.
