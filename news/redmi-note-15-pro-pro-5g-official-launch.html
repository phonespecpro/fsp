Redmi Note 15 Pro and Pro 5G Officially Launched With Advanced Displays and Powerful Cameras

December 18, 2025
By: Shedrack
Official Colors: Black, Brown, Blue, Purple

The Pro Series is Here: Display & Camera Focus

Xiaomi's Redmi Note series has consistently defined the mid-range smartphone segment, and with the official global launch of the Redmi Note 15 Pro and Redmi Note 15 Pro 5G, the company is raising the bar yet again. Following their initial debut in China in August 2025, these devices are now poised for international markets, bringing with them significant upgrades in display technology and camera capabilities.

The spotlight is firmly on the visual and photographic experience. Both Pro models feature advanced 6.83-inch 1.5K AMOLED displays with smooth 120Hz refresh rates and exceptional peak brightness reaching up to 3,200 nits, ensuring clarity in any lighting condition. Photographically, they are spearheaded by a formidable 200MP main camera with a large 1/1.4-inch sensor, promising exceptional detail and performance in varied lighting scenarios.

Detailed Specifications: Pro vs. Pro 5G

At a Glance: How They Compare

While sharing core philosophy, the Redmi Note 15 Pro and Pro 5G are differentiated by their chipsets, connectivity, and some build features. This table breaks down the key specifications based on the latest official information.

Note: Specifications, particularly battery size, may vary slightly between the Chinese and global models.

The Redmi Note 15 Pro+ 5G sits at the apex of the series. It shares the 200MP camera and 6.83-inch display but is distinguished by its Snapdragon 7s Gen 4 chipset and a camera system that leverages Xiaomi's "most advanced AI imaging pipeline" for superior zoom and 4K video. In Europe, it starts at €499 for the 8GB/128GB model.

Pre-orders start: January 5, 2026 India Launch: January 6, 2026

Market Context: With these launches, Redmi is directly challenging rivals in the £250-£450 segment by offering near-flagship-level displays and camera hardware. The differentiation between the Pro, Pro 5G, and Pro+ models allows consumers to choose based on their need for 5G connectivity, maximum camera AI features, and specific chipset preferences.
