Breaking News: OnePlus Unveils Special Ace Edition

OnePlus has officially announced the OnePlus 15R Ace Edition, a new gamer-focused variant of its upcoming affordable flagship, set to launch in India on December 17, 2025. This special edition introduces a striking new Electric Violet colorway and distinct design elements aimed at gaming enthusiasts.

The company stated, "OnePlus 15R is the ultimate value flagship, and the OnePlus 15R Ace Edition is perfect for those who wish to declare themselves the ultimate Ace gamer". The device will launch alongside the standard Charcoal Black and Mint Green variants of the OnePlus 15R and the new OnePlus Pad Go 2 at an event in Bengaluru.
