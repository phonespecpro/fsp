Revolutionary Design Meets Flagship Performance

Realme has taken the wraps off its most ambitious smartphone to date - the GT 8 Pro, which officially launched in India today. Following its debut in China last month, the Indian variant maintains all the key innovations that make this device stand out in the crowded flagship segment .

What sets the GT 8 Pro apart is its groundbreaking swappable camera module, a first in the smartphone industry. This innovative design allows users to customize the appearance of their device by changing the camera housing between circular, square, or unique "robot-inspired" designs . The modules are secured with tiny screws and can be swapped for different aesthetic preferences.

The GT 8 Pro features a stunning 6.79-inch AMOLED display developed in partnership with BOE, offering 2K resolution and an incredibly smooth 144Hz refresh rate . With a peak brightness of 7000 nits, this is one of the brightest smartphone displays ever created, ensuring perfect visibility even in direct sunlight.

Powering the device is Qualcomm's flagship Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor built on a 3nm process, delivering exceptional performance and power efficiency . This is complemented by LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage, creating a powerhouse capable of handling the most demanding applications and games.

Realme has partnered with Ricoh Imaging to bring professional-grade camera capabilities to the GT 8 Pro . The triple camera system is headlined by a 200MP periscope telephoto lens capable of up to 120x SuperZoom, making it one of the most powerful zoom systems in any smartphone.

The camera software includes a dedicated Ricoh GR Mode that brings signature elements from Ricoh's GR series of cameras, including distinctive film simulation modes, signature shutter sound, and Snap Mode behavior. Users can choose between various Ricoh-inspired color profiles including Standard, Positive Film, Negative Film, Monotone, and High-Contrast B&W .

The device is equipped with a massive 7000mAh Titan battery that Realme claims can provide up to 7.66 hours of continuous BGMI gameplay or over 21 hours of YouTube video streaming . With 120W wired charging, the company promises "all-day power" with just a 15-minute charge, addressing one of the biggest pain points for power users.

A New Direction for Smartphone Design

The Realme GT 8 Pro represents a significant step forward in smartphone customization and user personalization. By introducing a physically swappable camera module, Realme has challenged the conventional approach to smartphone design where aesthetics are fixed at the time of purchase.

With flagship-level specifications including the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, innovative 7000mAh battery with 120W charging, professional-grade camera system developed with Ricoh, and industry-leading display technology, the GT 8 Pro establishes itself as one of the most compelling flagship smartphones of 2025 .
