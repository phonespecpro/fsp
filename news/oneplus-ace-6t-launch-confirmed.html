OnePlus Ace 6T With Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 Officially Confirmed for Launch This Month

November 17, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

World's First Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 Smartphone

OnePlus confirms Ace 6T will debut with next-generation flagship chipset co-developed for optimal performance

Official Launch Confirmation

OnePlus has officially confirmed that the OnePlus Ace 6T will launch in China later this November, marking a significant milestone as the world's first smartphone to ship with Qualcomm's upcoming Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset . The announcement came directly from OnePlus China President Li Jie, who revealed that the company has been deeply involved in the chipset's development to optimize performance specifically for the Ace 6T .

Pre-orders for the device are already live on the Oppo China online store, though the exact launch date within November remains unspecified . The "T" designation represents OnePlus's core performance DNA, continuing the tradition established in previous T-series devices that emphasizes raw power and gaming capabilities .

Gaming & Performance Focus

OnePlus has emphasized that the Ace 6T is built specifically for gaming enthusiasts and power users. The device features a new "Wind Chaser gaming kernel" that OnePlus co-developed with Qualcomm, designed to deliver steady 165fps gameplay . The company has already secured 165Hz adaptation for at least one mainstream mobile game, which is widely believed to be Genshin Impact .

The gaming focus extends beyond raw performance to thermal management and sustained performance. OnePlus claims the Ace 6T can handle 165Hz gaming easily while maintaining stable frame rates and temperature control, addressing common pain points for mobile gamers .

Global Availability as OnePlus 15R

While the Ace 6T will debut in China this November, global markets are expected to receive the device as the OnePlus 15R . This rebranding strategy follows OnePlus's established pattern with previous Ace series devices that launched globally under the R-series naming convention.

OnePlus has already teased the OnePlus 15R debut during the global launch of the OnePlus 15, confirming that the device will reach international markets including India in the near future . The global version is expected to retain most of the core specifications while potentially offering some regional variations in storage configurations or color options.

Design & Color Options

The OnePlus Ace 6T will feature a premium build quality with a metal middle frame and is expected to include an ultrasonic in-display fingerprint sensor for faster and more secure unlocking . The device will reportedly draw design inspiration from the iconic OnePlus 6T, particularly with an "Electric Purple" colorway that pays homage to the OnePlus 6T's Thunder Purple variant .

In addition to the standard color options of Flash Black and Shadow Green, OnePlus is planning a Genshin Impact-themed special edition that will feature custom aesthetics, exclusive wallpapers, and potentially in-game rewards for fans of the popular title .

Market Positioning & Pricing

The OnePlus Ace 6T is positioned as a "performance flagship" that challenges industry conventions by offering top-tier specifications at a more accessible price point . Early reports suggest the device might be priced lower than the OnePlus Ace 6, with estimates pointing to a starting price around CNY 3,000 (approximately $420) .

This aggressive pricing strategy, combined with flagship-level specifications like the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 and 8000mAh battery, positions the Ace 6T as a compelling option in the competitive performance smartphone segment, potentially offering better value than many traditional flagships .
