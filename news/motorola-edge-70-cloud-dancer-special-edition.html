Motorola Edge 70 “Cloud Dancer” Special Edition Announced

December 5, 2025
By: Shedrack

A Fusion of Color, Craftsmanship, and Calm

Motorola has unveiled a new, highly exclusive special edition of its flagship Edge 70 smartphone, designed in collaboration with two iconic brands: Pantone and Swarovski. Dubbed the "Cloud Dancer" edition, this phone is finished in Pantone's official Color of the Year for 2026 and features carefully embedded Swarovski crystals on its back panel, marking it as a premium object of design as much as a piece of technology.

The launch continues Motorola's multi-year partnership with Pantone and expands its design-focused "Brilliant Collection", which emphasizes meticulous craftsmanship and luxury. Motorola frames this edition as a direct response to digital overload, aiming to create a device that embodies "calm confidence and refined expression".

Design Philosophy & Collaborative Details

The core of this special edition is the Pantone 11-4201 Cloud Dancer color. Described as an "airy white hue," Pantone states it communicates "clarity over clutter, softness over spectacle, and presence over pressure," acting as a symbol of calm and serenity.

Color Pantone 11-4201 Cloud Dancer – The Pantone Color of the Year 2026.
Luxury Accents Features crystals by Swarovski embedded into the back panel design for an "ethereal touch".
Design Collection Part of Motorola's Brilliant Collection, focused on timeless luxury and craftsmanship.
Back Material Leather-inspired back with a quilted pattern, providing a tactile contrast to the crystals.
Executive Statement “Cloud Dancer reflects a new kind of design mindset, one that celebrates simplicity as strength," said Ruben Castano, VP of Design at Motorola.

Full Phone Specifications

While the exterior is unique, the Cloud Dancer edition retains the complete high-end hardware of the standard Edge 70. This includes its claim to being one of the thinnest phones in its category and a powerful triple-camera system.

Dimensions & Build 5.99mm thin, 159g weight. Aircraft-grade aluminum frame with Gorilla Glass 7i. IP68/IP69 water resistance and MIL-STD-810H durability.
Display 6.67-inch pOLED, 1220 x 2712 pixels, 120Hz refresh rate, HDR10+, up to 4500 nits peak brightness, Pantone Validated for color accuracy.
Platform Qualcomm Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 chipset. 12GB RAM with 512GB storage (non-expandable). Runs Android 16 with 4 major OS update promise.
Camera System Triple 50MP cameras:
• 50MP main (OIS)
• 50MP ultrawide (120° FoV, macro vision)
• 50MP front camera
4K video recording on all sensors.
Battery & Charging 4800mAh silicon-carbon battery. 68W wired TurboPower charging (hours of power in 15 mins). 15W wireless charging support.
Audio & Connectivity Stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos. No 3.5mm jack. Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.4, NFC, 5G connectivity.

Availability, Pricing, and Market Context

Motorola has announced that the Edge 70 Cloud Dancer special edition will be available in select markets worldwide, but specific countries, release dates, and most importantly, pricing have not yet been disclosed.

For reference, the standard Motorola Edge 70 (12GB/512GB) launched in the UK at a price of £695 (~₹80,000) and in Europe starting from €799 (~₹81,000). Given the luxury materials and exclusive nature of this edition, it is expected to command a premium over the standard model.

This special edition arrives shortly after the Edge 70's initial launch in October 2025, where it was offered in three other Pantone-curated colors: Gadget Gray, Lily Pad, and Bronze Green. The collaboration underscores a growing trend of smartphone makers partnering with fashion and luxury brands to create distinctive, design-led devices for niche audiences.

Final Thoughts

The Motorola Edge 70 Cloud Dancer edition is a bold statement in a market often focused on raw specs. It successfully transforms the already sleek Edge 70 into a unique luxury accessory. The combination of the meaningful Pantone color story and the physical sparkle of Swarovski crystals creates a phone that is designed to stand out through subtlety and craftsmanship.

Bottom Line: This phone is not for everyone—it's for the buyer who prioritizes design, exclusivity, and a philosophical alignment with a calmer digital aesthetic as much as they do premium performance and camera capabilities. If the standard Edge 70's thin profile and capable cameras already appealed to you, this special edition offers a way to own that technology in a truly one-of-a-kind package.

It was initially offered in Gadget Gray, Lily Pad, and Bronze Green Pantone colors.

In the UK, the 12GB/512GB model is priced at £695, setting a baseline for the special edition's expected cost.
