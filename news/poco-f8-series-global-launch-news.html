Breaking News

POCO has officially confirmed the global launch of its highly anticipated F8 series, scheduled for November 26, 2025, in the picturesque setting of Bali, Indonesia . The launch event will mark the international debut of two flagship killers: the POCO F8 Pro and F8 Ultra, both promising to deliver exceptional performance with their premium specifications and innovative features.

The company has generated significant excitement by confirming a partnership with audio giant Bose, hinting at a "completely new acoustic design" that will provide a "smoother and more immersive gaming and multimedia experience" . This strategic collaboration represents POCO's continued focus on enhancing not just raw performance but the overall user experience.

Models Coming to Global Market

While the POCO F8 series includes three models in total, the November 26 event will focus on the two premium variants :

  • POCO F8 Ultra: The flagship model positioned as the "Leading technology flagship"
  • POCO F8 Pro: Described as the "All-round Flagship Benchmark" for balanced performance
  • POCO F8: The standard model expected to launch separately in early 2026

According to industry reports, the F8 Pro and F8 Ultra are rebranded versions of the Redmi K90 and K90 Pro Max that recently debuted in China, though with some notable modifications for the global market .

Notable Features & Changes

The POCO F8 series brings several noteworthy upgrades and modifications compared to their Chinese counterparts:

Bose Audio Partnership

POCO has emphasized its collaboration with Bose, with the F8 series boasting "brand-new acoustic components, expertly tuned by Bose engineers" . The F8 Ultra is expected to feature a 2.1 speaker system with a dedicated woofer, similar to the Redmi K90 Pro Max .

Battery Adjustments

Interestingly, reports suggest that the global versions may feature slightly smaller batteries compared to their Chinese counterparts. The F8 Pro might pack approximately 6,500mAh instead of 7,100mAh, while the F8 Ultra could have around 7,100mAh rather than 7,560mAh .

Software & Performance

Both devices are expected to run on HyperOS 3 based on Android 16 out of the box . The Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 in the F8 Ultra represents the current flagship mobile processor, promising significant performance and efficiency improvements for gaming and multitasking .

Early Reservations & Offers

POCO is already encouraging early reservations for the F8 series, offering incentives for those who sign up before the official launch. These include :

  • Discounts on the purchase price (region-dependent)
  • One free screen replacement service within 6 months
  • 24-month quality assurance
  • 100GB Google One cloud storage for 6 months

What to Expect

The POCO F8 series represents the brand's continued commitment to delivering flagship-level features at competitive price points. With the powerful Snapdragon 8 series chipsets, advanced camera systems, premium displays, and the notable Bose audio partnership, these devices are positioned to make a significant impact in the global smartphone market.

As we approach the November 26 launch date, more specific details about pricing, availability, and regional variations are expected to be revealed. Tech enthusiasts and potential buyers should stay tuned for the official announcements during the live launch event from Bali.
