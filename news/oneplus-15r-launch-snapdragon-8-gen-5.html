OnePlus 15R to Launch as the World's First Smartphone Powered by Snapdragon 8 Gen 5

November 26, 2025
By: Shedrack

üöÄ Historic Chipset Launch

OnePlus has officially confirmed that its upcoming OnePlus 15R will be the first smartphone globally to feature Qualcomm's newly-launched Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset. The device is scheduled to launch on December 17, 2025, marking a significant milestone in mobile processing technology.

In a strategic move that continues OnePlus's tradition of offering "flagship killer" devices, the company has partnered closely with Qualcomm to bring the next-generation Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 to market. According to OnePlus, this partnership represents a "Dual Flagship" chip strategy - the first time in Qualcomm's history that such close collaboration has occurred for processor development.

The OnePlus 15R will succeed the popular OnePlus 13R, which featured the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 mobile platform. With the new chipset, OnePlus promises significant performance improvements that could redefine the value flagship segment.

Snapdragon 8 Gen 5: Performance Leap

Quantifiable Performance Gains

The new Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 brings substantial improvements over its predecessor:

CPU Performance36% improvement

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 features Qualcomm's custom-built Oryon CPU, Adreno GPU, and Hexagon NPU. It includes the Snapdragon X80 5G Modem-RF System with 6Rx antenna support, capable of peak uplink speeds up to 3.5 Gbps. The integrated FastConnect 7900 Mobile Connectivity System offers Bluetooth 6 and Wi-Fi 7 connectivity.

According to Rudolf Xu, OnePlus senior product marketing manager, "It's not about who is first, it's about two brands partnering to achieve a shared vision. From day one at OnePlus, everything we build has been rooted in a single philosophy: Fast & Smooth".

Design & Durability

The OnePlus 15R will feature a design language consistent with recent R-series models, including a flat frame and distinctive camera module. OnePlus has confirmed the device will be available in Charcoal Black and Mint Breeze color options.

Where the 15R truly stands out is in its durability ratings. The phone will feature IP66, IP68, IP69, and IP69K certifications, making it exceptionally resistant to dust, water immersion, and even high-pressure, high-temperature water jets. This comprehensive protection exceeds the IP68 rating typically found on flagship devices.

The device is expected to feature a metal frame construction, maintaining the premium feel that OnePlus users expect, while the global version will likely be based on the OnePlus Ace 6T recently launched in China.

Final Thoughts

The OnePlus 15R represents a significant achievement in OnePlus's ongoing strategy to deliver flagship-level performance at accessible price points. By being the first to market with the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5, OnePlus demonstrates its strong partnership with Qualcomm and commitment to bringing cutting-edge technology to the value flagship segment.

With its massive 8000mAh battery, 165Hz display, comprehensive durability ratings, and next-generation processing power, the OnePlus 15R appears positioned to become one of the most compelling "flagship killer" devices of 2025.
