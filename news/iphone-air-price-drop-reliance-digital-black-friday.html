üíé Design & Premium Features

The iPhone Air represents Apple's boldest design innovation in recent years, measuring an incredibly slim 5.6mm thick and weighing just 165 grams, making it the thinnest and lightest iPhone ever created . The device features Ceramic Shield 2 protection on both front and back panels, ensuring durability despite its slender profile .

As an eSIM-only device running iOS 26, the iPhone Air embraces the future of connectivity while offering the full suite of Apple Intelligence features powered by the binned A19 Pro chip with second-generation Dynamic Caching technology . The 6.5-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion technology delivers buttery-smooth 120Hz performance and an impressive 3,000 nits peak brightness for excellent outdoor visibility .
