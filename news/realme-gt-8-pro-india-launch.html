Flagship Performance Meets Innovative Design

Realme has officially launched its most ambitious flagship smartphone to date in the Indian market - the GT 8 Pro. Following its successful debut in China last month, the Indian variant maintains all the groundbreaking features that make this device stand out in the competitive flagship segment .

The GT 8 Pro represents Realme's commitment to innovation with its unique switchable camera island covers, partnership with Ricoh for camera tuning, and industry-leading specifications including the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset and massive 7000mAh battery .

Display and Performance

The Realme GT 8 Pro features a stunning 6.79-inch AMOLED display with 2K resolution (3136 Ã— 1440 pixels) and an incredibly smooth 144Hz refresh rate . With a peak brightness of 7000 nits, this is one of the brightest smartphone displays ever created, ensuring perfect visibility even in direct sunlight.

Powering the device is Qualcomm's flagship Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor built on a 3nm process, delivering exceptional performance and power efficiency . The chipset is complemented by LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage, creating a powerhouse capable of handling the most demanding applications and games.

Camera System: Ricoh Partnership

Realme has partnered with Ricoh Imaging to bring professional-grade camera capabilities to the GT 8 Pro . The triple camera system is headlined by a 200MP periscope telephoto lens capable of 3x optical zoom, making it one of the most powerful zoom systems in any smartphone.

The camera software includes a dedicated Ricoh GR Mode that brings signature elements from Ricoh's GR series of cameras, including distinctive film simulation modes and professional shooting modes. Users can choose between various Ricoh-inspired color profiles including Standard, Positive Film, Negative Film, Monotone, and High-Contrast B&W .

Battery and Charging

The device is equipped with a massive 7000mAh Titan battery that supports both 120W wired charging and 50W wireless charging . Realme promises "all-day power" with just 15 minutes of charging, addressing one of the biggest pain points for power users.

The charging system is comprehensive, supporting various protocols including SuperVOOC 2.0, SuperVOOC, VOOC 3.0, QC, UFCS, PD, and PPS, making it compatible with a wide range of charging accessories .

Innovative Design Features

One of the most distinctive features of the GT 8 Pro is its switchable camera island covers, allowing users to customize the appearance of their device . This innovative design approach provides personalization options rarely seen in flagship smartphones.

The device also features an ultrasonic in-display fingerprint scanner from Goodix, stereo speakers, NFC, an IR blaster, and eSIM support including for the Indian model . The build quality is premium with IP68 and IP69 ratings for dust and water resistance.

Software and Update Policy

The Realme GT 8 Pro runs Android 16-based Realme UI 7.0 out of the box and comes with the promise of four years of Android OS upgrades and five years of security updates . This long-term software support ensures the device remains current and secure for years to come.

Realme UI 7.0 introduces several new features including improved customization options, enhanced privacy controls, and optimized performance for the flagship hardware .

Availability and Competition

The Realme GT 8 Pro is available in India through Realme's official Indian website, Flipkart, and offline retailers . The first sale began today at 12 PM IST, with both standard and Dream Edition variants available for purchase.

With its pricing and specifications, the GT 8 Pro positions itself as a direct competitor to other flagship smartphones in the Indian market, offering unique features like the switchable camera design and Ricoh partnership that differentiate it from the competition .
