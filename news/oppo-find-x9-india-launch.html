Oppo Find X9, X9 Pro Go Official in India

November 18, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Official Launch India Flagship

Oppo has officially launched its flagship Find X9 series in the Indian market, bringing top-tier specifications including MediaTek's Dimensity 9500 chipset, Hasselblad-tuned camera systems, and massive batteries that cross the 7000mAh threshold . The series, which includes the Find X9 and Find X9 Pro, marks Oppo's continued push in the premium smartphone segment with competitive pricing and flagship features.

Both smartphones will be available for purchase starting November 21 through Oppo's Indian website, Amazon.in, Flipkart, and offline retail stores . The company has also introduced the Oppo Hasselblad Teleconverter Kit, priced at ₹29,999, which is compatible exclusively with the Find X9 Pro .

Key Specifications Comparison

Display and Design

Find X9: 6.59-inch AMOLED display with 2760×1256 resolution, 120Hz refresh rate, and 3600 nits peak brightness. Protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 7i .

Find X9 Pro: Larger 6.78-inch LTPO AMOLED display with 2772×1272 resolution, 120Hz refresh rate, and same 3600 nits peak brightness. Features Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection .

Performance

Both models are powered by MediaTek's flagship Dimensity 9500 chipset built on 3nm process technology, paired with LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage . The devices run ColorOS 16 based on Android 16 out of the box, with promised support for 5 major OS upgrades and 6 years of security updates .

Battery and Charging

Find X9: 7025mAh silicon-carbon battery with 80W wired and 50W wireless charging support .

Find X9 Pro: Larger 7500mAh silicon-carbon battery with same 80W wired and 50W wireless charging capabilities .

Camera Systems: Hasselblad Partnership

Find X9 Camera Setup

Triple rear camera system: 50MP Sony LYT-808 main sensor with OIS, 50MP ultrawide camera, and 50MP Sony LYT-600 periscope telephoto with 3x optical zoom and OIS . Front-facing 32MP selfie camera .

Find X9 Pro Camera Upgrade

Advanced triple camera: 50MP Sony LYT-828 main sensor, 50MP ultrawide, and groundbreaking 200MP periscope telephoto with 3x optical zoom and OIS . Features a 50MP front camera for selfies .

Both devices feature Oppo's new Lumo Imaging Engine that utilizes computational photography for enhanced image processing . The Hasselblad partnership brings natural color calibration and professional photography features to both devices.

Build Quality and Durability

The Find X9 series boasts impressive durability credentials with IP66, IP68, and IP69 ratings for water and dust resistance . This makes the devices resistant to high-pressure water jets and immersion up to 1.5 meters for 30 minutes, providing comprehensive protection against various environmental conditions.

Market Context and Competition

The Find X9 series enters a competitive Indian flagship market, positioned against devices like the OnePlus 15, iQOO 15, and Vivo X300 series . With its combination of MediaTek's latest chipset, Hasselblad cameras, and large batteries, Oppo aims to capture significant market share in the premium segment.

Early Impressions

Initial reports highlight the devices' exceptional battery life thanks to the large silicon-carbon batteries, smooth performance from the Dimensity 9500 chipset, and versatile camera systems particularly praising the 200MP telephoto on the Pro model . The Hasselblad color tuning has been noted for delivering natural and accurate colors in various lighting conditions.

With competitive pricing and flagship specifications, the Oppo Find X9 series presents a compelling option for Indian consumers looking for premium smartphones with exceptional camera capabilities and all-day battery life. The November 21 sales date gives consumers immediate access to these new devices just in time for the festive season.
