Realme Narzo 90 and Narzo 90x 5G Go Official in India With 7,000 mAh Battery and Dual 50 MP Cameras

December 16, 2025
By: Shedrack

Realme has officially launched its much-anticipated Narzo 90 series in India today, December 16, 2025 . The lineup consists of two smartphones: the Realme Narzo 90 5G and the Realme Narzo 90x 5G, both targeting the budget and mid-range segments with a focus on exceptional battery life, capable performance, and solid camera systems .

The highlight of the new series is undoubtedly the massive 7,000mAh "Titan Battery" that powers both devices, promising to end battery anxiety for even the heaviest users . Paired with this powerhouse battery are capable MediaTek Dimensity chipsets and camera systems headlined by 50MP sensors, making the Narzo 90 series a compelling option in its price segment .

The 7,000mAh Titan Battery: Ending Battery Anxiety

Both the Narzo 90 and Narzo 90x are equipped with a massive 7,000mAh Titan Battery, which Realme claims can deliver up to two days of usage on a single charge under typical conditions . The battery supports 60W fast charging (VOOC Charging v3.0) and also features 6.5W reverse wired charging, allowing you to use your phone as a power bank for other devices .

Realme has implemented a "6-Year Health Battery" technology with bypass longevity charging to maintain battery health over extended use . The company claims the battery retains usable power even at 1%, allowing for navigation or sending urgent messages without sudden shutdowns .

Camera Capabilities: 50MP Photography

Both devices in the Narzo 90 series feature capable camera systems centered around 50MP primary sensors, though with different implementations .

The Realme Narzo 90 5G features a dual rear camera setup with a 50MP primary sensor accompanied by a 2MP depth sensor . For selfies, it boasts a high-resolution 50MP front camera . The device includes AI-powered photography features such as AI Edit Genie, AI Eraser, AI Unblur, and AI Snap Mode .

The Realme Narzo 90x 5G sports a single 50MP Sony IMX852 rear camera with an f/1.8 aperture . It also supports AI Edit Genie and comes with an 8MP front camera for selfies . Both phones support video recording at up to 1080p resolution at 30fps .

Design, Colors, and Durability

The Narzo 90 series features distinct designs for each model. The Narzo 90 5G has been noted for its resemblance to the iPhone 16 Pro with its triple rear camera design (though it only has two sensors) and comes in Victory Gold and Carbon Black color options . The gold variant features three distinctive stripes on the back .

The Narzo 90x 5G features a vertically arranged camera module on the rear with a small ring light and flash . It is available in Nitro Blue and Flash Blue shades .

In terms of durability, the Narzo 90 5G boasts impressive IP66 + IP68 + IP69 ratings for dust and water resistance . The Narzo 90x 5G comes with an IP65 rating, making it resistant to dust and water splashes . Both devices feature ArmorShell™ Protection and have undergone rigorous testing including a micro drop test 14,000 times and 300% bend resistance .

The Realme Narzo 90 series is competitively priced in the Indian market, with the Narzo 90x positioned as the more budget-friendly option and the Narzo 90 as the slightly more premium offering .

Availability: The Narzo 90x 5G will go on sale first on December 23, 2025, while the Narzo 90 5G will be available starting December 24, 2025. Both devices will be sold via Amazon and the Realme India online store .

Additional Features and Software

Both smartphones run on Android 15 with Realme UI 6.0 out of the box . The Narzo 90 is promised three Android OS updates and four years of security updates .

Other notable features include 400% Ultra Volume for louder audio output , Wet-Hand Touch Technology for reliable screen response even with wet hands , and comprehensive AI features including AI Network Boost, AI Brightness Boost, AI Performance Boost, and Outdoor Mode .

For connectivity, both devices support 5G (SA/NSA), dual 4G VoLTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.3/5.4, and multiple navigation systems (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, QZSS) .

Which One to Choose?

Choose Narzo 90 5G if:

  • You prefer AMOLED display with FHD+ resolution
  • Need better durability (IP66+IP68+IP69)
  • Want higher 50MP front camera
  • Prioritize slightly better performance (Dimensity 6400 Max)

  • You're on a tighter budget
  • Prefer higher 144Hz refresh rate
  • Want larger 6.8-inch display
