How the Pixel Upgrade Program Works

1

Purchase & Enroll

Buy an eligible Pixel 10 series phone from partner retail stores on a 24-month no-cost EMI plan through Bajaj Finance or HDFC Bank Credit Cards. Enroll in the program on Cashify's website within 30 days of purchase.

2

Use & Enjoy

Use your Pixel phone while making monthly EMI payments. After completing just nine payments (and before the fifteenth EMI), you become eligible to upgrade.

3

Upgrade Seamlessly

Trade in your current Pixel through Cashify. They will credit your bank account with the exact remaining loan balance, allowing you to close the original loan without pre-closure charges.

4

Get New Pixel

Start a fresh 24-month no-cost EMI plan for your brand-new Pixel phone. The cycle continues, ensuring you always have the latest technology.

Why This Matters for Indian Consumers

The Pixel Upgrade Program represents a significant shift in how premium smartphones are marketed and sold in India.
