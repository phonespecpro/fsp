Realme Narzo 90 and Narzo 90x Launch Date Confirmed for India

December 9, 2025
By: Shedrack

Official Launch Announcement

Realme has officially confirmed that its next-generation Narzo series smartphones, the Realme Narzo 90 and Realme Narzo 90x, will launch in India on December 16, 2025, at 12:00 PM IST. The company revealed the launch date through its social media channels and updated Amazon microsites, confirming that both phones will be available for purchase via Amazon India and Realme's official website.

Positioned as successors to the Narzo 80 lineup, these new models are designed to deliver what Realme calls "strong value for money" in the mid-range segment. The Narzo 90 is targeted at young students and entry-level professionals, while the Narzo 90x is specifically developed for college students with active digital lifestyles.

Confirmed Specifications & Features

Battery & Charging

Both phones in the Narzo 90 series will feature a massive 7,000mAh Titan Battery with 60W fast charging support. This marks the first time a Narzo series phone will include such a large battery capacity. Realme claims the battery can provide up to:

  • Narzo 90: 23.2 hours of video playback, 92.5 hours of music, 10 hours of gaming, or 26.9 hours of calls on a full charge.
  • Narzo 90x: 19 hours of video, 23.6 hours of gaming, 89.7 hours of music, or 104.2 hours of calls.

Remarkably, just a 6-minute charge can provide several hours of usage, with Realme also offering a 6-year battery health guarantee (over 1,200 charge cycles) for the Narzo 90. Both devices support bypass charging and reverse charging capabilities.

Display Technology

The Narzo 90 series introduces high refresh rate displays to the Narzo lineup for the first time:

  • Realme Narzo 90: Features a display with an impressive 4,000 nits peak brightness, designed to remain legible in bright outdoor conditions.
  • Realme Narzo 90x: Boasts a 144Hz refresh rate display with 1,200 nits peak brightness, optimized for smooth gaming and multimedia consumption.

Camera System

Photography capabilities receive a significant boost in both models:

  • Realme Narzo 90: Marketed as having "India's Only Dual 50MP Camera" system, though it's unclear whether this refers to two rear cameras or a combination of rear and front cameras.
  • Realme Narzo 90x: Features a 50MP Sony AI camera with advanced computational photography capabilities.

Both devices will include several AI-based camera editing features such as AI Edit Genie, AI Editor, and AI Eraser.

Design & Durability

The two models feature distinct design philosophies:

  • Realme Narzo 90: Showcases an "iPhone 16 Pro-like triple camera cutout" with three vertical racing stripes in Orange, Black, and Silver color options. Realme calls this the "Victory Power Design." The phone also boasts exceptional durability with IP66, IP68, and IP69 ratings for dust and water resistance.
  • Realme Narzo 90x: Features a rectangular dual camera design with an RGB Ring Light module at the back, available in light and dark blue variants.

Despite housing massive 7,000mAh batteries, both phones maintain sleek, premium designs according to Realme's claims.

Pricing Expectations & Availability

While Realme hasn't officially revealed pricing details, industry reports suggest both phones will be competitively priced under ₹15,000 in India. The Narzo 90x is expected to start at approximately ₹17,999 according to some sources.

Following the December 16 launch event, both smartphones will be available for purchase exclusively through Amazon India and Realme's official website. Realme typically follows a flash sale model for new launches, so interested buyers should prepare for quick sell-outs initially.

What This Launch Means

The Realme Narzo 90 series represents a significant upgrade over its predecessors, particularly with the introduction of massive 7,000mAh batteries and high refresh rate displays to the Narzo lineup. By targeting specific user segments—students and young professionals with the Narzo 90, and multimedia-focused college students with the Narzo 90x—Realme appears to be refining its strategy in the competitive mid-range market.

With key specifications already confirmed through official channels, the December 16 launch event will likely focus on final pricing, availability details, and any unannounced features such as processor specifications and software enhancements. The series' success will depend on how these impressive specifications translate to real-world performance at their expected price points.
