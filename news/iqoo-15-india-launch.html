iQOO 15 India Launch Announced: Snapdragon 8 Elite and 7,000mAh Battery for Gaming Power

November 6, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

INDIA, NOVEMBER 5, 2025 - iQOO has officially confirmed the India launch of its flagship iQOO 15 smartphone on November 26, positioning the device as a gaming powerhouse with Qualcomm's latest Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor and a massive 7,000mAh battery .

The device, which made its global debut in China last month, now prepares for the Indian market with what iQOO describes as "Not just a flagship. The flagship" - targeting gaming enthusiasts and power users with top-tier specifications and gaming-optimized features .

"The iQOO 15 represents our commitment to delivering flagship-level innovation for Indian gamers and power users," said company representatives. "With the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 and our advanced cooling system, we're setting new standards for mobile gaming performance."

Flagship Gaming Features

Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5

3nm processor with 8 cores up to 4.61GHz for elite gaming performance

7000mAh Battery

Massive silicon-carbon battery with 100W wired and 40W wireless charging

144Hz Display

6.85" 2K LTPO AMOLED with 144Hz refresh rate and 6000 nits peak brightness

Advanced Cooling

India's largest 8K VC cooling system for sustained gaming performance

Elite Gaming Performance

At the heart of the iQOO 15 lies the Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 mobile platform, featuring an 8-core CPU with two high-performance cores clocked at up to 4.61GHz and six efficiency cores at 3.63GHz, paired with the Adreno 840 GPU . This 3nm chipset demonstrates impressive benchmark performance with Geekbench scores of 3,558 (single-core) and 10,128 (multi-core), placing it among the most powerful smartphones available .

Gaming-Specific Enhancements

The iQOO 15 integrates several gaming-focused technologies including the Q3 Supercomputing gaming chip for enhanced stability and graphics processing, along with ray tracing support for more realistic gaming visuals . The device also features an 8,000 sq mm vapor chamber cooling system - one of the largest in the industry - to maintain optimal temperatures during extended gaming sessions .

Premium Display & Design

The iQOO 15 features a stunning 6.85-inch Samsung M14 LTPO AMOLED display with 2K resolution (1440 x 3168 pixels) and a smooth 144Hz refresh rate . With peak brightness reaching 6000 nits and support for Dolby Vision, HDR10+, and HDR Vivid, the display offers exceptional visibility even in direct sunlight while delivering vibrant, accurate colors for both gaming and media consumption .

Design-wise, the device maintains a premium aesthetic with an aluminum frame and IP68/IP69 ratings for comprehensive dust and water resistance . The Indian variant will be available in multiple color options, including the special Alpha Edition in matte black and Legend Edition in white with tri-color pattern branding .

Triple 50MP Camera System

The iQOO 15 features a versatile triple camera setup, with each sensor boasting 50MP resolution . The system includes a 50MP Sony IMX921 primary sensor with OIS, a 50MP Sony IMX882 periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom and OIS, and a 50MP ultra-wide-angle camera for group shots and landscapes .

For selfie enthusiasts, the device houses a 32MP front camera capable of recording 4K video . The camera system supports 8K video recording at 30fps and various high-frame-rate options, making it suitable for both photography and videography needs .

Software Experience & Pricing

The iQOO 15 marks a significant software transition in India, becoming the first device in the country to ship with OriginOS 6 based on Android 16, replacing the previous FuntouchOS . This new software experience brings improved customization options, enhanced performance optimization, and gaming-specific features tailored for the Indian market.

Pricing & Availability

While iQOO hasn't revealed official pricing for the Indian market, reports suggest the iQOO 15 may start around ₹59,999 - ₹64,999, positioning it slightly higher than its predecessor due to significant hardware upgrades . The device will be available through Amazon India and other authorized retailers starting November 26, following the official launch event .

A New Gaming Flagship Contender

The iQOO 15 enters the competitive Indian flagship market with an impressive combination of top-tier performance, gaming-focused features, and exceptional battery life. The Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, combined with the massive 7,000mAh battery and advanced cooling system, positions the device as a compelling option for gaming enthusiasts and power users.

With its November 26 launch, the iQOO 15 aims to challenge established players in the premium smartphone segment, offering flagship specifications at a potentially more accessible price point. The device's gaming optimizations, including the Q3 Supercomputing chip and ray tracing support, further strengthen its position as a dedicated gaming smartphone that doesn't compromise on other flagship features.

As the launch date approaches, all eyes will be on iQOO's final pricing strategy, which could determine whether the iQOO 15 becomes a true flagship killer or remains a niche offering for gaming enthusiasts in the Indian market.
