Google's AI Mode Expands to Handle Restaurant Bookings, Flights, and Itineraries

November 18, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
AI Technology Google Update Travel Tech

Google has announced a significant expansion of its AI Mode capabilities, transforming how users plan and book travel experiences. The November 2025 update introduces three major features: Canvas for travel planning, global expansion of Flight Deals, and agentic booking for restaurants, events, and local appointments .

These enhancements represent Google's strategic push to handle complete user journeys within Search, from initial research to final booking, creating a more integrated travel planning experience .

Canvas: Your AI Travel Planning Assistant

Canvas, previously available for general planning tasks, now specializes in creating detailed travel itineraries within AI Mode's side panel. Users can describe their trip requirements in natural language and generate comprehensive plans combining real-time flight and hotel data, Google Maps information, and relevant web content .

Smart Itinerary Building

Canvas generates day-by-day suggestions for activities, restaurants, and accommodations based on your preferences and optimizes them by travel time from your location .

Interactive Refinement

Ask follow-up questions and get help with tradeoffs, like choosing between proximity to dining versus attractions. The AI regenerates plans based on new requirements .

Continuous Access

Save and revisit your travel plans through AI Mode history, allowing for planning across multiple sessions .

Availability

Travel planning with Canvas is available on desktop in the U.S. for users opted into the AI Mode experiment in Google Labs .

Flight Deals: Global AI-Powered Bargain Hunting

Google's AI-powered Flight Deals tool, previously limited to the U.S., Canada, and India, is now expanding globally to more than 200 countries and territories . The feature uses natural language processing to understand travel preferences and find the best airfare bargains for flexible travelers.

Users can describe where, when, and how they'd like to travel using conversational language, and Flight Deals will use AI to display the most affordable destinations matching their criteria . The tool now supports more than 60 languages, making it accessible to travelers worldwide .

Agentic Booking: From Planning to Reservation

Google is rolling out AI Mode's agentic capabilities to more users, enabling direct booking of restaurants, event tickets, and local appointments through AI conversations . This technology searches across multiple reservation platforms simultaneously to find real-time availability.

How Agentic Booking Works

Users describe what they're looking for with specific preferences, and AI Mode searches across multiple reservation platforms to find options meeting their criteria . The system presents curated results with direct booking links to partner sites.

Booking Partners

OpenTable Resy Tock Ticketmaster StubHub SeatGeek Vivid Seats Booksy Fresha Vagaro

Current Availability

Restaurant booking is rolling out this week on AI Mode in the U.S. without requiring Labs opt-in . Event tickets and local appointments remain available to U.S. Labs users .

The Future: Flight and Hotel Bookings in AI Mode

Google confirmed it's developing agentic capabilities for flight and hotel bookings, though no specific timeline has been announced . The company is working with major travel partners including Booking.com, Expedia, Marriott International, IHG Hotels & Resorts, Choice Hotels International, and Wyndham Hotels & Resorts to build this experience .

According to Julie Farago, Google's vice president of engineering for travel and local search, the company is being "super thoughtful" about these developments since flight and hotel bookings tend to be large, infrequent purchases . Google will not serve as merchant of record; partner companies will manage transactions and service bookings made through AI Mode .

Industry Impact and Competitive Landscape

These updates continue Google's pattern of verticalizing high-value query types, handling complete user journeys from research to transaction within its own ecosystem . Rather than presenting traditional search results that send users to external sites, AI Mode now guides users through multi-step travel planning processes .

The travel technology space is increasingly competitive, with OpenAI's ChatGPT also featuring travel booking integrations with Expedia and Booking.com . However, Google's established relationships across the travel industry and integration with its existing services like Google Flights and Google Maps may provide a competitive advantage .

What This Means for Travelers

For consumers, these AI Mode enhancements represent a significant reduction in the friction traditionally associated with travel planning. The ability to research destinations, compare flights and hotels, plan daily itineraries, and book reservations through conversational AI creates a more streamlined experience .

As Google continues to develop these capabilities, travelers can expect even more integrated experiences where AI handles the busywork of trip planning, allowing users to focus on the enjoyment of travel itself .
