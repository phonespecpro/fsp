Realme 16 Pro+ Box Leak Hints at Return of Periscope Telephoto Camera

December 10, 2025
By: Shedrack

The Silver Box That Gave It Away

An image of the Realme 16 Pro+ retail packaging has surfaced online, and it clearly hints at a significant camera upgrade. The box design points to the inclusion of a periscope telephoto lens, a premium feature that was present in the Realme 14 Pro+ but notably missing from the entire Realme 15 series .

The Camera Comeback Story

Realme appears to be course-correcting with its upcoming flagship. After launching the Realme 15 Pro without a telephoto camera and skipping a Pro+ model altogether, the brand seems ready to reclaim its position in the competitive mid-range camera phone segment . This strategic move, hinted at by the box leak, is seen by industry watchers as a direct response to the rumored underwhelming sales of the Realme 15 series, pushing the company to pack its next device with more compelling hardware .

Launch Timeline & Expected Specifications

Multiple leaks and tipsters, including Sanju Choudhary, have converged on a single date: the Realme 16 Pro series is scheduled to launch in India on January 6, 2026 . This would be ahead of the brand's typical first-quarter release cycle, suggesting an eager push to get the new phones to market .

While the company has started teasing the phone on its Indian website, confirming a powerful Snapdragon chipset (more potent than the Snapdragon 7 Gen 4) and impressive battery life stats, the box leak adds a crucial piece to the puzzle .

Why the Periscope Lens is a Big Deal

The return of the periscope camera is significant for several reasons. A periscope lens uses a prism and a sideways-mounted lens to achieve true optical zoom (reportedly up to 10x in this case) without making the phone excessively thick, allowing for much clearer long-distance shots compared to digital zoom .

Premium Feature

This technology is typically reserved for higher-end flagship phones. Its inclusion in the 16 Pro+ marks Realme's push to elevate the device's market position .

Competitive Edge

In a crowded mid-range market, a periscope lens gives the 16 Pro+ a distinct advantage over rivals that often settle for lower-quality digital or hybrid zoom .

Pricing Implications

This premium hardware likely means the Realme 16 Pro+ will be more expensive than the Realme 15 Pro. Early estimates suggest a price range of ₹35,000 to ₹40,000 in India .

The Official Tease & What's Next

Adding credibility to the leaks, Realme has officially confirmed the upcoming launch of the 16 Pro series in India and has opened a microsite to tease its features . The company confirms a powerful Snapdragon chipset and boasts exceptional battery endurance, claiming up to 9.3 hours of gaming and 21 hours of video playback . The phone will also ship with the latest Realme UI 7 based on Android 16 .

Final Notes

As with all pre-launch leaks, it's wise to treat this information as highly credible but not 100% final until Realme's official announcement, which is expected very soon given the January 6 launch date . However, the combination of a physical box leak, multiple consistent tipsters, and official teasers makes a very strong case for the triumphant return of the periscope camera in Realme's 2026 flagship.
