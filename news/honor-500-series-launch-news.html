Honor 500 Series Confirmed to Launch on November 24

November 17, 2025

Official Launch Confirmation

Honor has officially confirmed that its highly anticipated 500 series will debut in China on November 24, 2025, at 7:30 PM CST (4:30 PM IST) . The lineup includes two models: the standard Honor 500 and the more premium Honor 500 Pro, both featuring significant upgrades over their predecessors and adopting a fresh design philosophy inspired by the iPhone Air.

Pre-orders for both devices are already live on Honor's official China website, giving enthusiasts an early opportunity to reserve their units in various color options and storage configurations . The company has been actively teasing the devices, revealing key details about their design and some specifications ahead of the formal unveiling.

Design & Color Options

Both Honor 500 series models feature a distinctive horizontal camera bar that draws clear inspiration from Apple's iPhone Air design language . The phones sport flat displays with hole-punch cutouts and slim, uniform bezels all around, complemented by flat back panels.

A notable design addition is a new customizable button positioned below the power button, which is expected to serve as a dedicated AI button for quick access to artificial intelligence features . This hardware addition highlights Honor's focus on AI integration in their 2025 smartphone lineup.

Notable Features & Innovations

Massive Battery Capacity

Both models in the Honor 500 series are expected to feature an impressive 8000mAh silicon battery, as confirmed by Honor's product manager and multiple leaks . This substantial capacity significantly exceeds most competitors in the sub-flagship segment and promises exceptional battery life for power users.

Advanced Display Technology

The 6.55-inch displays on both phones not only offer 120Hz refresh rates for smooth scrolling and animations but also feature 3,840 Hz PWM dimming . This high-frequency dimming reduces eye strain during low-light usage, making these displays among the most comfortable for extended reading or multimedia consumption.

Premium Build & Durability

The Honor 500 series is expected to offer comprehensive durability certifications with IP68/IP69/IP69K ratings for dust and water resistance . Additionally, both models will feature a 3D ultrasonic fingerprint sensor for faster and more secure biometric authentication compared to optical sensors .

While official pricing for the Honor 500 series remains under wraps, the previous Honor 400 series launched at approximately $347 for the standard model and $472 for the Pro variant in China . If Honor maintains similar pricing strategy, the 500 series could offer compelling value given its flagship-level specifications.

Market Context & Availability

The November 24 launch places the Honor 500 series in a highly competitive period, with Huawei and OnePlus also scheduled to launch new devices around the same time . This timing suggests Honor is confident in its product's ability to stand out in a crowded market.

Initial availability will be limited to the Chinese market, with global release dates expected to be announced in the coming months. The phones will ship with MagicOS 10 based on Android 16 out of the box , offering the latest software experience with Honor's customizations and features.

What to Expect

The Honor 500 series represents a significant step forward for Honor's sub-flagship lineup, combining flagship-level processors with exceptional battery capacity and capable camera systems. The November 24 launch event will provide complete details on pricing, availability, and any additional features Honor has kept under wraps.

With pre-orders already active in China, consumer interest appears strong for these devices that bridge the gap between mid-range and flagship smartphones. The combination of powerful chipsets, massive batteries, and premium features at expected competitive price points could make the Honor 500 series one of the most compelling smartphone launches in the latter part of 2025.
