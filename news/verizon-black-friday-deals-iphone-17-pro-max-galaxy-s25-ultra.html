How to Get These Verizon Deals

Important Requirements

These incredible bundle deals come with specific requirements that customers should understand before purchasing :

  • Require new smartphone line activation
  • Must choose Unlimited Ultimate or Unlimited Plus plan
  • 36-month installment agreement
  • Must add service plans for watch and tablet in same cart
  • Available for 256GB storage models only
  • Device activation fees and taxes still apply
Read the Fine Print

While these deals offer tremendous value, they involve long-term commitments. If you cancel your Verizon service before the 36-month period ends, you may be responsible for the remaining balance of the devices . These are essentially 3-year contracts with Verizon, so ensure you're comfortable with the commitment before proceeding.

Additional Verizon Black Friday Offers

Beyond the major bundle deals, Verizon is running several other attractive promotions for Black Friday 2025 :

  • iPhone 17 Family Bundle - Get four iPhone 17 devices free with new lines on Unlimited plans (save $3,320)
  • Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 7 - $900 (save $1,100 with eligible trade-in)
  • iPhone 17 Accessory Bundle - $100 (save $50 on case, screen protector, and charger)
  • Google Pixel Buds Pro 2 - $170 (save $60 when purchased with Pixel phone)
  • Apple Watch SE 3 - $8/month (save $300 with trade-in and Unlimited plan)

Deal Availability and Timing

Verizon's Black Friday deals are available for a limited time, with most offers expected to run through Cyber Monday on December 1, 2025 . However, some deals may end sooner, so interested customers should act quickly to secure these offers.

Pro Shopping Tip

When adding these bundle deals to your cart, don't try to add all items separately. Verizon's website is designed to guide you through the bundle process automatically once you add the primary smartphone to your cart . This ensures you get all the free devices properly applied to your order.

Final Thoughts

Verizon's 2025 Black Friday deals represent some of the most aggressive carrier promotions we've seen in recent years. The ability to get complete technology ecosystems - phone, watch, and tablet - without any trade-in requirements makes these offers particularly compelling for new Verizon customers or those looking to fully upgrade their device collections.

While the 36-month commitment is substantial, the value proposition is undeniable for customers who are already considering Verizon's premium unlimited plans. As always, we recommend carefully reviewing all terms and conditions and ensuring the plan costs fit within your budget before committing to these long-term agreements.
