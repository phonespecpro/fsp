Nothing Phone 3a Lite Goes on Sale in India — Price, Launch Offers, and Full Specifications

December 5, 2025
By: Shedrack

Nothing Phone 3a Lite Now Available in India

Nothing's latest mid-range smartphone, the Phone 3a Lite, has officially gone on sale in India as of December 5, 2025. The phone, which was initially launched in the country on November 27, aims to deliver the brand's signature design language, clean software, and unique features at a more accessible price point.

Sales have commenced through major online and offline channels, including Flipkart, Vijay Sales, and Croma, allowing customers across India to purchase the device. The launch is accompanied by several introductory offers from banks and e-commerce platforms, making the phone an even more compelling proposition in the competitive sub-₹25,000 segment.

Price in India and Launch Offers

The Nothing Phone 3a Lite is positioned aggressively in the Indian market, with a starting price that brings key features of the Nothing ecosystem to a wider audience.

Official Launch Price (India) ₹20,999 for 8GB + 128GB
Higher Storage Variant ₹22,999 for 8GB + 256GB
Effective Discounted Price ₹19,999 for the 128GB model after bank offers
Key Launch Offers Instant discount of ₹1,000 for ICICI Bank and OneCard customers. Additional cashback offers on Flipkart Axis Bank and SBI cards.
Exchange Bonus Up to ₹17,250 off with an old smartphone exchange on Flipkart
Availability Flipkart, Vijay Sales, Croma, and other leading retail stores
Color Options Black, White, and an India-exclusive Blue variant

Full Specifications and Key Features

Despite its "Lite" moniker, the Nothing Phone 3a Lite packs a robust set of specifications and several signature Nothing features, including a new, simplified Glyph Interface and the innovative Essential Key.

Display 6.77-inch FHD+ (1080 x 2392) Flexible AMOLED. 120Hz adaptive refresh rate. Impressive 3000 nits peak HDR brightness. Protected by Panda Glass.
Processor & Performance MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Pro chipset (4nm). Octa-core CPU (4x Cortex-A78 & 4x Cortex-A55). Paired with 8GB of LPDDR4X RAM and up to 8GB of Virtual RAM expansion.
Storage 128GB or 256GB of UFS 2.2 internal storage. Expandable via microSD card (Hybrid Slot) up to 2TB — a rare feature in modern smartphones.
Operating System Runs Android 15 with Nothing OS 3.5 out of the box. Committed to 3 major Android OS updates and 6 years of security updates. The Android 16 update is confirmed for early 2026.
Rear Camera System Triple Camera Setup:
• 50MP primary sensor with f/1.9 aperture, OIS (Optical Image Stabilization), and 1/1.57" sensor size.
• 8MP ultra-wide-angle lens with 120˚ field of view.
• 2MP macro sensor.
Capable of 4K video recording at 30fps.
Front Camera 16MP sensor housed in a punch-hole cutout. Supports 1080p video recording.
Battery & Charging Large 5000mAh battery. Supports 33W wired fast charging (50% in 20 minutes). Includes 5W reverse wired charging. Note: Charger is not included in the box.
Design & Durability Glass front and back (Panda Glass) with a plastic frame. Features the signature Glyph Interface, now in a new, simplified single-LED layout for notifications. Rated IP54 for dust and splash resistance.
Unique Features • Essential Key: A dedicated hardware button for quick-saving content, voice notes, and accessing the AI-powered Essential Space hub.
• Other features include an in-display fingerprint sensor, NFC, and stereo speakers. No 3.5mm headphone jack.

Market Position and Final Thoughts

The Nothing Phone 3a Lite enters a fiercely competitive segment in India, going up against popular models from brands like Motorola, Xiaomi, and Realme. Its strengths lie in offering a premium-looking AMOLED display with a high refresh rate, a clean and well-supported software experience with Nothing OS, and unique differentiators like the Glyph Interface and Essential Key.

However, potential buyers should note the compromises typical for the price, such as the use of a plastic frame, the absence of a charger in the box, and a camera system that relies heavily on its primary 50MP sensor.

Bottom Line: The Nothing Phone 3a Lite is a compelling option for users seeking a distinctive design, a smooth software experience with long-term update promises, and capable daily performance at around the ₹20,000 mark. The limited-time bank discounts at launch make it an even more attractive value proposition for early buyers.
