This positions the compact device as a serious competitor to full-sized flagships despite its smaller form factor.

Official Confirmation from Vivo Executive

In an exciting development for smartphone enthusiasts, Vivo executive Han Bo Xiao has officially confirmed on Weibo that the upcoming S50 Pro Mini will be powered by Qualcomm's flagship Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset . The revelation came alongside impressive benchmark numbers that showcase the device's raw performance capabilities.

The confirmation puts to rest speculation about the chipset configuration and positions the S50 Pro Mini as one of the most powerful compact smartphones ever created. According to Han Bo Xiao, the device achieved approximately 3 million points on AnTuTu v10 during regular room-temperature testing conditions .

Benchmark Performance Analysis

AnTuTu Version v10 (some reports mention v11)

Performance Gain Over 1 million points vs previous sub-flagships

The benchmark score of around 3 million points represents a massive performance leap over previous-generation devices in the same category . This score not only surpasses Vivo's own X200 Ultra, which scored approximately 2.9 million points in earlier tests, but also demonstrates a 600,000-point advantage over the X200 Pro mini with its Dimensity 9400 Plus chipset .

What makes this achievement particularly remarkable is that the S50 Pro Mini managed this performance in normal room-temperature conditions, suggesting efficient thermal management despite the compact form factor . The device utilizes the same LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.1 storage combination found in Vivo's flagship X300 series and X200 Ultra .

Camera System: No Compromises

Despite its compact dimensions, the Vivo S50 Pro Mini doesn't cut corners on camera capabilities. The device features a sophisticated dual-camera setup headlined by a 50MP Sony IMX9-series main sensor paired with a 50MP Sony IMX882 periscope telephoto lens . This configuration is particularly impressive for a compact device and matches the camera hardware found in much larger flagship smartphones.

The inclusion of a periscope telephoto lens suggests Vivo is targeting photography enthusiasts who prefer smaller form factors without sacrificing optical zoom capabilities. The horizontal camera bar design, reminiscent of modern flagship designs, houses these sophisticated imaging components while maintaining the device's sleek profile.

Launch Timeline and Availability

Vivo has confirmed through a pre-reservation page that the S50 series will launch in China this December, though an exact date hasn't been announced yet . The company has already begun teaser campaigns and is allowing customers to register their interest through official channels.

Industry reports suggest that the S50 Pro Mini might be rebranded as the Vivo X300 FE for global markets, following Vivo's pattern of releasing S-series devices internationally under different naming conventions . The Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset that powers the device is scheduled for full unveiling by Qualcomm on November 26, 2025 .

Market Impact and Competition

The Vivo S50 Pro Mini represents a significant development in the smartphone market, proving that compact devices can deliver flagship-level performance without compromises. With its powerful chipset, generous battery capacity, and advanced camera system, it challenges the conventional trade-offs associated with smaller form factors.

This device enters a growing segment of premium compact smartphones, competing with offerings from other manufacturers while establishing Vivo as a serious player in the high-end compact phone market. The impressive benchmark scores and flagship specifications position the S50 Pro Mini as an attractive option for users seeking top-tier performance in a more manageable size.
