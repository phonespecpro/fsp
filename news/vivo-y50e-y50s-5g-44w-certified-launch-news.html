Vivo Y50e and Y50s 5G Certified With 44W Fast Charging Ahead of Official Debut

December 18, 2025
By: Shedrack
Latest Update: As of December 18, 2025, new listings confirm Vivo is expanding its Y50 series with two new models, following the July 2025 launch of the Y50 5G and Y50m 5G.
Concept Image Based on Y50 Series

The Story

Vivo appears to be preparing to expand its affordable Y50 5G series with two new models. The Vivo Y50e 5G and Vivo Y50s 5G have been spotted on the Google Play Console's list of supported devices, signaling that their official launch could be just around the corner.

In a related development, a new Vivo smartphone with the model number V2531A has been certified by China's 3C authority. The certification listing reveals this device will support 44W wired fast charging—a significant feature for the budget segment. While not officially named, this model is speculated to be part of the same upcoming lineup.

Key Findings & Evidence

Google Play Console Listing

Both the Vivo Y50e 5G and Vivo Y50s 5G have appeared on the Google Play Console, a platform that lists devices compatible with Google's services and Android apps. This is often one of the first official indicators that a smartphone is nearing release.

The listing confirms both devices will support 5G connectivity. According to sources, they share the model number V2443A, which is also used by the existing Vivo Y50 5G. This suggests the new phones could be variations or rebranded versions of the current model, possibly with different memory configurations or regional tweaks.

3C Certification Details

The separate certification for model V2531A on China's 3C website provides concrete technical details. The listing includes two charging adapters with model numbers V4440L0A0-CN and V4440L0E0-CN, which are confirmed to enable up to 44W wired fast charging.

This 44W charging capability aligns with the existing Vivo Y50 5G series, which also features a large 6000mAh battery with 44W fast charging support. The 3C certification is valid until August 29, 2030.

What to Expect: Potential Specifications

While Vivo has not revealed official specs for the Y50e and Y50s, their connection to the existing Y50 series gives strong clues. The new models are expected to share core specifications with the Vivo Y50 5G, which launched in July 2025.

Market Context & What This Means

The original Vivo Y50 5G and Y50m 5G were launched as budget-friendly phones focusing on exceptional battery life and 5G connectivity, with prices starting around $165. The addition of the Y50e and Y50s suggests Vivo is aiming to cover more price points and consumer preferences within the same successful formula.

The appearance on Google Play Console typically precedes a launch by a few weeks or months. With the certifications now in place, an official announcement from Vivo could happen at any time, potentially before the end of 2025 or in early 2026.

The Bottom Line

For consumers, this news means more options in the affordable 5G segment. If the new models follow the Y50 series blueprint, they will offer a massive 6000mAh battery, a 90Hz display, and now-confirmed 44W fast charging—a combination that provides excellent value for money.

We will continue to monitor this story and update you as soon as Vivo makes an official announcement regarding the Vivo Y50e 5G and Y50s 5G.
