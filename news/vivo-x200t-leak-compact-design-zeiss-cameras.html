Vivo X200T Leak Shows Compact Design, Zeiss Cameras Before Launch

December 11, 2025
By: Shedrack

Expected India Launch

Late January 2026

According to multiple reports citing tipster Yogesh Brar, the Vivo X200T is scheduled to hit the Indian market by the end of January 2026. This positions it as a strategic "refresher" for the X200 series.

Note: This is based on leaks. The official date will be confirmed by Vivo.

The Formula: A Blend of Two Phones

Just when you thought the flagship phone season was over, a new leak suggests Vivo has one more surprise up its sleeve for 2025. The upcoming Vivo X200T has surfaced in leaks, described as a strategic hybrid that borrows the best features from two existing models in the lineup.

The device, which recently appeared in a GSMA IMEI database under the model number V2561, is tipped to launch in India soon, potentially as an online-focused offering.

The "X200T" Hybrid Theory

Insiders describe the X200T not as a direct successor, but as a "mix of the X200 and the X200 FE". Here's what that likely means for the final product:

Vivo X200

Donates: Flagship Performance (e.g., Powerful Chipset)

+
Vivo X200 FE

Donates: Compact Design & Form Factor

=
Vivo X200T

The Expected Hybrid

Expected Specifications & Features (Based on Leaks)

While Vivo hasn't confirmed anything, credible leaks from sources like Smartprix and tipster Yogesh Brar paint a clear picture of what to expect. The specs are largely predicted to overlap with the Vivo X200 FE.

Feature Expected Specification (Leaked) Source / Notes
Launch Late January 2026 (India) Tipster Yogesh Brar via Smartprix
Design & Display Compact design, likely similar to the 6.31-inch 1.5K (2640x1216) 120Hz LTPO AMOLED display of the X200 FE. Leaks point to a "compact design". Display specs inferred from X200 FE.
Processor (SoC) MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ chipset (similar to X200 FE). Some earlier speculation suggested the more powerful Dimensity 9400 from the standard X200. Recent leaks strongly favor the Dimensity 9300+.
Camera System Triple rear camera setup with Zeiss optics. Expected to mirror the X200 FE's 50MP main + 8MP ultra-wide + 50MP periscope telephoto setup. Zeiss branding is a staple of the X series. Specific lens details are speculated.
Battery & Charging Large battery (potentially 6,500mAh like the X200 FE) with support for 90W wired fast charging. "Large battery" and 90W charging explicitly mentioned in leaks.
Other Features Expected to retain IP68/IP69 water and dust resistance, high-resolution selfie camera, and potentially an online-focused sales strategy in India. Based on the profile of the X200 FE and market strategy leaks.
Expected Price (India) Could be positioned similarly or slightly lower than the Vivo X200 FE, which launched at ₹54,999 for the 12GB+256GB variant. Pricing is speculative; an "online-focused" model may have aggressive pricing.
Zeiss Optics

The leak confirms a triple rear camera system co-engineered with Zeiss, promising authentic color science and professional-grade features like Multifocal Portrait and Classic Negative film simulations.

90W Charging

A "large" battery coupled with 90W FlashCharge technology is tipped. If it uses the X200 FE's 6,500mAh battery, users can expect all-day endurance and incredibly quick top-ups.

Power & Form

The core appeal: flagship-grade performance (Dimensity 9300+) packed into a compact and ergonomic body, a combination that's becoming rare in the market.

Filling the Gap: Why the X200T Makes Sense

The X200T appears to be a clever play by Vivo. With the flagship X300 series already announced, the X200T serves as a refresher for the previous generation, slotting in between the X200 and X300 to capture buyers looking for near-flagship specs at a potentially better value.

Its rumored compact design is a significant differentiator. In a market dominated by large phones, a powerful device with a smaller footprint like the expected 6.31-inch screen appeals to users who prioritize one-handed use and portability without compromising on camera quality or performance.

Final Thoughts & What to Watch For

Based on the current leaks, the Vivo X200T shapes up to be a compelling "compact flagship" contender for early 2026. It promises a beloved combination: high-end Zeiss cameras, powerful internals, ultra-fast charging, and a manageable size.

What we're still waiting for: Official confirmation from Vivo, the exact launch date, final confirmed specifications (especially the chipset), color options, and the official price tag.

If the leaks hold true, the Vivo X200T could become a go-to recommendation for photography enthusiasts and power users who don't want a gigantic phone. Stay tuned to PhoneSpec Pro for official updates as we approach the late January 2026 launch window.
