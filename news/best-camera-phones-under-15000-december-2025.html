Budget Camera Phone Buying Guide

When choosing a camera phone under ₹15,000, consider these key factors to ensure you get the best photography experience for your budget:

Camera Setup Variety

  • Look for multiple lenses (main, ultrawide, telephoto)
  • Prioritize phones with dedicated sensors over depth cameras
  • Check for advanced features like portrait mode and night mode

Image Processing

  • Natural color reproduction over artificial saturation
  • Good dynamic range in challenging lighting
  • Reliable skin tone rendering

Video Capabilities

  • 4K video recording support
  • Electronic stabilization (EIS)
  • Good audio recording quality

Overall Value

  • Balance between camera quality and other features
  • Software update commitment from manufacturer
  • Battery life for extended photography sessions

Final Verdict

For December 2025, the CMF Phone 2 Pro emerges as the clear winner for the best camera phone under ₹15,000, thanks to its versatile triple-camera setup that includes a rare 50MP telephoto lens. However, your choice should depend on your specific photography needs and budget constraints:
