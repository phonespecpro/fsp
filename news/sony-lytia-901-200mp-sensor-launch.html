Quad-Quad Bayer Coding

The sensor employs a revolutionary QQBC array that clusters 16 adjacent pixels (4Ã—4) with identical color filters. This innovative arrangement enables both high-resolution capture and exceptional low-light performance through intelligent pixel binning .

Built directly into the sensor, the AI processing circuit handles complex remosaicing calculations in real-time, preserving fine details, text, and patterns during zoom operations while eliminating artifacts that plague traditional demosaicing approaches .

Advanced HDR System

Combining DCG-HDR, Fine12bit ADC, and Hybrid Frame-HDR technologies, the sensor achieves an exceptional dynamic range exceeding 100dB - significantly higher than conventional mobile sensors and closer to human visual perception .
