Premium Design Meets Performance

Lava's Agni 4 represents a significant step up in the company's smartphone portfolio, featuring an aluminium alloy metal frame typically found on devices priced above â‚¹50,000 . The phone sports a 6.67-inch flat AMOLED display with 1.5K resolution, 120Hz refresh rate, and an impressive 2400 nits peak brightness, making it one of the brightest displays in its category .

What sets the Agni 4 apart is its clean software experience - it runs Android 15 without any bloatware and comes with a promise of 3 years of Android OS updates and 4 years of security updates . This commitment to long-term software support is unusual in the mid-range segment and adds significant value to the package.

The camera system on the Agni 4 focuses on quality over quantity, featuring a capable 50MP main sensor with optical image stabilization (OIS) for stable shots and videos . Both the front and rear cameras support 4K video recording at 60fps, which is impressive for a device in this price segment. The camera app includes various AI-powered features for scene optimization and skin-tone enhancement .

The 5000mAh battery provides all-day usage, with Lava claiming up to 14 hours of YouTube streaming . The 66W fast charging can replenish 50% of the battery in just 19 minutes, minimizing downtime . A standout feature is the customizable Action Key, which offers over 100 combinations through short, double, and long presses to quickly access frequently used functions .

Market Position and Verdict

The Lava Agni 4 enters a highly competitive mid-range segment but distinguishes itself with its premium build quality, clean software experience, and innovative AI features. The aluminum alloy frame, in particular, gives it a premium feel uncommon in this price range .

With its capable Dimensity 8350 chipset, high-quality AMOLED display, and comprehensive AI suite, the Agni 4 presents a compelling package for users seeking premium features without the flagship price tag. The company's commitment to long-term software updates further enhances its value proposition, making it one of the most interesting mid-range offerings of 2025 .
