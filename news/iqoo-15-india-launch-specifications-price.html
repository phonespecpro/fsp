Flagship Performance Redefined

iQOO's latest flagship makes its Indian debut with specifications that push the boundaries of mobile performance. The device was officially launched today following its initial China release last month, bringing iQOO's signature gaming-focused features to the competitive Indian premium smartphone segment .

With pre-bookings starting November 27 and general sales beginning December 1, the iQOO 15 positions itself as a direct competitor to established flagships from OnePlus, Samsung, and Apple in the Indian market. The company is offering attractive launch discounts that make this premium device more accessible to performance-hungry consumers .

Gaming & Performance Excellence

At the heart of the iQOO 15 lies Qualcomm's flagship Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor built on a 3nm process, delivering exceptional performance and power efficiency. This is complemented by iQOO's proprietary Supercomputing Chip Q3, which enhances graphics processing and computational efficiency for superior gaming experiences .

The device has demonstrated impressive benchmark performance, scoring over 4.18 million points on AnTuTu according to company claims. This represents significant improvements in both CPU and GPU performance, with 23% better GPU performance and 25% improved ray-tracing capabilities compared to previous generation flagships .

To maintain consistent performance during extended gaming sessions, iQOO has incorporated an advanced 8K VC Cooling System with a massive 8,000 sq mm vapor chamber - one of the largest in any smartphone. This ensures that the device maintains optimal temperatures even during the most demanding tasks .

Display Innovation

The iQOO 15 features a groundbreaking Samsung 2K M14 LEAD OLED display that sets new standards for mobile visual experiences. With a 6.85-inch panel offering 2K resolution and 144Hz adaptive refresh rate, it provides incredibly smooth scrolling and responsive touch experiences ideal for gaming and media consumption .

What truly sets this display apart is its exceptional brightness capabilities - reaching up to 6,000 nits of local peak brightness and 2,600 nits in high brightness mode. This ensures perfect visibility even under direct sunlight and enables stunning HDR content playback. The display also features advanced eye protection technologies including TÃœV Rheinland certification .

Professional-Grade Camera System

iQOO has significantly upgraded the camera capabilities in the 15, featuring a versatile triple 50MP camera system that covers all essential photography needs. The setup includes a 50MP Sony IMX921 main sensor with OIS, a 50MP Sony IMX882 periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, and a 50MP ultra-wide camera .

The inclusion of a high-quality periscope telephoto lens is particularly noteworthy, offering 3x optical zoom, 3.7x "lossless zoom," and up to 10x digital zoom capabilities. This makes the iQOO 15 a compelling option for photography enthusiasts who require flexibility in their shooting scenarios without compromising on image quality .

Software Experience & Long-Term Support

The iQOO 15 ships with Android 16-based OriginOS 6, marking a significant shift in iQOO's software strategy for global markets. The company has committed to an impressive software support policy, promising 5 years of Android OS updates and 7 years of security patches - among the longest in the industry .

This extended software support ensures that the device will remain current and secure through 2032, providing exceptional long-term value for consumers investing in this premium flagship. The commitment reflects iQOO's confidence in the hardware's longevity and its dedication to customer satisfaction .

Availability & Market Position

The iQOO 15 will be available through multiple channels including Amazon.in, iQOO e-store, and vivo exclusive stores. Priority Pass users gain early access starting November 27 at noon, with general sales beginning December 1 .
