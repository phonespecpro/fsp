Why AirPods 4 With ANC Are Worth It

Even at their regular $179 price point, AirPods 4 with Active Noise Cancellation represent a significant advancement in Apple's audio technology. The combination of premium features at this discounted $99 price makes them arguably the best value in wireless earbuds currently available.

Active Noise Cancellation

Powered by the H2 chip and upgraded microphones, ANC reduces environmental noise like airplane engines and city traffic before it reaches your ears.

H2 Chip Intelligence

The powerful H2 chip enables Adaptive Audio, Transparency mode, and Conversation Awareness that automatically adjusts based on your environment.

Studio-Quality Recording

With iOS 26 updates, AirPods 4 gain studio-quality audio recording capabilities, making them perfect for content creators and podcasters.

Camera Remote

New camera remote functionality allows you to take photos or start/stop video recordings by pressing and holding the AirPods stem.

Dust & Water Resistant

IP54 rating protects against dust, sweat, and water, making them suitable for workouts and outdoor use.

Smaller Case Design

The charging case is more than 10% smaller than previous generations while maintaining 30 hours of total battery life.

Why This Deal is Exceptional

Current-generation Apple technology rarely sees discounts approaching 50%, making this AirPods 4 ANC deal particularly noteworthy. As 9to5Toys noted, "landing current-generation Apple anything this close to 50% off in brand new condition from a trusted retailer is very rare".

Should You Buy Now or Wait?

Given the historical pricing patterns and the fact that this represents the lowest price ever recorded for AirPods 4 with ANC, most experts recommend purchasing immediately if you find stock at $99. The Black Friday 2025 AirPods 4 with ANC deal represents one of the most significant Apple product discounts of the year.
