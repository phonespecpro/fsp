Redmi Note 15 5G 108 Master Pixel Special Edition India Launch Date Announced; Amazon Listing Confirms Availability

December 9, 2025
By: Shedrack

Official Launch Date and Amazon Partnership Confirmed

Xiaomi's sub-brand, Redmi, has officially confirmed that the Redmi Note 15 5G 108 Master Pixel Edition is launching in India soon. The brand made the announcement via its social media channels and a dedicated Amazon microsite, which reveals the smartphone will debut on January 6, 2026 . The Amazon listing directly confirms that the device will be available for purchase on the e-commerce platform, continuing Redmi's successful partnership with Amazon for its major launches .

This "Master Pixel" edition appears to be a special variant focused on camera prowess and design refinement, launching alongside the anticipated Redmi Note 15 series. Tipsters suggest the launch will be staggered, with the standard Note 15 models arriving first on January 6th, followed by the Pro and Pro+ variants at a later date .

Design Teaser and Expected Specifications

Premium Design Focus

Redmi has released an official teaser image that provides a clear look at the phone's side profile . The image reveals a slim and sleek form factor with a curved display. Notably, the left side of the frame appears completely clean, suggesting the power and volume buttons will be located on the right side. The frame itself has a premium look, hinting at a possible metal build, which would be a significant upgrade in the Note series' design language .

Expected Key Specifications (Based on Leaks)

While Redmi has not officially detailed the full spec sheet, multiple tipsters and reports have provided a consistent picture of what to expect from the 108 Master Pixel Edition and the standard Note 15 5G . It's important to note that some specifications, like the exact display size, have minor conflicts between different leaks . The table below consolidates the most frequently cited expectations.

Series Overview and What Comes Next

The Broader Note 15 Series

The 108 Master Pixel Edition is part of the larger Redmi Note 15 series refresh. Reports indicate that the standard Redmi Note 15 is expected to share many of the core specs listed above, including the 120Hz AMOLED display, Snapdragon 6 Gen 3 chip, 108MP camera, and large battery .

Redmi Note 15 Pro & Pro+ (Expected)

For users seeking even higher performance, the Redmi Note 15 Pro and Pro+ models are tipped to launch separately, possibly in February 2026 . These models are expected to feature significant upgrades :

  • Display: Larger 6.83-inch CrystalRes AMOLED panels with 120Hz refresh rate and Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection.
  • Processor: Pro+ with Snapdragon 7s Gen 4; Pro with MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra.
  • Camera: A massive 200MP primary camera with Optical Image Stabilization (OIS).
  • Durability: Higher IP68 dust and water resistance rating.
  • Battery: Around 6,500mAh capacity, with the Pro+ possibly supporting 100W fast charging.

The Pro models are expected to be priced between ₹27,000 and ₹35,000 in India .

Positioning and Final Thoughts

The announcement of the Redmi Note 15 5G 108 Master Pixel Edition sets the stage for Redmi's 2026 offensive in the competitive Indian mid-range segment. By confirming the launch date and Amazon availability early, Redmi is building significant anticipation .

The focus on a "Master Pixel" camera, a refined curved design, and core specs like a 120Hz AMOLED display and a large battery suggests Redmi is aiming to deliver a premium feel at an accessible price point, likely around the ₹20,000 mark . The device will compete directly with other brands' offerings that emphasize camera capabilities and design.

With the official launch event set for January 6, 2026, all eyes will now be on Redmi to confirm the final specifications, features, and pricing. Based on the company's track record and the current leaks, the Redmi Note 15 series, led by this special edition, is shaping up to be a strong contender for the title of the best value-for-money smartphone in the first quarter of 2026.
