OnePlus has officially confirmed that the highly anticipated Ace 6T will debut in China on December 3, 2025 . The company made the announcement through its China President, Li Jie Louis, on Weibo, ending weeks of speculation and teasers about the device's launch timeline .

üéØ Record-Breaking Performance

The OnePlus Ace 6T has achieved an impressive 3.56 million points on AnTuTu benchmark, showcasing its raw processing power and positioning it as one of the most powerful smartphones in its category . This exceptional performance is driven by the combination of the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset and OnePlus's "Wind Chaser Gaming Kernel" optimization .

üñ•Ô∏è Premium Display & Build Quality

The Ace 6T features a 6.7-inch LTPO AMOLED display with 1.5K resolution (1272 x 2800 pixels) and support for 1 billion colors . The 165Hz refresh rate ensures buttery-smooth scrolling and gaming experiences, while HDR10+, Dolby Vision, and HDR Vivid support deliver exceptional visual quality for multimedia consumption.

In terms of build quality, the device boasts a metal frame with micro-arc oxidation coating, providing a premium ceramic-like feel . The back panel varies by color option - the black and green models feature silky textured glass, while the purple variant uses fiberglass material . The phone also includes comprehensive durability with IP68, IP69, and IP69K ratings for dust and water resistance .

üì∏ Camera Capabilities

The imaging system on the Ace 6T consists of a dual rear camera setup featuring a 50MP main sensor with optical image stabilization (OIS) and an 8MP ultra-wide-angle lens with 112-degree field of view . The main camera utilizes a large sensor that captures excellent detail in various lighting conditions, supported by AI enhancements for improved image processing.

For selfies and video calls, the device houses a 32MP front-facing camera capable of recording 4K video . The camera system benefits from OnePlus's computational photography algorithms, ensuring sharp, vibrant images across different scenarios including low-light environments.

üîÑ Rebranded for International Markets

For markets outside China, the Ace 6T will be released as the OnePlus 15R on December 17, 2025 . This follows OnePlus's established pattern of rebranding Ace series devices for global markets. The global version is expected to retain most of the core specifications, though there may be some regional adjustments based on market requirements.

The global launch event for the OnePlus 15R is scheduled for December 17, where the company will reveal international pricing and availability details . While the core specifications are expected to remain consistent, the global version may not include the purple color option initially, with black and green being the primary color choices for international markets .

This pre-order system is typical for Chinese smartphone launches and allows interested customers to secure early access.
