OxygenOS 16 Rolls Out Globally for OnePlus 12R With New AI Features and Performance Upgrades

December 01, 2025
By: Shedrack

Global Rollout Begins for OnePlus 12R

OnePlus has officially commenced the global rollout of OxygenOS 16 based on Android 16 for the OnePlus 12R, marking a significant milestone in the company's software update schedule . The update, which began in India under build number CPH2585_16.0.1.300(EX01), is now expanding to global markets in batches, making the OnePlus 12R one of the first devices to receive the latest software experience .

This major update introduces a comprehensive redesign with the new "Liquid Glass" aesthetic, extensive AI-powered features under the "Plus Mind" suite, and significant performance improvements that transform the user experience . The rollout follows OnePlus's announced schedule that placed the 12R in the initial batch of smartphones set to receive OxygenOS 16 in November 2025, with older models expected to follow in December and through early 2026 .

Update Details and Availability

Rollout Information

The OxygenOS 16 update for OnePlus 12R is being distributed incrementally, starting with users in India before expanding to other global markets . The update carries software version CPH2585_16.0.1.300(EX01) and represents a substantial download, with the full package weighing approximately 7.2GB .

Users can check for the update manually by navigating to Settings → About device → OxygenOS version and tapping "Check for updates" . As with all OnePlus updates, the rollout is conducted in phases to ensure stability, so it may take a few days to reach all devices even within the same region .

Liquid Glass Design and Visual Enhancements

OxygenOS 16 introduces OnePlus's new "Liquid Glass" design language that completely transforms the visual experience with cleaner icons, smoother transitions, and a more unified aesthetic across system apps and menus . The design philosophy focuses on creating a more fluid and cohesive visual language that enhances both aesthetics and usability.

Enhanced Animations

System animations have been completely overhauled with smoother app opening/closing transitions, fluid drag-and-drop animations for home screen elements, and seamless status bar transitions . The update also introduces interactive swipe-back animations that preview the previous page while swiping .

Improved Haptics

The haptic feedback system has been refined to feel more precise and satisfying, complementing the visual changes with enhanced tactile responses that make navigation feel more responsive and engaging .

Customization Options

New flux themes, motion wallpapers, adjustable icon sizes, improved folder layouts, and a full-screen Always On Display provide users with more personalization options than ever before . The Luminous Rendering Engine creates more vivid, immersive multi-sensory experiences .

Plus Mind AI Features Suite

OxygenOS 16 introduces a comprehensive suite of AI-powered features under the "Plus Mind" umbrella, designed to boost productivity and streamline common tasks . These intelligent features learn from user behavior and adapt to individual workflows, making the smartphone experience more personalized and efficient.

Key AI Features Include:

Mind Space

An organizational hub that allows instant saving of content through "Plus to Mind Space," suggests collections, and includes a Mind Assistant for searching saved content . Coming soon, Mind Space will integrate with Google's Gemini for truly personalized AI assistance .

AI-Powered Photos

Enhanced photo editing capabilities including automatic straightening, motion photo collages, video editing features, and AI Portrait Glow that reconstructs balanced illumination in challenging lighting conditions .

AI Writer & Notes

Advanced writing assistance with block editing mode, text formatting tools, link sharing, and improved rewriting tools with multiple tone options . The AI Writer can generate content ranging from work summaries to social media posts .

AI Recorder & VoiceScribe

Smart recording with noise reduction, automatic context detection for meetings or lectures, and AI-powered call summaries that can be exported in various formats .

Performance and Multitasking Enhancements

Under the hood, OxygenOS 16 brings significant performance improvements through the Trinity Engine, which optimizes workload and resources in real-time to maintain smooth performance during gaming, video playback, and extended camera use . The update enhances memory management, optimizes background processes, and improves stability during demanding workloads .

Multitasking Upgrades

The Smart Sidebar has been revamped with quicker access to apps and tools, while floating windows and split-screen modes have been made more stable and responsive . App resizing and context switching have been refined for smoother workflow transitions, making it easier to jump between messaging apps and media or productivity tasks .

System Optimization

Applications now launch faster directly from the home screen, photo albums load quicker with less power consumption, and cached video previews load faster in supported third-party apps . The power saving mode now features tiered performance management that adapts to battery level, ensuring longer endurance with smoother performance .

Privacy and Security Enhancements

OxygenOS 16 incorporates Android 16's updated privacy framework with more granular permission settings and clearer indicators for location and app access . The introduction of Private Computing Cloud processes user data locally without sharing it with third parties, enhancing privacy while maintaining functionality .

The security model includes hardware-level encryption with both CPU and GPU protection through a Trusted Execution Environment that safeguards data on both cloud and devices . OnePlus emphasizes that even they cannot access user data processed through these secure environments .

Future Rollout Schedule and Eligible Devices

November 2025

OnePlus 12R leads the initial rollout alongside other flagship devices including OnePlus 13 series, OnePlus Open, and OnePlus Pad 2 . The 12R being among the first devices reflects OnePlus's commitment to providing timely updates to recent models.

December 2025

Older models including OnePlus 11R 5G, OnePlus 11 CE 5G, OnePlus Nord CE 5G, and OnePlus Nord CE 5G Lite are scheduled to receive OxygenOS 16 . This phased approach ensures stability across different device configurations.

First Quarter 2026

Legacy devices including OnePlus 10 Pro 5G, OnePlus 10 CE 5G, and OnePlus Pad Lite are expected to receive the update, completing the initial rollout cycle . OnePlus may add more devices to the schedule over time.

A Transformative Update

The OxygenOS 16 update represents one of the most significant software upgrades in recent OnePlus history, bringing substantial improvements across design, AI capabilities, performance, and privacy . For OnePlus 12R users, the update transforms the device with a fresh visual identity, intelligent features that adapt to user behavior, and enhanced performance that maintains smooth operation even during demanding tasks.

As the rollout continues globally, OnePlus 12R owners can look forward to a software experience that feels both new and familiar, building upon the foundation of previous OxygenOS versions while introducing meaningful innovations that enhance daily smartphone use. The successful deployment of OxygenOS 16 to the 12R sets the stage for broader availability across OnePlus's device ecosystem throughout late 2025 and early 2026.
