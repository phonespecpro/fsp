First Vivo X Fold6 Specs Leak Along With Expected Release Window

November 20, 2025
By: Shedrack

ðŸš€ Next-Gen Foldable Powerhouse

Major specifications for Vivo's upcoming flagship foldable, the X Fold6, have surfaced online, revealing a significant camera upgrade and powerful new chipset. According to leaks from tipster Smart Pikachu, the device will feature a 200MP camera sensor and Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor, positioning it as a serious competitor in the premium foldable market .

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 represents a notable performance improvement over the X Fold5's Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, with Geekbench 6 scores showing 2,981 single-core and 10,136 multi-core results .

Camera System

Main Sensor 200MP UPGRADE
Camera Branding Zeiss Optics (Expected)
Previous Gen 50MP triple camera

The massive jump to 200MP represents a significant upgrade from the X Fold5's 50MP triple camera setup, though it's unclear whether this will be the main or telephoto sensor .

Launch Timeline

According to multiple sources, the Vivo X Fold6 is expected to debut in Q2 2026, which places the launch window between April and June next year . This follows Vivo's established pattern with the X Fold5, which was released in June 2025 .

Performance and Chipset Details

The Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset confirmed for the X Fold6 is described as a "sub-flagship" processor that will make its debut later this month with the OnePlus Ace 6T . This continues Vivo's pattern of equipping its foldables with high-end, but not the absolute top-tier, Qualcomm chipsets - the X Fold5 used the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 instead of the more powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite .

Despite this positioning, the performance improvements appear substantial. Recent Geekbench 6 CPU tests revealed scores of 2,981 in single-core and 10,136 in multi-core, suggesting meaningful gains over the previous generation . The Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 is built on TSMC's 3nm N3P process, with early AnTuTu scores reportedly above 1.5 million in initial tests .

Camera System Analysis

The 200MP camera sensor represents one of the most significant upgrades in the X Fold6. While the exact implementation remains unclear - sources are uncertain whether it will serve as the main camera or telephoto lens - this resolution dramatically exceeds the 50MP sensors used in competing foldables from Honor and Oppo .

The camera system is expected to feature Zeiss-branded optics, continuing Vivo's partnership with the legendary German optics company seen on previous X-series flagships . This combination of high-resolution sensors and professional-grade optics could give Vivo a distinct advantage in the competitive foldable camera market.

Design and Features

Fingerprint Sensor Side-mounted scanner embedded in power button
Previous Generation Similar side-mounted approach on X Fold5
Market Competition Samsung Galaxy Z Fold, Xiaomi Mix Fold, Honor Magic V

The leak confirms the X Fold6 will continue using a side-mounted fingerprint scanner rather than transitioning to an under-display solution . This approach may be preferred due to durability considerations and technical challenges associated with implementing under-display sensors in foldable displays .

This positions Vivo to compete more aggressively against established foldable competitors like Samsung's Galaxy Z Fold series, Xiaomi's Mix Fold lineup, and Honor's Magic V foldables . The combination of flagship-level camera capabilities and cutting-edge Qualcomm silicon creates a compelling package in the premium foldable segment.

Market Context and Expectations

If these leaked specifications materialize, the Vivo X Fold6 will enter the market with a potentially significant camera advantage over its competitors . The jump to a 200MP sensor could become the defining feature of Vivo's next-generation foldable, particularly given the company's consistent focus on imaging excellence across its product lineup.

The Q2 2026 launch timeframe suggests Vivo is maintaining a consistent annual update cycle for its flagship foldable series. With the X Fold5 having been well-received for its slim design (4.3mm unfolded, 217g weight) and substantial 6,000mAh battery, expectations are high for the X Fold6 to build upon these strengths while introducing meaningful upgrades in performance and camera capabilities .
