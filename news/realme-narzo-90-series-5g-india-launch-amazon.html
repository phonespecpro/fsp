Realme Narzo 90 Series 5G India Launch Announced, Will Be Available on Amazon

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack
Teaser image via Amazon India

Series Launch Confirmed for India

Realme, in partnership with Amazon India, has officially begun teasing the launch of its next-generation Narzo 90 Series 5G smartphones. The teaser campaign confirms that the upcoming phones will be "Amazon Specials," meaning they will be available for purchase exclusively through the Amazon platform in India.

The launch comes months after the company introduced the Narzo 80 Series 5G in the country and signals a significant update to its popular budget-friendly 5G lineup.

Two Distinct Models Teased

A comic-style teaser on a dedicated Amazon microsite clearly reveals that the series will consist of two separate smartphone models with distinct rear camera designs.

Model 1 (Likely Narzo 90 Pro 5G) Features a camera layout reminiscent of the iPhone 16 Pro Max, similar to its predecessor, the Narzo 80 Pro 5G. This model is expected to be the higher-end variant of the series.
Model 2 (Likely Narzo 90x 5G) Sports a rectangular-shaped camera module with vertically-aligned lenses, a design language carried over from the Narzo 80x 5G.

Both phones are shown with flat frames and rounded corners, continuing Realme's recent design trends for a modern and uniform aesthetic.

Teased Upgrades & Features

While Realme has not revealed the full specifications, the Amazon microsite uses specific branding to hint at the key upgrades coming with the Narzo 90 Series.

Supercharged & Power Maxed Strongly suggests that the new models will pack larger batteries and support for faster charging technology compared to their predecessors.
Snap Sharp Points to significant improvements in the camera department, likely through upgraded sensors for sharper and better-quality photos.
Glow Maxed Indicates that the displays on both phones will have higher peak brightness levels, improving outdoor visibility.

What Happens Next? Mark Your Calendar

The current teaser campaign ends with a clear message for tech enthusiasts: "Gear Up for December 9. The Plot Gets Thicker."

This confirms that Realme will reveal more detailed information about the Narzo 90 Series 5G on December 9, 2025. This next reveal is expected to include more concrete specifications, features, and potentially the official launch date for the smartphones in India.

Given the Amazon exclusive tag, consumers can expect the phones to be available for pre-orders or sale directly on Amazon India following the official announcement.
