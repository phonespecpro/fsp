By: Shedrack

A New Era for R-Series Selfies

In a series of announcements building hype for its December 17 launch, OnePlus has confirmed that the OnePlus 15R will receive the most significant front camera upgrade in the history of its R-Series lineup. The company states the phone will feature the "most advanced selfie camera ever fitted to an R Series device," moving beyond mere megapixel counts to bring meaningful feature improvements.

The key upgrade is a 32-megapixel front-facing sensor equipped with autofocus (AF). This is a substantial leap from the 16MP fixed-focus camera used in the preceding OnePlus 13R. The inclusion of autofocus is particularly notable, as it has often been reserved for flagship devices. This technology allows the camera to automatically adjust focus for sharper selfies and clearer video calls, especially when the subject is at varying distances from the phone.

Beyond the Selfie: Flagship Tech Trickles Down

The camera enhancements are part of a broader pattern where OnePlus is bringing technology from its flagship number series to the more accessible R line. OnePlus confirmed that the 15R will utilize the same DetailMax Engine found in the flagship OnePlus 15. This software package includes advanced computational photography features like Ultra Clear Mode, Clear Burst for action shots, and the Clear Night Engine for low-light photography.

Powering these features is the new Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 processor. OnePlus highlights that the 15R will be one of the first phones globally to launch with this chipset. The phone is also confirmed to feature a high-refresh-rate 1.5K resolution AMOLED display with a 165Hz refresh rate, matching the smoothness of the flagship model.

Massive Battery Confirmed

Adding to the compelling specs sheet, OnePlus has confirmed the 15R will pack a 7,400mAh batteryâ€”reportedly the largest cell used in a OnePlus device in markets like India to date. The company also claims this battery will retain up to 80% of its health after four years of use.

Launch Details and What to Expect

The official global unveiling of the OnePlus 15R is set for December 17, 2025. The launch event will be held in Bengaluru, India. It will be accompanied by other ecosystem products, including the OnePlus Pad Go 2 tablet and a OnePlus Watch Lite.

While the official global design and full specifications will be revealed at the event, the recently launched OnePlus Ace 6T in China provides a strong indication of what to expect. This model, expected to be rebranded as the 15R internationally, features the same core specs like the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 and a 165Hz display.

Early Reactions

News of the upgrade has generated buzz among tech enthusiasts. Comments on early reports welcome the addition of autofocus and 4K video as overdue improvements for content creators and power users, suggesting OnePlus is seriously elevating the capabilities of its mid-range lineup. All eyes are now on the pricing, which will be revealed at the December 17 launch event.
