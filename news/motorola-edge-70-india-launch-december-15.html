Motorola Edge 70 India Launch Set for December 15

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack
Pantone Bronze Green

Official Launch Announcement

Motorola has officially confirmed that the much-anticipated Edge 70 will launch in India on December 15, 2025. The company began teasing the device last week and has now solidified the date, with sales commencing on the same day through Flipkart, the Motorola online store, and other major retailers.

This launch marks the Indian debut of Motorola's thinnest smartphone yet, which aims to compete in the premium mid-range segment with its blend of sleek design and capable hardware.

Key Specifications & Features

Design & Display 5.99mm ultra-thin profile, 159g weight, 6.7-inch 1.5K pOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate and 4500 nits peak brightness
Performance Qualcomm Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 processor, up to 12GB RAM with RAM Boost, up to 512GB storage
Camera System Triple 50MP cameras: Main (with OIS), Ultrawide (120°), and Front. Features moto AI for photo enhancement
Battery & Charging 4800mAh silicon-carbon battery, 68W TurboPower wired charging, 15W wireless charging
Durability & Software IP68 & IP69 water/dust resistance, MIL-STD-810H rating, Android 16 with Hello UI, 3 OS updates

Availability & Expected Pricing

The Motorola Edge 70 will be available for purchase starting December 15 through the following channels:

  • Flipkart (Online)
  • Motorola India Online Store (motorola.in)
  • Authorized Offline Retailers across the country

While Motorola has not announced the official Indian price, it is expected to be around ₹30,000-₹35,000 (approximately $390). This positions it against other premium mid-range smartphones in the Indian market.

The Thinnest in Its Category

Motorola is heavily marketing the Edge 70's design, highlighting its 5.99mm thickness which makes it one of the slimmest smartphones available. The company has even compared it to a standard pencil (7mm) in its promotional material to emphasize this feat.

Despite the slender profile, the phone doesn't compromise on build quality. It features an aircraft-grade aluminum frame with Gorilla Glass 7i protection and meets military-grade durability standards (MIL-STD-810H).

Camera & AI Capabilities

The Edge 70 boasts a comprehensive camera system with three 50MP sensors – one on the front and two on the back. The rear setup includes a main sensor with Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) and an ultrawide lens with a 120-degree field of view that also doubles as a macro camera.

Motorola's moto AI enhances the photography experience with features like a Photo Enhancement Engine, Signature Style presets, and Action Shot for crisp movement. The AI also extends to productivity with features like instant transcription, summarization, and on-screen context awareness.

Market Context & Competition

The Edge 70 enters an Indian market that has seen several slim smartphone launches in 2025, including offerings from Samsung and Apple, though with mixed commercial success. Motorola aims to differentiate itself by combining this slim factor with robust specifications and Motorola's traditional durability.

The device will likely compete with other phones in the ₹30,000-₹40,000 range, appealing to users who prioritize design, display quality, and camera performance alongside day-to-day reliability.
