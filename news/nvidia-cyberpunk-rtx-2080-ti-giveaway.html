NVIDIA Marks Cyberpunk 2077's 5th Anniversary With Ultra-Rare RTX 2080 Ti Giveaway Signed by CEO Collector's Dream

December 12, 2025
By: Shedrack
The Prize
Custom RTX 2080 Ti GPU with Cyberpunk 2077 artwork
Signature
Personally signed by NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang
Eligibility
Open to participants worldwide*
Deadline
December 31, 2025 at 11:59 PM PT

A Legendary Card Returns for a Milestone Celebration

NVIDIA is pulling out all the stops to celebrate the 5th anniversary of CD Projekt Red's groundbreaking RPG, Cyberpunk 2077. In a move that has electrified the gaming community, the company is giving away an incredibly rare piece of gaming history: a custom-designed GeForce RTX 2080 Ti graphics card, the very GPU that was synonymous with the game's ambitious visual promises at launch, now personally hand-signed by none other than NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang.

This isn't just any giveaway. It's a tribute to a rollercoaster five-year journey for the game and its players. The RTX 2080 Ti was the flagship card that CD Projekt Red showcased during the game's tumultuous pre-release period, making it a symbolic centerpiece for this anniversary celebration. The unique card features a custom shroud design inspired by the neon-drenched, corporate-dominated world of Night City.

The Ultimate Collector's Prize: More Than Just a GPU

CEO SIGNED - AUTHENTIC COLLECTOR'S ITEM

The centerpiece of this giveaway is a fully functional GeForce RTX 2080 Ti that has been transformed into a one-of-a-kind work of art for this occasion. It is not just a test card or a shell; it is a genuine, working graphics card from the Turing generation.

Key Features of the Prize:

  • Custom Artwork: The card's shroud has been specially painted with iconic Cyberpunk 2077 themes—think neon yellows, corporate Arasaka reds, and gritty Night City textures.
  • The Signature: The most coveted feature: the clear signature of Jensen Huang, NVIDIA's iconic CEO, applied directly to the card, making it a unique piece of tech memorabilia.
  • Historical Significance: As the top-tier consumer card when Cyberpunk 2077 was announced and initially demoed, the RTX 2080 Ti holds a special place in the game's legacy, representing the high-end visual target it aimed for.
  • Complete Package: The winner will receive the card in special commemorative packaging befitting a collector's item.

While no longer the cutting-edge, the RTX 2080 Ti remains a capable 4K gaming card, especially for a title like Cyberpunk 2077 with DLSS enabled. However, its true value lies in its unmatched uniqueness and symbolic history.

How to Enter the Giveaway

NVIDIA has made entering the contest straightforward, focusing on community engagement around the anniversary. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. 1
    Find the Official Post: Locate the giveaway announcement on NVIDIA's official social media channels. The primary post is on their X (formerly Twitter) account (@NVIDIA).
  2. 2
    Like & Retweet/Repost: Engage with the official contest post by liking it and sharing it (retweeting on X, reposting on threads, etc.) to your own followers.
  3. 3
    Tag and Share Memories: Tag a friend in the comments and share your own memory of playing Cyberpunk 2077. This could be your first time entering Night City, a favorite mission, or how the game's visuals blew you away.
  4. 4
    Follow the Accounts: Ensure you are following both @NVIDIA and @CyberpunkGame on the platform you enter from.

Rules, Eligibility & The Fine Print

Important: This summary is for informational purposes. Always refer to the official rules on NVIDIA's contest page for complete, legally binding details.

Key Eligibility & Rule Details:

  • Eligibility: The giveaway is open to participants worldwide, with some common-sense exceptions (e.g., countries under embargo, where prohibited by law).
  • Age Requirement: Participants must be of the age of majority in their country/region (typically 18 or older).
  • One Entry Per Person: Multiple entries from the same individual will likely be disqualified.
  • No Purchase Necessary: As with most legitimate giveaways, you do not need to buy anything to enter or win.
  • Winner Selection & Notification: The winner will be selected at random from all eligible entries after the contest closes. NVIDIA or its agents will contact the winner via the social media platform used to enter. The winner will have a limited time to respond and claim the prize, failing which a new winner may be drawn.
  • Prize is Non-Transferable: The prize cannot be exchanged for cash or an alternative item.

The contest is being run directly by NVIDIA, not by third-party giveaway sites, which adds to its legitimacy.

Why This Giveaway is a Big Deal for Gamers

Beyond the chance to win an incredible item, this giveaway is significant for several reasons:

  • Full-Circle Moment: It acknowledges Cyberpunk 2077's remarkable redemption arc—from a controversial launch to a celebrated title praised in its 2.0 and Phantom Liberty incarnations. Honoring it with its "launch partner" GPU is a classy nod from NVIDIA.
  • CEO Involvement: Jensen Huang's personal signature is exceptionally rare on consumer hardware. It transforms the card from a cool prize into a genuine piece of tech history, appealing to collectors far beyond just gamers.
  • Community Celebration: By asking for shared memories, NVIDIA is fostering positive community engagement, letting fans be part of the anniversary story.
  • Symbolic Gesture: In an era of rapid GPU generational turnover, celebrating a 5-year-old card is a refreshing reminder of lasting technological impact and fan loyalty.

Ready to Enter Night City's Ultimate Raffle?

If you're a fan of Cyberpunk 2077, PC gaming history, or just incredible tech collectibles, this is a giveaway you won't want to miss. The combination of a legendary game, a historic GPU, and the signature of one of tech's most recognizable CEOs creates a prize that is truly one-of-a-kind.

Your Mission, Should You Choose to Accept: Head over to NVIDIA's official X (Twitter) page, find the contest post, and follow the entry steps before time runs out on December 31, 2025. Good luck, choomba—you're gonna need it in this crowded field.
