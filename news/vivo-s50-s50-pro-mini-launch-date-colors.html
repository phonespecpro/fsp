Vivo S50 and S50 Pro Mini Launch Date Confirmed; Color Options Revealed

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack
Confession White

Official Launch Announcement

Vivo has officially confirmed that its new S50 series smartphones will launch in China on December 15, 2025. The launch event is scheduled for 7:00 PM local time (4:30 PM IST) and will introduce two models: the standard Vivo S50 and the more compact flagship, the Vivo S50 Pro Mini.

Following weeks of teasers on Chinese social media platform Weibo, the company has also opened pre-reservations for the devices through its official online store in China, generating significant anticipation for this camera-focused series.

Confirmed Color Options

Ahead of the launch, Vivo has officially revealed the color variants for both new phones through its online store. The choices emphasize a premium and serene aesthetic.

Vivo S50 Colors Confession White, Inspirational Purple, Space Black, and Serene Blue.
Vivo S50 Pro Mini Colors Confession White, Inspirational Purple, and Space Black.

Market Context & Global Availability

The December 15 launch is confirmed for the Chinese market. Vivo has a history of rebranding its 'S' series for international markets; industry reports suggest the S50 series could launch globally later under different names, such as the Vivo V70 series in India.

The S50 Pro Mini, in particular, is generating interest as a "compact flagship," a niche segment that combines high-end specifications with a more manageable form factor. Its combination of the powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 and a large 6,500mAh battery in a compact body is a key selling point.

What to Expect at the Launch

The official launch event on December 15 will provide final confirmation on:

  • Official pricing for all RAM and storage variants in China.
  • Complete camera specifications and software features.
  • Detailed design walkthrough and hands-on impressions.
  • Potential announcement regarding rollout to other regions.

With pre-orders already live in China, Vivo is poised for a significant year-end release aimed at photography enthusiasts and users seeking powerful performance in both standard and compact form factors.
