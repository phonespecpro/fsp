Smart Shopping Tips for Streaming Deals

Use Alternate Emails

Many deals are for new subscribers only. Use a different email address to qualify for returning subscriber discounts.

Set Reminders

Mark your calendar to cancel before auto-renewal at full price, or check if you can extend the discount.

Consider Bundles

Bundle deals often provide better value than individual subscriptions, especially for related services.

Check Add-On Options

Many services offer deeper discounts as add-ons through Prime Video Channels or other platforms.

Don't Miss These Limited-Time Offers

With most Black Friday streaming deals ending on December 1st, now is the time to act if you want to lock in significant savings on your favorite streaming services. The HBO Max deal at $2.99/month for a full year is particularly noteworthy, offering unprecedented savings on one of the most comprehensive streaming libraries available.

Remember that these discounted rates are typically reserved for new and returning subscribers, so if you're currently paying full price for any of these services, it might be worth considering cancellation and resubscription to take advantage of the savings. As streaming prices continue to rise across the industry, these Black Friday 2025 deals represent some of the best value we've seen in recent years.
