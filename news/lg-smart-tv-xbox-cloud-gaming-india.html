LG Gaming Portal: A Unified Hub for Cloud Gaming

South Korean electronics giant LG has announced the launch of its innovative Gaming Portal in the Indian market, featuring native integration with Xbox Cloud Gaming . This strategic move transforms compatible LG Smart TVs into powerful gaming platforms without the need for additional hardware or consoles.

The LG Gaming Portal serves as a unified hub that brings together cloud gaming services, allowing users to instantly access thousands of games without downloads or complex setup processes . According to Brian Jung, Director at LG Media Entertainment Solution Company, "We're thrilled to bring LG Gaming Portal with Xbox to LG Smart TVs in India. Compatibility and Rollout Information

Rollout Pattern Phased software update across major Indian cities
Access Method Via LG home screen after software update

The Xbox Cloud Gaming feature is currently rolling out in phases to compatible LG Smart TV models through software updates . Users with 2021-2025 LG webOS TVs should watch for the update notification, after which they'll find the Gaming Portal readily accessible from their LG home screen.

The rollout is beginning in major Indian metropolitan areas and will gradually expand to cover more regions. LG has indicated that the update availability may vary depending on the specific TV model and geographical location within India .

How to Get Started with Xbox Cloud Gaming

Setting up Xbox Cloud Gaming on compatible LG Smart TVs is a straightforward process that requires just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Check Compatibility

Ensure your LG Smart TV is from 2021-2025 and has received the latest software update containing the Gaming Portal .

Step 2: Access Gaming Portal

Navigate to the LG home screen and locate the new Gaming Portal interface that appears after the update .

Step 3: Subscribe to Game Pass

Choose an Xbox Game Pass plan that suits your gaming needs and budget from the three available tiers in India .

Step 4: Connect Controller

Pair a compatible Bluetooth controller with your LG Smart TV for the optimal gaming experience .

Step 5: Start Playing

Select your desired game from the extensive library and begin playing instantly through cloud streaming .
