OPPO Find X9 Pro Debuts with Hasselblad Master Camera and Breakthrough Zoom Technology

November 6, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

BARCELONA, NOVEMBER 5, 2025 - OPPO has officially launched its groundbreaking Find X9 Pro globally, setting a new standard for flagship smartphones with an unprecedented 200MP Hasselblad Telephoto camera system and revolutionary zoom capabilities that challenge professional photography equipment.

The device, which began global availability in early November, represents what OPPO calls "a giant leap forward in mobile imaging," combining industry-leading camera innovation with a massive 7500mAh battery - the largest ever in an OPPO flagship - and top-tier performance powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 9500 chipset.

"Find X9 Series represents a giant leap forward in mobile imaging, driven by industry-leading innovations like the 200MP Hasselblad Telephoto," said Pete Lau, SVP and Chief Product Officer at OPPO. "As OPPO's new-generation flagship, Find X9 Series delivers a comprehensive, top-to-bottom upgrade, setting a new global standard for smartphone excellence".

Hasselblad Master Camera System: Redefining Mobile Photography

The Find X9 Pro's camera system represents one of the most significant advancements in smartphone photography, centered around the groundbreaking 200MP Hasselblad Telephoto camera. This 3x periscope telephoto features a massive 1/1.56-inch sensor - one of the largest ever in a telephoto lens - paired with an ultra-fast f/2.1 aperture for superior light gathering.

What sets this system apart is OPPO's Active Optical Alignment process, which precisely matches the lens with the sensor to boost resolution by an additional 15%. The result is exceptional clarity even in challenging lighting conditions, with the ability to capture high-quality lossless zoom up to 13.2x and enhanced clarity up to 120x using OPPO's Super Zoom algorithm.

The camera system also includes a 50MP main camera with a customized 1/1.28-inch Sony LYT 828 sensor featuring Real-Time Triple Exposure technology, and a 50MP ultra-wide camera that doubles as a macro lens. Completing the setup is an industry-first True Color Camera with a dedicated spectral sensor that precisely measures ambient light for more accurate color reproduction.

The Ultimate Concert and Event Companion

OPPO has specifically engineered the Find X9 Pro to be "the perfect companion for concerts and live events," providing unrivaled zoom capabilities in both photo and video shooting. The device's Stage Mode enhances concert photos with improved contrast and tone for dramatic, atmospheric effects, while 4K 120fps Dolby Vision HDR video recording ensures professional-grade video capture.

For professional users, the Find X9 Pro supports LOG recording with ACES certification, providing flat, data-rich files ideal for professional color grading. This makes the device not just a consumer smartphone but a legitimate tool for content creators and professional photographers.

Optional Hasselblad Teleconverter: Beyond Smartphone Limitations

In an unprecedented move for smartphone photography, OPPO offers an optional Hasselblad Teleconverter that transforms the Find X9 Pro into a 10x optical super-zoom powerhouse. This professional-grade optical accessory enables up to 200x digital zoom for photos and 50x for video, effectively bridging the gap between smartphones and professional camera systems.

The teleconverter attaches to a dedicated carbon fiber case and provides 3.28x magnification over the native 70mm telephoto lens, resulting in a 230mm equivalent focal length. While it does reduce light intake by approximately 3.4 stops (to an equivalent f/6.9 aperture), the massive 200MP sensor provides ample resolution for cropping while maintaining impressive detail.

Revolutionary Battery Technology

The Find X9 Pro's massive 7500mAh battery represents a significant achievement in smartphone engineering, made possible by OPPO's third-generation Silicon-Carbon Battery technology with 15% silicon content. This advanced battery chemistry allows for higher capacity in the same physical space while maintaining a slim 8.25mm profile.

Perhaps even more impressive is the battery's longevity - OPPO engineers the battery to retain over 80% of its original capacity even after five years of typical use. Combined with 80W wired charging and 50W wireless charging, the Find X9 Pro sets new standards for both battery life and charging convenience in flagship smartphones.

Pricing & Global Availability

The OPPO Find X9 Pro is now available globally with a starting price of €1,300 for the 16GB + 512GB configuration. The device comes in two sophisticated color options: Silk White and Titanium Charcoal, both featuring a matte aluminum frame and fingerprint-resistant glass back.

OPPO is positioning the Find X9 Pro as a premium flagship competitor, with pricing that undercuts major rivals like the Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra and Apple iPhone 17 Pro while offering arguably more innovative camera technology and superior battery capacity.

A New Benchmark for Flagship Smartphones

The OPPO Find X9 Pro represents a significant milestone in smartphone evolution, particularly in the realms of mobile photography and battery technology. With its revolutionary 200MP Hasselblad Telephoto camera, industry-leading zoom capabilities, and groundbreaking 7500mAh battery, the device challenges not only other smartphone manufacturers but also encroaches on territory traditionally dominated by dedicated camera systems.

While the smartphone market continues to see incremental improvements year over year, the Find X9 Pro delivers genuine innovation that expands what's possible with mobile photography. The optional Hasselblad Teleconverter accessory further blurs the lines between smartphones and professional photography equipment, offering capabilities previously unimaginable in a mobile device.

For photographers, content creators, and power users who demand both exceptional camera performance and all-day battery life, the OPPO Find X9 Pro sets a new standard that will likely influence the entire smartphone industry in the years to come.
