Camera Innovation & Telephoto Extender

The Vivo X300 series brings revolutionary camera capabilities with its unique sensor arrangement. Interestingly, the standard X300 features a 200MP main sensor while the Pro model uses a 50MP Sony LYT-828 primary sensor but pairs it with a massive 200MP periscope telephoto lens.

Both devices feature Zeiss-tuned optics and benefit from Vivo's advanced imaging chips. The V3+ chip handles computational photography while the VS1 chip provides pre-processing capabilities, resulting in superior image quality, especially in low-light conditions.

The telephoto extender kit, priced at ‚Çπ19,999, includes Zeiss 2.35x teleconverter lenses that provide extended optical zoom without quality loss. The kit features NFC support for automatic activation when attached.

Official Launch Details

Vivo has confirmed the India launch for December 2, 2025, at 12 PM IST. The company has teased an India-exclusive Summit Red color variant for the standard X300 model, while the Pro version will be available in Dune Brown and Phantom Black finishes.

While the leaked prices appear substantial, official pricing will only be confirmed during the launch event. The devices will run Android 16-based OriginOS 6 out of the box, marking Vivo's transition from FunTouch OS in the Indian market.
