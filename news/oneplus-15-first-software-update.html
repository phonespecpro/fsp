OnePlus 15 Gets Its First Software Update After Launch

November 15, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Update Information

OnePlus has pushed out the first software update for the OnePlus 15 less than 24 hours after the phone's launch. The company is rolling out OxygenOS 16.0.1.303 in both India and the EU markets .

First Update Details

OnePlus has demonstrated its commitment to software support by releasing the first update for the newly launched OnePlus 15 within 24 hours of its availability. The OxygenOS 16.0.1.303 update brings important improvements including the latest November 2025 Android security patch, performance enhancements, and new customization options .

The update is being rolled out incrementally, with wider availability expected over the coming days. OnePlus notes that the OTA may arrive at different times depending on the region, and users can check for updates manually in their device settings .

How to Update

The incremental rollout means the update may not be immediately available to all users. OnePlus typically starts with a small percentage of users before expanding to the entire user base over several days. Indian users can report any device issues directly from the OnePlus Community App under the Bug Report section, or by dialing *#800# to access the feedback tool .

About OnePlus 15

The OnePlus 15, which recently launched globally, represents the company's latest flagship offering featuring the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor, a 6.78-inch 165Hz LTPO display, and a massive 7,300mAh battery with 120W wired charging support . The device runs OxygenOS 16 based on Android 16 out of the box, featuring enhanced AI capabilities and the new Mind Space organization tool integrated with Google Gemini .

This first software update addresses early user feedback and optimizes the performance of the device's triple-chip architecture, which includes the main Snapdragon processor, a dedicated touch response chip supporting 3200Hz instant sampling, and a custom Wi-Fi chip for improved connectivity .
