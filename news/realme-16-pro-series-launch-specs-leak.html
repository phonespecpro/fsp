Realme 16 Pro Series Leak Reveals Launch Timeline, Storage Variants, Colors, Camera and Battery Specs

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack
Concept image based on leaks

Leaks Point to Major Early 2026 Launch

Realme appears to be accelerating its release schedule, with multiple leaks now pointing to an imminent launch for its next-generation mid-range champions. According to credible tipsters, the Realme 16 Pro series could debut in India as soon as January 6, 2026—ahead of the company's typical first-quarter cycle . While Realme has not issued an official confirmation, the consistent rumors suggest the new lineup, expected to include the Realme 16 Pro and a higher-end Realme 16 Pro+, will bring significant upgrades to design, performance, and particularly photography .

Leaked Specifications & Features at a Glance

Expected Launch Date January 6, 2026 (India, as per leaks)
Models in Series Realme 16 Pro, Realme 16 Pro+ (expected)
Display 6.78-inch 1.5K (2772x1272 pixels) OLED / AMOLED display with a 144Hz refresh rate.
Processor & Performance Expected to feature a Snapdragon 7-series chipset (specific model unconfirmed). Leaks suggest a focus on "fast performance and fluid visuals".
RAM & Storage Variants Realme 16 Pro: Expected in 8GB+128GB, 8GB+256GB, 12GB+256GB, and 12GB+512GB configurations.
Realme 16 Pro+: Similar memory configurations are also rumored. A TENAA listing for a model (RMX5121) suggested up to 16GB RAM and 1TB storage for a China variant.
Rear Camera System Realme 16 Pro: Dual-camera setup headlined by a massive 200-megapixel primary sensor, paired with an 8-megapixel secondary lens (likely ultrawide). This is a major jump from the Realme 15 Pro's dual 50MP cameras.
Realme 16 Pro+: Also tipped to get the 200MP main sensor, with additional rumors of a telephoto camera for optical zoom.
Front Camera 50-megapixel sensor expected for high-resolution selfies and video calls.
Battery & Charging A colossal 7,000mAh battery is repeatedly mentioned in leaks, likely paired with 80W wired fast charging support. A TENAA certification listed a 6,830mAh (rated) cell.
Design & Build Leaked dimensions point to a slim profile of roughly 162.6 x 77.6 x 7.75mm and a weight of about 192 grams. Expected to run Android 16-based Realme UI 7.

Leaked Color & Storage Options

The leaks provide a clear picture of the aesthetic and configuration choices buyers might have. It's important to note that color names differ slightly between sources, but the palettes are consistent.

Model Leaked Color Options (Names may vary) Leaked RAM & Storage Configurations
Realme 16 Pro Pebble Grey / Master Grey, Master Gold, Orchid Purple / Inspirational Purple. 8GB+128GB, 8GB+256GB, 12GB+256GB, 12GB+512GB.
Realme 16 Pro+ Camellia Pink, Master Gold, Master Grey (as per separate leak). Expected to mirror the Pro model's configurations (8GB+128GB to 12GB+512GB).

A Significant Mid-Range Contender in the Making

If the leaked specifications hold true, the Realme 16 Pro series is shaping up to be a formidable player in the competitive mid-range segment for early 2026. The combination of a 200MP camera and a 7,000mAh battery addresses two of the most common consumer demands: exceptional photography and all-day (or multi-day) battery life.

The rumored January launch would position Realme to capture the post-holiday upgrade market. The series will likely succeed the Realme 15 Pro, which launched in mid-2025 with a starting price of around ₹31,999 in India. While pricing for the new series is still unknown, expectations are that it will remain competitive within the ₹30,000 to ₹40,000 range.

Disclaimer: All details mentioned are based on unconfirmed leaks, rumors, and certifications. Official specifications, features, pricing, and the launch date will be confirmed by Realme at a formal announcement event, expected in the coming weeks.
