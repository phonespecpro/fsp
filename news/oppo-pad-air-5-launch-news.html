Oppo Pad Air 5 Launches With 120 Hz Display, Dimensity 7300-Ultra Chip and Huge 10,050 mAh Battery

December 25, 2025
By: Shedrack
News Update: This article was updated with final specifications and pricing following the official launch on December 25, 2025.

Oppo has officially launched the Oppo Pad Air 5 tablet in China, bringing a compelling mix of a high-refresh-rate display, capable performance, and exceptional battery life to the mid-range tablet segment. The device, which appears to be a rebranded version of the OnePlus Pad Go 2 for the Chinese market, was unveiled on December 25 and is now available for pre-order.

Positioned as a balanced device for entertainment, light productivity, and long-term use, the Pad Air 5 succeeds the Oppo Pad Air 2 and will go on sale starting December 31.

Display & Design

The Oppo Pad Air 5 features a sizable 12.1-inch IPS LCD display with a sharp "2.8K" resolution of 2800 x 1980 pixels and a 7:5 aspect ratio. The screen's standout feature is its 120Hz refresh rate, promising smooth scrolling and responsive interactions, complemented by a 540Hz touch sampling rate for gaming.

With a typical brightness of 600 nits and a peak of 900 nits, the display should remain usable in various lighting conditions. It also covers 98% of the DCI-P3 color gamut and supports 1 billion colors for vibrant visuals.

Encased in a slim metal body, the tablet measures just 6.83mm in thickness and weighs approximately 597 grams (Wi-Fi model), making it relatively portable for its screen size.

Performance & Software

Processor MediaTek Dimensity 7300-Ultra (Octa-core, up to 2.5GHz)
GPU Arm Mali-G615 MC2
RAM 8GB / 12GB LPDDR5X
Storage 128GB / 256GB UFS 3.1
Operating System Android 16 with ColorOS 16
Other Features Quad speakers, Wi-Fi 6, 5G option, Face Unlock

Powering the tablet is the MediaTek Dimensity 7300-Ultra chipset, an octa-core processor designed to handle everyday tasks and moderate gaming efficiently. It is paired with fast LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 3.1 storage. The device runs the latest Android 16 operating system, overlaid with Oppo's ColorOS 16 interface, which includes optimizations for the large screen and productivity features like split-screen multitasking.

Camera, Battery & Connectivity

For cameras, the Pad Air 5 takes a minimalist approach suitable for a tablet. It features an 8-megapixel sensor on both the front and rear. Both cameras support 1080p video recording at 30fps, adequate for video calls and occasional photos.

The headline feature in this department is the massive 10,050mAh battery, which promises extended usage times for media consumption and productivity. When it's time to recharge, the tablet supports 33W SuperVOOC wired fast charging, along with UFCS, PPS, and PD standards for flexibility.

Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth, a USB Type-C port, and an optional 5G cellular model for mobile data on the go. The tablet also supports a keyboard and stylus for enhanced productivity.

Price, Colors & Availability

The Oppo Pad Air 5 is available in multiple configurations and special editions, including standard and "Soft Light" variants. The latter are designed with a display that reduces glare, making them more suitable for prolonged reading.

Availability: The tablet is currently available for pre-order in China and is scheduled to go on sale starting December 31, 2025, via Oppo's official online store. As of now, there is no official information regarding its launch in global markets outside China.

Final Thoughts

The Oppo Pad Air 5 presents itself as a strong contender in the competitive mid-range tablet space. By combining a large, smooth 120Hz display, a capable Dimensity 7300-Ultra processor, and an enormous battery, it checks the key boxes for users prioritizing media consumption and all-day battery life.

While its camera system is basic and its initial availability is limited to China, its pricing offers good value for the specifications on offer. For consumers in its launch market looking for a well-rounded tablet for entertainment and light work without a flagship price tag, the Oppo Pad Air 5 is certainly worth considering.
