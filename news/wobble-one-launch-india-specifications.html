Wobble One Debuts in India, Packs Dimensity 7400 and 50MP Triple Rear Camera

November 19, 2025
By: Shedrack

Breaking News

Indian consumer electronics brand Wobble has officially launched its first smartphone, the Wobble One, marking the company's strategic entry into the highly competitive smartphone market. The device positions itself as a premium mid-range offering with impressive specifications including a MediaTek Dimensity 7400 processor and a 50MP triple camera system.

Owned by Bengaluru-based Indkal Technologies, Wobble has expanded from its existing portfolio of audio products and smart TVs to make its smartphone debut. The company emphasizes that the Wobble One carries a "Made in India" tag and represents their vision for the Indian smartphone market.

Market Impact & Analysis

The Wobble One enters a crowded mid-range smartphone segment in India, competing with established players like Xiaomi, Realme, and Samsung. However, its combination of premium features like the 120Hz AMOLED display, Dimensity 7400 chipset, and clean Android experience gives it a distinctive positioning in the market.

Anand Dubey, CEO of Indkal Technologies, emphasized the significance of this launch: "Wobble entering the smartphone space is not just a product launch—it's a manifestation of our vision. We set out to build a smartphone that reflects the ambition and expression of today's users, while delivering performance that is at least world-class".

The device's "Made in India" credentials align with the government's production-linked incentive (PLI) scheme and growing consumer preference for domestically manufactured electronics. This strategic positioning could work in Wobble's favor as it expands its smartphone portfolio in the coming years.
