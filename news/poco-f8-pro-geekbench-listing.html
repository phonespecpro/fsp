Poco F8 Pro Spotted on Geekbench, Snapdragon 8 Elite Chipset Revealed

November 15, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Geekbench Discovery

Poco F8 Pro has surfaced on Geekbench database with model number 2510DPC44G, revealing key specifications including the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset and 12GB of RAM . The listing confirms the device will run Android 16 out of the box .

Benchmark Performance Revealed

The upcoming Poco F8 Pro has made its first appearance on Geekbench, providing crucial insights into the device's performance capabilities ahead of its official launch. The benchmark listing, spotted on November 14, 2025, reveals impressive scores that position this device as a strong contender in the upper mid-range segment .

According to the Geekbench database, the Poco F8 Pro achieved a single-core score of 2,288 points and a multi-core score of 8,494 points in version 6.5.0 of the benchmark . These results demonstrate significant performance improvements over its predecessor and suggest that Poco is continuing its tradition of offering flagship-level performance at competitive prices.

Snapdragon 8 Elite Specifications

The Geekbench listing confirms that the Poco F8 Pro is powered by Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, featuring an octa-core configuration with two high-performance cores clocked at 4.32 GHz and six efficiency cores running at 3.53 GHz . This 3nm chipset represents a significant generational improvement in both performance and power efficiency.

Early analysis suggests the Poco F8 Pro enjoys approximately 10% single-core advantage over its predecessor, the F7 Ultra, which used the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. However, the multi-core performance uplift exceeds 38%, thanks to Qualcomm's Oryon Gen 2 GPU cores in the Snapdragon 8 Elite . The device tested featured 12GB of RAM, though more memory configurations could be offered at launch .

Launch Timeline & Market Position

The Poco F8 Pro has already received multiple certifications including NBTC, TDRA, and IMDA, suggesting that the global launch is imminent . Industry observers expect the device to debut either in late 2025 or early 2026 at the latest .

Interestingly, reports suggest that the global version may not include the 16GB RAM variant available on the Chinese Redmi K90, with Poco potentially focusing only on 12GB RAM configurations for international markets . This strategic decision likely helps maintain competitive pricing while still offering substantial performance.

The device is expected to arrive in color options including Black, Blue, and Titanium Silver, replicating most of the Redmi K90's finishes . With its flagship-level chipset and expected competitive pricing, the Poco F8 Pro appears positioned to continue Poco's tradition of offering exceptional value in the performance smartphone segment.
