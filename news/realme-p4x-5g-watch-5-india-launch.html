Realme P4x 5G India Launch Set for Early December, Will Debut Alongside Watch 5

November 26, 2025
By: Shedrack

ðŸš€ Dual Product Launch

Realme has officially announced the India launch of its new P-series smartphone, the Realme P4x 5G, alongside the Realme Watch 5. The launch event is scheduled for December 4, 2025, at 12:00 PM IST, marking another significant addition to Realme's expanding product portfolio in the Indian market.

The Chinese smartphone maker confirmed the launch date through its official channels and has already set up dedicated microsites on Flipkart, confirming availability on the e-commerce platform. The company has been actively teasing key specifications of both devices, building anticipation among Indian consumers.

This launch comes just months after Realme introduced the P4 and P4 Pro smartphones in August 2025, expanding the P-series lineup with a more battery-focused variant that promises exceptional endurance and gaming performance.

Battery & Charging Excellence

ðŸ”‹ 7000mAh Titan Battery - "All-Day Use" Guaranteed

The Realme P4x 5G packs a massive 7000mAh Titan Battery that Realme claims will provide "all-day use" for most users. This positions the device as one of the few smartphones in its segment offering such extensive battery capacity.

Complementing the large battery capacity is 45W wired fast charging support, which should provide reasonably quick top-ups despite the substantial battery size. Realme markets the P4x as "The Fastest 7000mAh Pioneer," emphasizing its combination of endurance and charging speed.

For gamers and heavy users, this battery capacity translates to extended gaming sessions and reduced dependency on power banks throughout the day, making it an ideal device for users who prioritize battery life above all else.

Gaming & Performance

The Realme P4x 5G is positioned as a capable gaming device in the budget segment. Realme specifically highlights that the phone will support up to 90 fps gameplay on Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) and up to 120 fps on Free Fire.

Powering these gaming capabilities is the MediaTek Dimensity 7400 Ultra 5G chipset, which Realme claims has scored over 780,000 points on the AnTuTu benchmarking platform. The processor is paired with what Realme calls "dynamic RAM" that can extend up to 18GB, ensuring smooth multitasking and gaming performance.

To maintain optimal performance during extended gaming sessions, Realme has equipped the P4x 5G with a substantial 5,300 square millimeter vapour chamber (VC) cooling solution. The company claims this cooling system can reduce CPU temperature by up to 20 degrees Celsius.

Both the Realme P4x 5G and Watch 5 will be available for purchase through multiple channels including Realme's official website, Flipkart, and mainline retail stores after the December 4 launch. The availability on Flipkart has already been confirmed through dedicated microsites that went live ahead of the official announcement.

The Realme Watch 5, which will be launched alongside the smartphone, is already official in markets like the Philippines and is expected to offer comprehensive health and fitness tracking features aimed at everyday use.

Market Impact & Final Thoughts

The Realme P4x 5G enters a highly competitive budget smartphone segment in India, distinguishing itself with its massive 7000mAh batteryâ€”a feature typically found in more expensive gaming phones or specialized battery-focused devices. The combination of this substantial battery capacity with 5G connectivity, a high-refresh-rate display, and capable gaming performance creates a compelling package for budget-conscious consumers.

Realme's strategy with the P4x appears to focus on the "battery anxiety" that many smartphone users experience, particularly those who game extensively or use their devices heavily throughout the day. By addressing this pain point directly while maintaining competitive performance specifications, Realme positions the P4x as a practical choice rather than just a spec-driven offering.

The December 4 launch event will reveal whether the Realme P4x 5G and Watch 5 live up to their promising specifications and whether their pricing will make them truly compelling options in their respective categories.
