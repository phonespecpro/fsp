The OPPO A6x 5G is expected to feature a practical design with attention to durability. The device will measure 8.58mm in thickness and weigh approximately 212 grams, making it substantial but manageable for daily use .

Leaked promotional images suggest the phone will sport a vertically arranged pill-shaped camera module positioned in the top left corner of the rear panel. The device is expected to be available in grey and light blue color options, catering to different aesthetic preferences .

At the heart of the OPPO A6x 5G is the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset, which promises reliable 5G performance for budget-conscious users. This 6nm processor is capable of handling everyday tasks smoothly and provides efficient 5G connectivity for faster downloads and streaming .

The device will ship with Android 15-based ColorOS 15 out of the box, offering the latest software features and security updates. OPPO's ColorOS 15 is expected to bring optimization improvements and new customization options to enhance the user experience .

For selfie enthusiasts and video calls, the 5MP front camera should suffice for basic needs. The camera system is expected to support 1080p video recording at 30fps from both front and rear cameras, making it suitable for casual video recording and social media content creation .
