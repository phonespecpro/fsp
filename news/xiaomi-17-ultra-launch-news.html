Xiaomi 17 Ultra Packs 1-Inch Sensor, 200 MP Periscope Zoom and 6,800 mAh Battery in New Flagship Launch

December 25, 2025
By: Shedrack

Xiaomi has officially launched the Xiaomi 17 Ultra, its most ambitious camera flagship to date, setting a new benchmark for mobile photography with a groundbreaking 200MP periscope telephoto camera and a massive 1-inch main sensor . Launched in China on December 25, 2025, the device is a direct statement of intent to compete at the very top of the smartphone market .

Alongside the standard model, Xiaomi unveiled a special Leica Edition, which introduces the industry's first mechanical zoom ring on a smartphone, blending classic camera craftsmanship with modern technology . The phone is powered by the latest Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset and features a colossal 6,800mAh battery, the largest ever in an Ultra model, all packed into a body that is over 1mm thinner than its predecessor .

The Revolution in Your Pocket

200MP Continuous Zoom
A 1/1.4-inch periscope telephoto offering true, lossless optical zoom from 75mm to 100mm (3x to 4.3x), a first for smartphones .
1-Inch Light Hunter
The 50MP main camera uses a 1-inch Light Hunter 1050L sensor with LOFIC tech for an incredible 14EV native dynamic range .
Snapdragon 8 Elite
Driven by the cutting-edge Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset, offering top-tier performance for gaming and AI tasks .
Massive 6,800mAh
A giant silicon-carbon battery enables multi-day use, supported by 90W wired and 50W wireless charging .

Camera System: Redefining Mobile Photography

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra's triple-camera system is engineered in partnership with Leica and represents a significant leap forward, particularly in zoom technology.

The 200MP Zoom Advantage

Unlike other flagships that offer fixed zoom steps (like 3x and 5x), the Xiaomi 17 Ultra's 200MP Samsung HPE sensor provides continuous optical zoom between 75mm and 100mm . This means whether you're at 3.2x or 4.1x, the image remains true optical quality without digital cropping . Xiaomi has showcased sample photos demonstrating superior detail and consistent color compared to unnamed competitors . The system also supports telephoto macro shots as close as 30cm .

Main Camera 50MP, f/1.67, 1-inch type "Light Hunter 1050L" sensor with LOFIC technology
Periscope Telephoto 200MP, 1/1.4-inch Samsung HPE sensor, 75-100mm continuous optical zoom, Leica APO certified
Ultra-Wide Camera 50MP, 115° field of view, 5cm macro focus
Front Camera 50MP
LEICA Edition: For the Purist

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra Leica Edition transcends being a mere smartphone; it's a homage to classic photography. It features a unique mechanical zoom ring built into the frame, allowing tactile control over focal length, exposure, and white balance—a world-first for smartphones . Its design is inspired by Leica M-series cameras, complete with a knurled pattern on the frame and the iconic red Leica logo .

The Master Zoom Ring: Rotate the physical ring to adjust zoom smoothly or switch it to control exposure settings. It comes with a special gift box including a magnetic case, lens cap, and cleaning cloth .

Software enhancements include exclusive "Leica Moment" modes with classic film simulations and a 3:2 aspect ratio for a full-frame camera feel . It also boasts a dedicated encryption chip and dual-satellite communication support .

Display, Performance & Endurance

Display 6.9-inch flat AMOLED LTPO, 2608 x 1200p, 1-120Hz, 3,500 nits peak brightness
Processor Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5
RAM & Storage Up to 16GB LPDDR5X RAM, up to 1TB UFS 4.1 storage
Battery & Charging 6,800mAh silicon-carbon battery, 90W wired, 50W wireless, 22.5W reverse charging
OS & Software HyperOS 3.0 based on Android 16. Includes AI features and cross-platform mirroring with Apple devices
Durability IP66/IP68/IP69 ratings for water, dust, and high-pressure resistance

The device features a sophisticated cooling system with a dual-path liquid cooling pump, improving thermal conductivity by 50% over the previous generation to handle sustained performance .

Pricing, Colors & Availability

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra is positioned as a premium flagship, with sales in China starting on December 27, 2025 . Global availability is expected in the coming months .

Color Options: The standard model is available in Black, White, Cool Smoke Purple, and Starry Green . The Starry Green variant features mineral particles for a metallic finish . The Leica Edition comes in Black and Off-White .

Final Verdict: A New Camera Champion

The Xiaomi 17 Ultra is not an iterative update; it's a strategic strike at the heart of the high-end smartphone market. By focusing its immense engineering resources on solving core photography challenges—like offering true continuous zoom and maximizing light capture—Xiaomi has built a device that will compel its competitors to respond .

While its premium price and initial limited availability are considerations, the Xiaomi 17 Ultra, especially the innovative Leica Edition, sets a compelling new standard for what a camera phone can be. It is arguably the most complete and ambitious flagship Xiaomi has ever created .
