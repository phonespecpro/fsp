Realme 16 Pro Storage and Colour Variants Leak Well Before Launch

November 19, 2025
By: Shedrack

Realme's upcoming mid-range champion, the Realme 16 Pro, has had its complete storage and color lineup revealed months ahead of its expected launch, giving consumers a clear picture of what to expect from the device's variant strategy in the Indian market .

üì± Complete Variant Breakdown

The Realme 16 Pro, carrying model number RMX5120 for the Indian market, will be available in three distinct color options and four storage configurations, providing ample choice for different consumer segments and budgets .

Color Options: Sophisticated Palette

Realme appears to be continuing its tradition of offering stylish color options with the 16 Pro lineup. The device will debut in three sophisticated colorways designed to appeal to different style preferences .

Pebble Grey

Timeless elegance with a neutral tone perfect for professionals seeking a sophisticated, understated look .

Master Gold

Luxury aesthetics with a premium metallic finish that delivers a high-end appearance .

Orchid Purple

Vibrant personality and contemporary flair, targeting younger audiences and fashion-forward users .

Storage Configurations: Extensive Choice

Realme is offering four distinct storage combinations for each color, ensuring the 16 Pro competes across multiple price segments while catering to different user needs .

The storage lineup begins with the entry-level 8GB RAM + 128GB storage variant for budget-conscious buyers, followed by the 8GB + 256GB option that provides balanced performance for mainstream users handling multitasking and media storage .

For power users, Realme offers the 12GB + 256GB configuration, while the flagship 12GB RAM + 512GB storage variant targets content creators, gaming enthusiasts, and users who demand maximum performance and ample space for apps, games, and 4K content .

Market Context and Launch Timeline

This leak comes at a strategic time, as Realme's mid-range number series has settled into a predictable six-month refresh cycle . The company launched the Realme 15 lineup back in July 2025, which positions the Realme 16 series for an arrival sometime around January or February 2026 .

‚è∞ Expected Launch Timeline

Based on Realme's established product cycle, consumers can expect the Realme 16 Pro to debut in India around January or February 2026, with the comprehensive color and storage matrix indicating Realme is preparing a major assault on India's competitive smartphone landscape .

What Remains Unknown

While the storage and color options are now clearer, the leak doesn't reveal anything about the device's design, chipset, or camera upgrades . However, given Realme's product strategy, we can expect the 16 Pro to share many specifications with its predecessor, the Realme 15 Pro .

The Realme 15 Pro featured a 6.8-inch AMOLED display with 144Hz refresh rate, Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 chipset, dual 50MP rear cameras, and a 7000mAh battery with 80W charging . It's reasonable to expect similar or slightly upgraded specifications for the 16 Pro, though official details remain forthcoming .

Competitive Landscape

With the model number RMX5120 confirmed for the Indian market, Realme appears ready to challenge competitors like the OnePlus 15R, iQOO 15, and Xiaomi's upcoming flagships . The diverse storage options allow aggressive pricing across tiers while maintaining premium appeal through color differentiation.

The 12GB RAM variants position the device firmly in the performance flagship category, while 8GB options maintain accessibility for mid-range buyers. This strategy mirrors successful launches from competing brands targeting India's price-sensitive yet feature-hungry market .

As we move closer to the expected launch timeframe, more details about the Realme 16 Pro's complete specifications, pricing, and availability are likely to emerge. For now, consumers have a clear picture of the storage and color choices that will be available when the device officially launches in early 2026.
