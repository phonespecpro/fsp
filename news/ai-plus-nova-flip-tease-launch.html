Ai+ Teases Nova Flip Foldable Smartphone With Sleek Design Ahead of Launch

December 16, 2025
By: Shedrack
Sleek Silver/Grey

Ai+ Makes Its Foldable Debut

The smartphone brand Ai+, led by former Realme India CEO Madhav Sheth, has officially stepped into the foldable arena . The company has teased its first flip-style foldable smartphone, the Ai+ Nova Flip, marking a significant expansion of its portfolio following the launch of the Nova 5G and Pulse models earlier in 2025 .

Announced via social media teasers and an official press release, the Nova Flip is confirmed to launch in India in the first quarter of 2026 (Q1 2026) with a disruptive price tag of under ₹40,000 . This positions it as one of the most affordable foldable options aiming to bring the form factor to a wider audience .

Sleek Design & First Look

The initial teaser provides a clear glimpse of the Nova Flip's aesthetics and build .

  • Color & Finish: A sleek Silver or Grey chassis is shown in the teaser .
  • Signature Accent: A distinctive red-accented power button, which is also expected to double as a fingerprint scanner .
  • Layout: The right side panel houses the volume rockers and the signature power button. Visible antenna lines and a protruding camera module are also noted .
  • Form Factor: A classic vertical (flip-style) foldable design, prioritizing compactness .

Adaptive NxtQuantum OS

The software is positioned as a core differentiator, not just a hardware gimmick .

  • State-Aware: The NxtQuantum OS adapts its interface and functionality based on whether the phone is folded or unfolded .
  • Closed Mode: Optimized for quick, glanceable interactions—checking notifications, time, or weather without opening the phone .
  • Open Mode: Delivers a full, uncompromised smartphone experience .
  • Privacy-First: Promises zero pre-installed bloatware, no invasive trackers, and user control over data .

Brand Strategy & Positioning

The Nova Flip is more than a product; it's a strategic move for the Ai+ brand .

  • Flagship Series Debut: It is the first device in Ai+'s new flagship "Nova Series," which will later include Nova Pro, Nova Ultra, and a foldable variant .
  • Market Gap: Aims to make the premium foldable experience accessible at a mid-range price point (under ₹40,000) .
  • Philosophy: Focuses on "purposeful" design—bringing back compactness and a sense of control over device engagement .

Flip smartphones bring back things people genuinely miss: compactness, physical interaction, and a sense of control. There’s nostalgia in that experience, but there’s also a very modern need behind it. People want devices that are easier to carry, easier to live with, and less demanding of their attention.

— Madhav Sheth, CEO of Ai+ Smartphone

Analysis: A Calculated Entry into a Growing Market

The teaser of the Ai+ Nova Flip is a strategic and well-calculated move. By entering the foldable market with a price-focused device, Ai+ is targeting a significant gap. While brands like Samsung, Motorola, and Huawei have established foldables at premium prices, the sub-₹40,000 segment in India remains relatively open .

The emphasis on the adaptive NxtQuantum OS and a bloatware-free experience aligns with growing consumer demand for cleaner software and greater control, potentially giving it an edge over competitors that bundle heavy skins and apps .

What to Watch For: The success of the Nova Flip will ultimately hinge on the full technical specifications—especially the performance of its chipset, the quality of its foldable display, and the durability of its hinge—all at that aggressive sub-₹40,000 price. If Ai+ can deliver a balanced package, the Nova Flip could become a major catalyst for mainstream foldable adoption in India. More details are expected as we approach the Q1 2026 launch window.
