Oppo's Budget 5G Contender Leaks in Full

Oppo is gearing up to launch the A6x in India, and key specifications have surfaced online ahead of the official announcement . According to leaks from reliable tipster Abhishek Yadav, the device will feature MediaTek's Dimensity 6300 chipset paired with a massive 6,500mAh battery, positioning it as a strong contender in the budget 5G segment .

The A6x appears to be the successor to the Oppo A5x, which launched in May 2025 at a starting price of Rs. 13,999 . With its impressive battery capacity and 5G capabilities, the A6x could set new standards for what consumers can expect from budget smartphones in 2025.

Display and Design

Display Size 6.75-inch HD+ LCD
Refresh Rate 120Hz
Dimensions 8.58mm thickness, 212g weight
Design Vertical pill-shaped camera module
Colors Blue and Black options

The Oppo A6x will feature a substantial 6.75-inch HD+ LCD display with a smooth 120Hz refresh rate, making it one of the larger phones in its segment . The high refresh rate should provide smoother scrolling and more responsive touch interactions compared to standard 60Hz displays commonly found in budget devices.

Leaked promotional images reveal a significant design departure from its predecessor, featuring a vertical pill-shaped camera module that houses a dual-camera setup . The device will be available in blue and black color options, maintaining Oppo's tradition of offering stylish color choices even in its budget segments.

Performance and Software

Chipset MediaTek Dimensity 6300
RAM/Storage 4GB + 128GB (base variant)
Operating System Android 15 with ColorOS 15
Durability IP64 dust and splash resistance

At the heart of the Oppo A6x lies the MediaTek Dimensity 6300 chipset, the same processor that powers its predecessor, the A5x . This 5G-capable chipset should deliver reliable performance for everyday tasks including browsing, streaming, and moderate gaming, while maintaining power efficiency to complement the large battery.

The device will ship with Android 15-based ColorOS 15 out of the box, providing users with the latest software features and security updates . The IP64 rating for dust and splash resistance adds an extra layer of durability, protecting the device from everyday accidents involving water exposure .

Camera System

Main Camera 13MP primary sensor
Secondary Camera VGA sensor
Front Camera 5MP selfie camera

The camera setup on the A6x appears to be one area where Oppo may have made compromises to hit the budget price point. The device is tipped to feature a 13-megapixel primary camera paired with a VGA sensor, representing a downgrade from the 32MP main shooter found on the A5x .

For selfies and video calls, a 5-megapixel front-facing camera is expected . While the camera specifications may not be class-leading, they should suffice for basic photography needs and social media sharing, which aligns with the expectations for devices in this price segment.

Battery and Charging

Battery Capacity 6,500mAh
Charging Speed 45W wired fast charging
Battery Increase 500mAh larger than A5x

The 6,500mAh battery represents a significant 500mAh increase over the 6,000mAh cell in the A5x, making it what Oppo claims will be "the segment's biggest battery" phone . This substantial capacity should easily provide all-day battery life for most users, even with heavy usage patterns.

Despite the larger battery, Oppo has maintained 45W fast charging support, which should provide reasonably quick top-ups when needed . The combination of massive battery capacity and fast charging addresses two key concerns for budget smartphone users - battery anxiety and charging wait times.

Launch Timeline and Market Position

The Oppo A6x is expected to launch in India soon, following its recent spotting on Malaysia's SIRIM database with model number CPH2819 . This certification suggests an imminent debut in Asian markets, with India likely being one of the first launch countries.

With an expected starting price of Rs. 15,499 for the 4GB + 128GB variant, the A6x will compete in the highly contested sub-Rs. 15,000 segment . It will face competition from other budget 5G devices from brands like Xiaomi, Realme, and Samsung, but its massive battery capacity could be a key differentiating factor.
