OnePlus 15R Spotted on Geekbench With 12GB RAM and Android 16

December 5, 2025
By: Shedrack

Benchmark Leak Reveals Key Specs Ahead of Launch

With less than two weeks to go until its official unveiling, the highly anticipated OnePlus 15R has made a telling appearance on the Geekbench benchmarking database. The listing, first spotted on December 5, 2025, provides the clearest look yet at the core hardware powering OnePlus's upcoming "value flagship" .

The device, identified by the model number CPH2767, is widely believed to be the global variant of the OnePlus 15R, set to launch in India, Europe, and North America on December 17, 2025 . The benchmark confirms it will be the world's first smartphone to ship with Qualcomm's new Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset, paired with 12GB of RAM and running Android 16 out of the box .

Geekbench Performance Analysis

The Geekbench listing offers concrete numbers to gauge the phone's potential. According to the results from the Geekbench 6.5.0 test, the OnePlus 15R achieved the following scores:

Single-Core Score 2,784 points
Multi-Core Score 9,329 points
Test Platform Geekbench 6.5.0 for Android AArch64
Detected RAM 10.88GB (marketed as 12GB)
Motherboard ID "canoe"

These scores indicate robust performance, placing the device firmly in the high-end segment. While they are slightly lower than the figures posted by the flagship OnePlus 15 (which uses the more powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5), they suggest the 15R will deliver a near-flagship experience for a lower price . The chipset configuration listed includes an octa-core CPU with two high-performance cores clocked at 3.80GHz and six performance cores at 3.32GHz, aligning perfectly with the specifications of the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 .

Confirmed and Expected Specifications

Beyond the benchmark leak, OnePlus has officially confirmed several key features, and more details have surfaced from related leaks. The 15R is expected to be a global rebrand of the OnePlus Ace 6T recently launched in China .

Launch Date December 17, 2025
Chipset Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 (confirmed)
Display 1.5K (likely 1264x2780) AMOLED, 165Hz refresh rate, 1800 nits brightness (confirmed)
Durability IP66, IP68, IP69, IP69K ratings for dust/water resistance (confirmed)
Battery (Rumored) Likely around 8,000mAh (based on Ace 6T's 8,300mAh) with fast charging
Camera (Rumored) Dual-camera system with a 50MP main sensor
Colors Charcoal Black and Mint Breeze (confirmed)

OnePlus has also confirmed the phone will feature a custom G2 Wi-Fi chip for superior connectivity and the same Detailmax Engine found in the flagship OnePlus 15 for enhanced gaming and display performance .

The New Snapdragon Strategy and Market Position

The inclusion of the Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 is particularly significant. This chip represents Qualcomm's new dual-flagship strategy, inspired by Apple's approach with its A-series chips . For the first time, Qualcomm is offering two top-tier chipsets in the same generation: the elite "Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5" for absolute flagship phones and the "Snapdragon 8 Gen 5" for high-performance devices like the 15R .

This allows OnePlus to equip the 15R with a current-generation, flagship-architecture chipset rather than a previous year's model, delivering better performance and efficiency to the "value flagship" segment . Rudolf Xu, OnePlus's Senior Product Marketing Manager, explained that this strategy makes it easier for the company to port software and performance features from its flagship model down to the R-series, benefiting consumers with the latest technology .

Launch Event and Final Thoughts

The OnePlus 15R will not debut alone. OnePlus has confirmed it will be unveiled alongside two other ecosystem devices on December 17: the OnePlus Pad Go 2 tablet and the OnePlus Watch Lite . Pre-order perks, including discounts and free accessories like cases and adapters, are expected in various regions .

Bottom Line: The Geekbench listing solidifies the OnePlus 15R's position as a formidable contender in the high-value performance segment. By combining the new Snapdragon 8 Gen 5, a high-refresh-rate display, exceptional durability ratings, and a likely large battery, it aims to deliver a near-flagship experience. All official details will be revealed at the global launch event on December 17, 2025 .
