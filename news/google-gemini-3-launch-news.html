Google has launched Gemini 3, its most intelligent AI model to date, marking a significant leap forward in reasoning capabilities, multimodal understanding, and practical usability. The new model represents Google's biggest AI release this year, featuring state-of-the-art reasoning designed to grasp depth and nuance while delivering more genuine insights instead of clichÃ©d responses .

Alphabet CEO Sundar Pichai announced that Gemini 3 is now shipping across Google's ecosystem, noting that "it's amazing to think that in just two years, AI has evolved from simply reading text and images to reading the room" . This release also marks the first time Google is shipping a new model to Search on day one, demonstrating the company's confidence in Gemini 3's capabilities .

Breaking News: Key Launch Details

Gemini 3 Pro is available starting today for Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search. The model significantly outperforms Gemini 2.5 Pro across every major AI benchmark and introduces groundbreaking capabilities like generative interfaces and enhanced agentic functions .

Revolutionary Capabilities

What sets Gemini 3 apart is its sophisticated approach to understanding context and intent. The model is "much better at figuring out the context and intent behind your request, so you get what you need with less prompting" . This translates to responses that are "smart, concise and direct, trading clichÃ© and flattery for genuine insight â€” telling you what you need to hear, not just what you want to hear" .

Generative UI: A New Paradigm

One of the most striking innovations in Gemini 3 is its ability to create "generative interfaces" - dynamic, interactive responses that go beyond traditional text answers. As Josh Woodward, VP of Google Labs, explained, "Visual layout generates an immersive, magazine-style view complete with photos and modules. These elements don't just look good but invite your input to further tailor the results" .

This capability represents a fundamental shift from AI that simply answers questions to AI that creates customized tools and experiences for each query. The model automatically designs appropriate interfaces based on the context - creating child-friendly explanations with interactive elements for young learners, while generating sophisticated analytical tools for professional queries .

Google Antigravity: The Developer Revolution

Alongside Gemini 3, Google launched Antigravity, a new agentic development platform that represents "our new agentic development platform, evolving the IDE into the agent-first era" . This platform enables developers to operate at a higher, task-oriented level, with AI agents that can autonomously plan and execute complex software tasks .

Antigravity uses Gemini 3's advanced reasoning to drive end-to-end workflows. In demonstrations, agents independently planned, coded applications, and validated their execution through browser-based computer use, showing the potential to significantly boost developer productivity .

Enhanced Search Experience

Gemini 3 is transforming Google Search through improved reasoning and new generative UI experiences. The model enables Search to perform additional background queries, uncovering sources that older models would have missed and better understanding the intent behind questions .

Starting immediately, Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers in the U.S. can select "Thinking: 3 Pro reasoning and generative layouts" in Search's AI Mode to access these enhanced capabilities . The company plans to roll out Gemini 3 to all AI Mode users in the US soon, with subscribers receiving higher usage limits .

Availability & Access

Immediate Access: Gemini 3 Pro is available now for Google AI Pro and Ultra subscribers in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search .

Developers: Accessible today through Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and the new Antigravity platform .

Coming Soon: Gemini 3 Deep Think mode will launch in the coming weeks for AI Ultra subscribers, offering enhanced reasoning for complex problems .

General Availability: Free access to Gemini 3 in Search's AI Mode will roll out to all US users in the coming weeks .

The Future of AI Assistance

Gemini 3 represents a fundamental shift from chatbots to true digital coworkers. As Ethan Mollick from One Useful Thing noted after testing the model, "The era of the chatbot is turning into the era of the digital coworker. To be very clear, Gemini 3 isn't perfect, and it still needs a manager who can guide and check it. But it suggests that 'human in the loop' is evolving from 'human who fixes AI mistakes' to 'human who directs AI work'" .

With its superior reasoning, contextual understanding, and ability to generate interactive experiences, Gemini 3 sets a new standard for what's possible with AI assistance. As Demis Hassabis, CEO of Google DeepMind, stated, "It's the best model in the world for multimodal understanding and our most powerful agentic and vibe coding model yet, delivering richer visualizations and deeper interactivity" .
