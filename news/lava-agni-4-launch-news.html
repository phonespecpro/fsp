Lava Agni 4 Release Date Set: Affordable Dimensity 8350 Phone with 7,000mAh Battery and Dual 50MP Cameras

November 6, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

INDIA, NOVEMBER 5, 2025 - Lava has officially confirmed that its highly anticipated Agni 4 smartphone will launch in India on November 20, 2025, positioning the device as a formidable contender in the affordable flagship segment with its MediaTek Dimensity 8350 chipset and massive 7,000mAh battery capacity .

The company has been steadily revealing design details and key specifications through social media teasers, showcasing a completely redesigned smartphone that moves away from the Agni 3's secondary rear display in favor of a more conventional but premium horizontal camera bar housing dual 50MP sensors .

"Engineered for precision. Launching on 20.11.25," Lava stated in their official announcement, emphasizing the phone's premium construction and focused design approach that targets users seeking flagship-level performance without the premium price tag .

Completely Redesigned Aesthetic

The Lava Agni 4 represents a significant design departure from its predecessor, featuring a distinctive horizontal pill-shaped camera module that houses the dual-camera system and dual-LED flash . Gone is the secondary rear display that characterized the Agni 3, replaced by a cleaner, more focused aesthetic with prominent "AGNI" branding .

Premium Build Quality

One of the most notable upgrades in the Agni 4 is its aluminum frame, marking a significant step up in build quality from previous models . The right edge features not only the standard volume rocker and power button but also an additional customizable Action Key that can be configured to launch specific functions or applications .

The device will be available in two color options: Lunar Mist and Phantom Black, both featuring the same premium materials and construction . Early teasers suggest the phone maintains a relatively slim profile despite housing the substantial 7,000mAh battery, with visible antenna lines confirming the metal construction .

Performance That Competes With Flagships

At the heart of the Lava Agni 4 lies the MediaTek Dimensity 8350 chipset, a 4nm octa-core processor built on TSMC's manufacturing process that delivers flagship-level performance while maintaining power efficiency . This places the Agni 4 in the same performance category as established competitors like the OnePlus Nord CE 5 and Infinix GT 30 Pro .

The device is expected to feature UFS 4.0 storage, significantly faster than the UFS 3.1 standard found in many mid-range devices, enabling quicker app loading times, faster file transfers, and smoother overall system performance . Combined with LPDDR5X RAM, the Agni 4 promises to deliver a premium user experience that belies its expected price point.

Immersive Display Technology

The Lava Agni 4 is expected to feature a 6.78-inch AMOLED display with Full HD+ resolution and a 120Hz refresh rate, providing smooth scrolling and responsive touch experience ideal for both multimedia consumption and gaming . The display will utilize a flat panel design rather than curved edges, potentially appealing to users who prefer more practical screen geometry .

While exact brightness specifications haven't been confirmed, the panel is expected to offer vibrant colors and deep contrasts characteristic of AMOLED technology, making it suitable for watching videos and playing games. The device is also confirmed to feature dual stereo speakers, enhancing the multimedia experience beyond what was offered in the previous generation .

Dual 50MP Camera System

The Agni 4's camera system represents both a simplification and an upgrade over its predecessor. While moving from a triple-camera to dual-camera setup, both sensors are expected to be 50-megapixel units, suggesting Lava is focusing on quality over quantity in the camera department .

The horizontal camera bar houses both sensors along with a dual-LED flash positioned between them, creating a balanced and distinctive visual identity . The camera module is partially surrounded by LED lights, potentially serving both functional and aesthetic purposes . While exact camera specifications beyond the resolution remain unconfirmed, the setup is expected to deliver significant improvements in image quality over the Agni 3.

Unprecedented Battery Life

One of the most significant upgrades in the Agni 4 is its massive 7,000mAh battery, a substantial 40% increase over the 5,000mAh cell in the Agni 3 . This capacity places it among smartphones with the largest batteries in the market, potentially delivering multi-day battery life for average users.

The battery capacity was hinted at through certification listings, including a Nemko certification for a 7,050mAh lithium-ion cell that strongly correlates with the Agni 4 . While exact charging speeds remain unconfirmed, the device is expected to support at least 66W fast charging, matching its predecessor's capability . Given the substantial battery capacity, even moderate charging speeds should provide significant runtime from shorter charging sessions.

Clean Software Experience

Following Lava's recent approach, the Agni 4 is expected to ship with a clean, near-stock version of Android, free from bloatware and unnecessary customizations . This software approach has been well-received in previous Lava devices, providing a smooth user experience without the performance overhead of heavy skins.

Interestingly, a recent fan-led petition has emerged urging Lava to develop its own in-house Android OS and UI, arguing that it could provide "a more secure and personalised user experience that aligns with the specific needs of Indian consumers" . While this petition has gathered some support, Lava has not indicated any departure from their current software strategy for the Agni 4.

Pricing & Availability

While Lava has not officially revealed pricing for the Agni 4, the device is expected to be positioned in the ₹20,000 to ₹25,000 price range in India, making it a compelling value proposition given its specifications . This would place it slightly above the Agni 3's starting price of ₹20,999 while offering significant upgrades in performance, battery life, and build quality .

Launch Date: November 20, 2025 (India)

Colors: Lunar Mist, Phantom Black

A New Contender in the Value Flagship Segment

The Lava Agni 4 represents an ambitious effort to redefine the value flagship segment, combining premium features like the Dimensity 8350 chipset and metal frame with exceptional battery life through its 7,000mAh capacity. The device's November 20 launch comes at a crucial time when competition in the mid-range segment is intensifying.

With its focused approach to cameras, clean software experience, and premium construction, the Agni 4 has the potential to challenge established players like the OnePlus Nord CE 5 and Poco X7 . As the launch date approaches, all eyes will be on Lava's final pricing and availability details, which will ultimately determine whether the Agni 4 can deliver on its promise of flagship-level features at an accessible price point.
