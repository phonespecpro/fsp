OnePlus Nord 5 Receives Stable OxygenOS 16 Update Based on Android 16

November 26, 2025
By: Shedrack

üöÄ Stable Update Rolling Out

OnePlus has officially begun rolling out the stable OxygenOS 16 update based on Android 16 to OnePlus Nord 5 devices in India. This major software upgrade brings a completely redesigned user interface, enhanced AI capabilities, and significant performance improvements to the popular mid-range smartphone.

The update, which carries version number CPH2707_16.0.1.300(EX01) for the Nord 5, marks the device's transition from OxygenOS 15 based on Android 15 to the latest Android 16 platform . This rollout follows OnePlus's official release of OxygenOS 16 in India last month and represents a significant milestone for Nord 5 users.

According to reports from Beebom, the update is being released in a phased manner, meaning it may take some time to reach all devices. Users can manually check for the update through their device settings if they haven't received the notification yet .

About OnePlus Nord 5

The OnePlus Nord 5, launched on July 8, 2025, represents a significant step up for the Nord series with its Snapdragon 8s Gen 3 chipset - making it the first Nord device to feature an 8-series processor . The phone boasts impressive specifications that make it well-suited for the OxygenOS 16 upgrade:

Display 6.83-inch Swift AMOLED, 144Hz, 1.5K resolution
Processor Qualcomm Snapdragon 8s Gen 3
RAM & Storage 8GB/12GB RAM, 256GB/512GB storage
Cameras 50MP main (OIS) + 8MP ultrawide, 50MP selfie
Battery 6800mAh with 80W fast charging
Previous OS OxygenOS 15 based on Android 15

With its powerful hardware foundation, the Nord 5 is perfectly positioned to take full advantage of the performance enhancements and AI features introduced in OxygenOS 16 and Android 16.

Update Commitment & Future Support

OnePlus has committed to providing the Nord 5 with four years of major Android OS updates and six years of security patches . This commitment ensures that the device will receive support through multiple future Android versions, making it a solid long-term investment.

The OxygenOS 16 update also includes the latest security patches, further enhancing the device's protection against potential vulnerabilities. Users can expect regular security updates as part of OnePlus's ongoing support for the device.

Looking ahead, the Nord 5 is expected to receive Android 17-based OxygenOS 17 in the future, continuing OnePlus's track record of providing substantial software support for its devices, particularly in the competitive mid-range segment.

Final Thoughts

The stable OxygenOS 16 rollout for the OnePlus Nord 5 represents a significant software milestone that brings the device to the latest Android platform while introducing numerous enhancements and new features. From the completely redesigned user interface with Flux Themes 2.0 to the expanded AI capabilities and improved performance, this update substantially enhances the user experience.

Nord 5 owners in India should check for the update now through their device settings, keeping in mind the phased nature of the rollout. For those who haven't received it immediately, patience is advised as the update will likely reach all devices within the coming days.
