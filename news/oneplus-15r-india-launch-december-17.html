Official Launch Confirmation

OnePlus has officially confirmed that the much-anticipated OnePlus 15R will make its debut in the Indian market on December 17, 2025 . The smartphone will be unveiled alongside the OnePlus Pad Go 2, marking a significant dual-product launch event for the company as it closes out the year.

The Chinese smartphone maker revealed that both devices will be available through Amazon India and the company's official online store, ensuring wide accessibility for Indian consumers . This launch follows the recent introduction of the flagship OnePlus 15 in India earlier this month, completing the company's current-generation smartphone lineup.

The OnePlus 15R represents a significant upgrade over its predecessor, particularly in the performance department. The device is expected to be powered by Qualcomm's upcoming Snapdragon 8 Gen 5 chipset, positioning it as one of the first smartphones to feature this new processor . This represents a strategic decision by OnePlus to use a newer chipset rather than the Snapdragon 8 Elite found in some Chinese variants.

Remarkably, the 15R matches the flagship OnePlus 15's comprehensive IP ratings, featuring IP66, IP68, IP69, and IP69K certifications for exceptional dust and water resistance . This level of protection is unusual in this price segment and demonstrates OnePlus's commitment to delivering premium features across its product lineup.

The OnePlus Pad Go 2 introduces several firsts for the company's tablet lineup. Most notably, the Shadow Black variant will become the first OnePlus tablet to offer 5G connectivity, significantly enhancing its utility for users who need constant internet access on the go . This represents a major upgrade over the previous generation's connectivity options.

Another significant addition is the introduction of stylus support through the new OnePlus Pad Go 2 Stylo . This feature expands the tablet's capabilities for note-taking, creative work, and productivity tasks, positioning it as a more versatile device for students and professionals alike. The tablet will succeed the original OnePlus Pad Go, which was launched in October 2023 .

Expected Pricing and Market Position

While OnePlus has not officially revealed pricing for either device, industry analysts expect the OnePlus 15R to see a price increase compared to its predecessor. Both devices will be positioned as premium mid-range offerings, with the 15R serving as a "value flagship" alternative to the main OnePlus 15 series, while the Pad Go 2 aims to provide tablet productivity at an accessible price point.

Global Launch Strategy

The December 17 launch event extends beyond India, with OnePlus confirming availability in multiple global markets. Both the OnePlus 15R and Pad Go 2 will be released in North America, Europe, and India simultaneously . Additionally, the event will see the introduction of the OnePlus Watch Lite, though this smartwatch will be exclusive to European markets .

This coordinated global launch strategy underscores OnePlus's commitment to building a comprehensive ecosystem of devices across multiple price points and product categories. The timing positions these products perfectly for the holiday shopping season, allowing consumers to consider them for year-end purchases and early 2026 upgrades.
