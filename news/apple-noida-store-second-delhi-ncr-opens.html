Apple Deepens Delhi-NCR Retail Roots with Noida Store

Apple has officially opened the doors to its latest retail destination in India: Apple Noida. Located in the bustling DLF Mall of India in Sector 18, this marks the iPhone maker's fifth company-owned store in the country and, significantly, its second in the Delhi-National Capital Region (NCR). The store opened to customers on Thursday, December 11, 2025, at 1:00 PM IST.

This expansion underscores Apple's aggressive retail strategy in one of its fastest-growing markets. Following the successful openings of Apple Saket in Delhi and Apple BKC in Mumbai in 2023, and more recent stores in Bengaluru and Pune, the Noida outlet strengthens Apple's direct-to-consumer presence in a key economic zone.

Why Noida? The Strategic Retail Play

The choice of Noida is a calculated move by Apple. The Delhi-NCR region is a massive contributor to India's premium smartphone market, accounting for over 9% of sales in the segment. Apple's sales in the region grew nearly 30% year-on-year until September 2025, with areas like Noida, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad surging over 50%.

Opening a second store in the NCR allows Apple to cater to distinct catchments. While the Saket store primarily serves South Delhi and Gurugram, the Noida store taps into the vibrant communities of Noida, East Delhi, Ghaziabad, and Greater Noida. This dual-store strategy maximizes reach and convenience for customers in one of India's most important markets.

Final Thoughts & How to Visit

Apple Noida is more than just a new store; it's a statement of commitment to the Indian market. It brings the full spectrum of Apple's products, services, and community-focused experiences closer to millions of potential customers in the northern part of the NCR.

For customers: Whether you're looking to buy the latest iPhone 17, get expert help with your Mac, attend a creative workshop, or simply experience the Apple ecosystem firsthand, Apple Noida is now your destination.
