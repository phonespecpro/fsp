Redmi 15C 5G Begins Sales in India With Big Battery and 50MP Camera

December 11, 2025
By: Shedrack

Sales Begin for Redmi's Latest Budget 5G Powerhouse

As of today, December 11, 2025, the much-anticipated Redmi 15C 5G is officially on sale in India. Following its launch announcement earlier this month, the smartphone is now available for purchase, bringing a compelling mix of a massive battery, capable camera, and 5G connectivity to the budget segment.

Xiaomi's sub-brand Redmi is targeting users who prioritize battery life and media consumption, offering a device that promises to last more than a day on a single charge while handling daily tasks and entertainment smoothly.

Complete Specifications & Features

Beyond the headline features, the Redmi 15C 5G packs several other noteworthy specs that make it a well-rounded offering for its price.

Display 6.9-inch IPS LCD, 720 x 1600 pixels (HD+), 120Hz refresh rate, 240Hz touch sampling, 810 nits peak brightness, TÜV Rheinland certified for eye comfort.
Platform MediaTek Dimensity 6300 (6nm) chipset, Octa-core CPU (2x2.4 GHz Cortex-A76 & 6x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55), Mali-G57 MC2 GPU. Runs Android 15 with HyperOS 2. Promised 2 OS upgrades & 4 years of security updates.
Memory & Storage 4GB/6GB/8GB LPDDR4X RAM options, 128GB UFS 2.2 internal storage. Dedicated microSDXC slot for expansion up to 1TB/2TB.
Camera System Rear: Dual-camera setup. Primary: 50MP, f/1.8, PDAF. Secondary: Auxiliary lens. Front: 8MP, f/2.0 camera for selfies and video calls. Video recording at 1080p@30fps for both.
Battery & Charging 6000 mAh (typical) large capacity battery. Supports 33W wired fast charging (charger included in box). Also supports 10W reverse wired charging.
Design & Durability Dimensions: ~173.2 x 81.1 x 8.2 mm. Weight: 211g. Plastic frame and back. IP64 rating for dust and water splash resistance. Side-mounted fingerprint sensor.
Connectivity & Audio 5G (7 bands in India), 4G LTE, Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.4, NFC (market dependent), GPS. Includes a 3.5mm headphone jack and an FM radio.

It is also available through authorized Xiaomi retail partners across the country. The device comes in three color options: Dusk Purple, Midnight Black, and Moonlight Blue. The Moonlight Blue variant features a sparkle design with a glossy finish.

First Impressions & Verdict

The Redmi 15C 5G enters a highly competitive budget 5G market in India. Its strongest selling points are clearly the massive 6000mAh battery and the inclusion of a 50MP primary camera at this price point.

The 120Hz refresh rate on the large 6.9-inch display is another welcome feature for smooth scrolling and media consumption, though the HD+ resolution is a typical compromise in this segment. The promised two years of Android OS updates and four years of security patches add value for longevity.

Who is it for? This phone is ideal for users who prioritize battery life above all else, enjoy consuming videos and reels on a large screen, and want to step into 5G connectivity without a significant investment. If you are looking for the best possible camera or display resolution in the budget category, you may want to compare other options. However, for its asking price starting at ₹12,499, the Redmi 15C 5G presents a very compelling package of endurance and essential features.
