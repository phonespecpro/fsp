From Solo Assistant to Collaborative Platform

OpenAI has dramatically expanded ChatGPT's capabilities with the global rollout of group chats, marking one of the most significant updates since the AI's launch . What began as a pilot program in Japan, New Zealand, South Korea, and Taiwan just one week ago has now expanded worldwide following overwhelmingly positive early feedback .

The new feature enables friends, family members, or coworkers to collaborate with each other and ChatGPT in shared conversations, effectively transforming the AI from a one-on-one assistant into a collaborative workspace . According to OpenAI, this represents "just the beginning of ChatGPT becoming a shared space to collaborate and interact with others" .

How Group Chats Work

Access Method Tap people icon in top-right corner of any chat
Invitation System Share link with up to 20 people
Profile Setup Name, username, and photo required for all participants
AI Behavior Decides when to respond based on conversation context
Manual Activation Mention "ChatGPT" to prompt response
Technical Base Powered by GPT-5.1 Auto model

Starting a group chat is intentionally straightforward. Users simply tap the people icon in the top-right corner of any new or existing chat, then invite others by sharing a link . When adding someone to an existing conversation, ChatGPT creates a copy as a new group chat, preserving the original private conversation .

Each participant must set up a brief profile including their name, username, and photo, ensuring everyone knows who's participating in the conversation . Group chats appear in a clearly labeled section of the ChatGPT sidebar, separate from personal conversations .

Smart AI Behavior and Social Features

OpenAI has taught ChatGPT new social behaviors specifically for group settings. The AI follows conversation flow and decides when to respond naturally versus when to stay quiet based on context . This prevents ChatGPT from becoming the "annoying person in the group chat who replies to everything" .

Beyond text responses, ChatGPT can now react to messages with emojis and reference profile photos to create personalized images within group conversations . The AI can also be directed through custom instructions specific to each group chat, allowing teams to define preferred tones, personalities, or response styles .

Privacy Protection and Global Availability

Memory Separation Personal memories not used in group chats
Youth Protection Automatic content filtering for users under 18
Access Control Invitation-only, participants can leave anytime
Plan Availability Free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans supported
Platform Support Web and mobile apps (iOS & Android)

Privacy was a fundamental design consideration. Group chats remain completely separate from private conversations, and personal ChatGPT memory is never used or created from these shared discussions . This ensures sensitive personal information doesn't accidentally surface during group collaborations.

The feature is available to all logged-in users across ChatGPT Free, Go, Plus, and Pro plans, representing one of OpenAI's most accessible premium features to date . For younger users, additional safeguards automatically reduce exposure to sensitive content for everyone in the chat when participants under 18 are detected .

Technical Foundation and Limitations

Group chats are powered by GPT-5.1 Auto, which intelligently selects the most appropriate model based on each prompt and the models available to the user ChatGPT is responding to . This ensures optimal performance regardless of whether participants are on Free or paid plans.

Rate limits function differently in group settings - they only apply when ChatGPT responds, not to messages exchanged between human participants . When ChatGPT does respond, it counts toward the usage limit of the person it's directly responding to based on their plan type .

The Future of Collaborative AI

OpenAI describes group chats as "just the beginning of ChatGPT becoming a shared space to collaborate and interact with others" . This update represents a strategic shift from positioning ChatGPT as a personal assistant toward establishing it as a social productivity network .

The successful global rollout following such a brief pilot period suggests OpenAI is accelerating its platform evolution. As the company noted, "As ChatGPT becomes an even better partner in group conversations, it will help you spark ideas, make decisions, and express your creativity with the people who matter most in your life" .
