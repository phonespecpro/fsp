OnePlus 15 Pre-Orders Go Live in the US — Pricing and Key Features Revealed

December 8, 2025
By: Shedrack
Infinite Black

US Pre-Orders Officially Begin

After a delay caused by the recent US government shutdown, the OnePlus 15 is now available for pre-order in the United States. Pre-orders went live on December 4, 2025, at 11 AM ET / 8 AM PT directly through OnePlus's official website.

The launch was initially postponed because the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) could not certify the device during the shutdown, which lasted from October 1 to November 12. With certification now complete, US customers can finally secure what OnePlus is calling a "two-generational upgrade" over its predecessor.

The OnePlus 15 launches in the US with two clear configurations, with color availability linked to the storage tier.

Key Specifications & Features

Positioned as a powerhouse for gamers and power users, the OnePlus 15 is built around several headline features that justify its "flagship" status.

Performance & Display Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5.
Display: 6.78-inch flat LTPO AMOLED with a dynamic 165Hz refresh rate and 1.5K resolution.
Gaming Focus: Features a dedicated 3200Hz touch response chip and is the "Champion's Choice" for the 2025 Call of Duty: Mobile World Championship.
Battery & Charging Battery: A massive 7300mAh Silicon NanoStack battery, marketed as the largest in a consumer smartphone in North America.
Charging: Supports 120W wired fast charging (100% in ~40 min) and 50W wireless charging.
Camera System Rear Camera: Triple 50MP system (wide, periscope telephoto with 3.5x optical zoom, ultrawide).
Imaging Engine: Powered by the new OnePlus DetailMax Engine for enhanced clarity and improved low-light (Clear Night Engine) and action (Clear Burst) photography.
Software & AI OS: Ships with OxygenOS 16 based on Android 16.
AI & Productivity: Features the AI-driven "Plus Key" (replacing the Alert Slider) and "Mind Space," which integrates deeply with Google Gemini for personalized assistance.
Build & Durability Rating: IP68/IP69K dust and water resistance.
Build: Glass front (Gorilla Glass Victus 2) with an aluminum alloy frame.

Additional Launch Offers & Shipping

Beyond the free pre-order gift, OnePlus is running several other promotions to sweeten the deal:

  • Trade-In Offer: Get a guaranteed $100 instant credit for trading in any smartphone in any condition, plus a $100 cashback bonus when the purchase includes a trade-in.
  • Student Discount: Verified students can save an additional 5% on their purchase.
  • Accessory Discounts: Up to 30% off additional OnePlus accessories when buying the OnePlus 15.

Shipping Timeline: Orders are expected to begin shipping around December 23, 2025. This means delivery for most customers will likely occur after the Christmas holiday.
