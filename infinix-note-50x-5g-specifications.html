Infinix Note 50X 5G

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Titanium Grey

Overview

The Infinix Note 50X 5G represents a significant leap forward in budget 5G smartphone technology, launching in March 2025 as the world's first device powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7300 Ultimate processor . This device combines impressive performance with premium features typically found in more expensive smartphones, making 5G technology accessible to a wider audience.

With its gem-cut camera module featuring active halo lighting, military-grade durability certification, and robust 5500mAh battery with 45W fast charging, the Note 50X 5G delivers exceptional value. The device runs on the latest Android 15 with Infinix's refreshed XOS 15 interface, offering a smooth and modern user experience with various AI enhancements .
