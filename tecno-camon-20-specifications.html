TECNO Camon 20

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Predawn Black

Overview

The TECNO Camon 20 is a feature-packed smartphone that was first released in May 2023, offering impressive specifications at an affordable price point. This device stands out with its capable MediaTek Helio G85 processor, vibrant 6.67-inch AMOLED display, and a versatile camera system headlined by a 64MP main sensor.

Designed for users who prioritize photography and media consumption, the Camon 20 combines modern aesthetics with practical features like IP53 dust and water resistance, ensuring durability against everyday splashes and dust exposure. With its massive 5000mAh battery and 33W fast charging capability, this phone is built to keep up with your daily demands without frequent charging interruptions.
