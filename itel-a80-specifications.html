Overview

The itel A80 emerges as a compelling budget smartphone option in 2025, offering premium features typically found in more expensive devices. Launched in January 2025, this device combines a smooth 120Hz display with capable performance and reliable battery life, making it an excellent choice for budget-conscious consumers .

With its IP54 dust and water resistance rating and 50MP main camera, the A80 stands out in the crowded budget segment. Running on Android 14 Go Edition with itel OS 14, this device provides a modern and intuitive user experience for everyday tasks, social media, and light applications .
