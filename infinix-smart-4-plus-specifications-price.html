Infinix Smart 4 Plus - Full Phone Specifications & Price

December 2, 2025
By: Shedrack

2025 Price Update

As of December 2025, the Infinix Smart 4 Plus remains a popular budget choice with a current price starting at ₹9,999 in India ($107 approximately). The phone was originally launched on July 21, 2020, but continues to offer value in the entry-level segment.

Midnight Black

Overview

The Infinix Smart 4 Plus is a budget smartphone that made waves at its launch with one standout feature: a massive 6000mAh battery. Designed for users who prioritize battery life above all else, this phone promises extended usage without frequent charging. Launched in July 2020, it continues to be relevant in 2025 for those seeking an affordable device for basic tasks.

With its 6.82-inch HD+ display, MediaTek Helio A25 processor, and basic camera setup, the Smart 4 Plus targets first-time smartphone users, students, and anyone needing a reliable backup phone. While it lacks 5G support and high-end features, its battery capacity remains its primary selling point in the competitive budget segment.

Battery Performance

The 6000mAh battery is the centerpiece of this device. According to Infinix, it can provide up to 38 hours of talk time and 31 days on standby. For typical users, this translates to 2-3 days of moderate use on a single charge, making it ideal for travel or situations where charging access is limited.

Final Verdict

The Infinix Smart 4 Plus in 2025 remains a specialist device for battery-conscious users on an extreme budget. Its massive 6000mAh battery is truly exceptional for the price, offering multi-day endurance that few phones can match.

Who should consider it:

  • Users who need a phone primarily for calls and messaging with minimal charging
  • First-time smartphone buyers or seniors who value simplicity
  • As a reliable backup/secondary device
  • Those in areas with limited electricity access

Limitations to consider:

  • Outdated Android 10 OS without major update prospects
  • No 5G support (4G only)
  • Basic performance for 2025 standards (3GB RAM, Helio A25)
  • Standard 10W charging for a 6000mAh battery means long charging times

While newer budget phones offer better performance and cameras, the Smart 4 Plus continues to find its niche among those for whom battery life is the non-negotiable priority.
