Overview

The Poco X7 is a performance-focused smartphone designed for gamers and power users, offering a fast AMOLED display, strong battery life, and flagship-level processing at a budget-friendly price.

With a high refresh rate screen, advanced cooling, and a versatile camera setup, the Poco X7 delivers an excellent balance of speed, efficiency, and everyday usability.
