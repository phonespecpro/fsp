Overview

The itel P65 emerges as a compelling budget smartphone option in 2025, offering impressive features typically found in more expensive devices. Launched in August 2024, this device combines a smooth 120Hz display with capable performance and reliable battery life, making it an excellent choice for budget-conscious consumers .

With its gaming-inspired design featuring a unique LED notification ring on the back, the P65 stands out in the crowded budget segment. Running on the latest Android 14 with itel OS 14, this device provides a modern and intuitive user experience for everyday tasks, social media, and light gaming .
