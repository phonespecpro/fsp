Infinix Hot 40

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Starlit Black

Overview

The Infinix Hot 40 continues Infinix's tradition of delivering impressive value in the budget smartphone segment. Launched as a successor to the popular Hot 30 series, this device brings notable improvements in camera capabilities, display technology, and charging speed while maintaining an accessible price point that appeals to cost-conscious consumers.

With its MediaTek Helio G88 gaming-focused processor, 50MP AI dual camera system, and massive 5000mAh battery supported by 33W fast charging, the Hot 40 offers features typically found in more expensive devices. The 90Hz refresh rate display ensures smooth scrolling and responsive touch experience, making it ideal for both entertainment and daily productivity tasks.
