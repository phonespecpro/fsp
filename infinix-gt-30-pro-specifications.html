Overview

The Infinix GT 30 Pro represents a significant leap in gaming smartphone technology for 2025, positioning itself as a premium gaming device with flagship-level features at an accessible price point. Launched in June 2025, this device combines powerful performance with specialized gaming features that rival much more expensive alternatives .

With its MediaTek Dimensity 8350 Ultimate chipset, sophisticated 144Hz AMOLED display, and innovative GT Trigger shoulder buttons, the GT 30 Pro delivers a comprehensive gaming experience. The device also features customizable RGB lighting, IP64 dust and water resistance, and advanced cooling systems that make it both functional and aesthetically striking for gaming enthusiasts .
