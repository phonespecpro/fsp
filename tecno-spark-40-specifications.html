TECNO Spark 40

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Ink Black

Overview

The TECNO Spark 40 represents a significant step forward in budget smartphone technology, combining reliable performance with impressive durability features at an accessible price point. Launched in July 2025, this device brings together MediaTek's capable Helio G81 processor with a smooth 120Hz display and substantial 5200mAh battery.

Designed for everyday reliability, the Spark 40 features an IP64 rating for dust and water resistance, along with drop resistance up to 1.5 meters, making it one of the most durable options in its class. With its comprehensive AI features including the "Ask Ella" assistant and advanced camera capabilities, the Spark 40 delivers exceptional value for budget-conscious consumers seeking a full-featured smartphone experience.
