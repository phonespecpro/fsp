TECNO Pova 7 Ultra 5G

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Geek Black

Overview

The TECNO Pova 7 Ultra 5G represents a significant leap forward in the mid-range gaming smartphone segment, combining flagship-level performance with innovative AI features and exceptional battery life. As the top model in the POVA 7 series, this device brings together MediaTek's powerful Dimensity 8350 Ultimate processor with a stunning 144Hz AMOLED display and massive 6000mAh battery.

Designed with an interstellar spaceship-inspired aesthetic, the Pova 7 Ultra 5G features a unique triangular camera island with dynamic LED lighting effects that respond to notifications, gaming events, and charging status. With its official gaming phone status for major esports tournaments including the 2025 Honor of Kings International Championship, this device is engineered to deliver uncompromising performance for competitive mobile gamers and power users alike.
