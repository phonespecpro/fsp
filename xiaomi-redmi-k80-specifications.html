Xiaomi Redmi K80

November 14, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Aurora Black

Overview

The Xiaomi Redmi K80 represents a significant milestone in flagship smartphone value, bringing premium Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 performance to an accessible price point. Launched in November 2024, this device delivers exceptional power for gaming, photography, and intensive multitasking with its advanced 4nm chipset and dual-loop 3D ice cooling system that maintains optimal temperatures even under sustained heavy loads. The combination of flagship-grade specifications and competitive pricing continues Xiaomi's tradition of disrupting the premium smartphone segment.

Beyond raw performance, the Redmi K80 impresses with its stunning 6.67-inch 2K OLED display reaching 3200 nits peak brightness, massive 6550mAh silicon-carbon battery with 90W fast charging, and IP68 water resistance. The device features a premium dual camera system with 50MP OIS main sensor and Light Fusion 800 technology, stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos, and the latest HyperOS 2.0 based on Android 15. Protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 7i and equipped with an ultrasonic fingerprint sensor, the Redmi K80 delivers a flagship experience that challenges devices at twice its price.
