Oppo Find X8 Ultra 5G: Full Phone Specifications, Price & Review

December 2, 2025
By: Shedrack

Important Availability Note

The Oppo Find X8 Ultra was announced on April 10, 2025, and released on April 16, 2025. However, it is currently only officially available in China. There is no official information about a global or Indian launch, making it an import-only device for most international markets.

Matte Black / Hoshino Black

Overview & Design Philosophy

The Oppo Find X8 Ultra, launched in April 2025, represents the pinnacle of Oppo's flagship smartphone technology. Designed as the "Ultra" model, it pushes the boundaries with a pro-grade quad-camera system co-engineered with Hasselblad, a top-tier Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, and a massive 6100mAh battery.

One of its standout design achievements is its form factor. Despite packing high-end components, the phone maintains a relatively sleek profile with a thickness of approximately 8.8mm, challenging the notion that Ultra phones must be bulky. It also features a robust "Sky Dome" architecture with an IP68/IP69 rating for dust and high-pressure water resistance.

Expected Price & Global Market Status

Since the Find X8 Ultra is not officially sold outside China, prices vary significantly based on import channels and local taxes.

Critical Consideration for Buyers: The Oppo Find X8 Ultra is not officially launched in India or most global markets. The prices listed for India and other regions are estimates for imported units. Purchasing an imported Chinese model means you may not have a local warranty, official software support for your region, or guaranteed compatibility with all local 5G bands. In India, Oppo officially sells the Find X8 and Find X8 Pro instead.

Key Features & Hands-On Analysis

Based on official specifications and early reviews, here are the defining aspects of the Oppo Find X8 Ultra.

What Makes It Stand Out?

  • Unmatched Camera Versatility: The dual periscope telephoto setup (3x and 6x) is rare, offering exceptional optical zoom range. The 1-inch main sensor promises great low-light performance.
  • Exceptional Battery Capacity: At 6100mAh, it has one of the largest batteries in any flagship, promising excellent endurance.
  • Robust Build: The IP68/IP69 rating offers protection against dust and high-pressure water jets, which is beyond the standard IP68.
  • Powerhouse Performance: The Snapdragon 8 Elite ensures top-tier performance for gaming and multitasking.

Important Considerations

  • Limited Availability: Its China-only launch is the biggest drawback for international users, affecting warranty, software, and network support.
  • Size & Weight: While slim, at 226g it is a substantial device.
  • Market Competition: In regions where it's not sold, it competes with locally available flagships from Samsung, Apple, and Xiaomi that offer official support.
  • Software Experience: ColorOS, while feature-rich, may not appeal to users who prefer stock Android or other interfaces.

Final Verdict & Who Should Consider It?

The Oppo Find X8 Ultra is a technological showcase, particularly for mobile photography enthusiasts. However, its appeal is heavily dictated by geography.

Consider the Oppo Find X8 Ultra (via import) if:

  • You are a photography enthusiast who values a versatile, pro-grade camera system with dual periscope lenses.
  • Maximum battery life is a non-negotiable priority for you.
  • You are tech-savvy, comfortable importing devices, and understand the risks regarding warranty and network compatibility.
  • You live in or can easily import from China.

Look for Officially Available Alternatives if:

  • You require a local manufacturer's warranty and official after-sales support.
  • Guaranteed compatibility with your country's 5G bands is essential.
  • You prefer purchasing from local retailers or carriers with installment plans.
  • You are in India (where the Find X8 Pro is the official top model) or other markets where it is not launched.

The Bottom Line: On paper, the Oppo Find X8 Ultra is one of the most compelling camera-focused flagships of 2025, especially with its unique dual periscope zoom and huge battery. In practice, its China-limited availability significantly limits its practicality and value for most global consumers. It remains a fantastic device for those within its official market or for import adventurers willing to trade support for specs.
