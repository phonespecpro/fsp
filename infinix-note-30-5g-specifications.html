Infinix Note 30 5G

November 12, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Magic Black

Overview

The Infinix Note 30 5G stands as a compelling budget-friendly smartphone that brings 5G connectivity to the masses without compromising on essential features. Launched as part of Infinix's popular Note series, this device combines a powerful MediaTek Dimensity 6080 processor with a stunning 108MP camera system, making it an excellent choice for users seeking premium features at an accessible price point .

With its large 6.78-inch 120Hz display, massive 5000mAh battery supporting 45W fast charging, and comprehensive 5G band support, the Note 30 5G delivers a well-rounded smartphone experience that punches above its weight class. The device also features JBL-tuned dual speakers, IP53 dust and splash resistance, and expandable storage up to 2TB, positioning it as one of the most feature-complete options in the budget 5G segment .
